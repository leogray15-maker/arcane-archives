47. Dependence

Dependence in Transurfing refers to the act of attaching too much importance to a goal, person, or situation. This attachment creates a form of polarized energy (energy that is either positive or negative), which disrupts the neutrality of the Universe. When you raise the importance of something, you create an energy field that activates equilibrium forces in the Universe, pushing you further from what you want. The Universe, in its neutral state, will always seek balance, and excessive attachment or dependence will trigger a counter-force that resists your desires.

Declaration:

The central message is that when you insist on a particular outcome or desire too strongly, you create an imbalance. The Universe, in its neutral state, tries to restore balance by distancing you from that desire. The more importance you place on something, the more resistance the Universe will generate to counteract that importance.

When you feel that the Universe is not acting in your favor, it's a signal that you are raising the importance of something too much. The solution is to let go—release the need to control, to achieve, or to make something happen.

Key Principles:

  • Release Importance: If you place too much importance on something, whether it's a goal, relationship, or outcome, the Universe will push back. You need to approach your desires without emotional attachment.
  • Neutrality of the Universe: The Universe is neutral—it doesn’t favor you or your desires. It merely responds to the energy you put out. If you put out polarized energy (excessive desire, fear, or attachment), the Universe will create a counter-force to restore balance.
  • Equilibrium Forces: When you raise the importance of something, you activate equilibrium forces. These forces are the Universe's way of restoring balance, and they will push you away from what you desire, leading you to a line of life where that desire doesn’t exist.
  • Emotional Detachment: To move towards your goal with ease, you need to approach it with no emotions attached. It should feel as neutral as going to the store to buy something. The more empty you are in your desire (i.e., without intense emotion or attachment), the faster you will manifest your goal.
  • Danger of Dependency: Dependency—whether emotional, physical, or financial—on a goal, person, or outcome creates a huge energy force that works against you. For example, thinking “if you love me, you will do this” creates a strong emotional attachment that creates a counter-force.
  • Avoid Comparisons: Comparing yourself to others is another form of polarized energy that creates conflict. Everyone has their own unique path. When you compare yourself, you create a disturbance in the Universe's neutrality, triggering imbalance and resistance. do not feel envy towards anybody. key rule.
  • Life is for Experience, Not Accomplishment: You are not here to prove anything or to accomplish something for someone else. Life is about experience, and your value does not depend on your achievements. Let go of the pressure to perform or succeed in the eyes of others. rather just take what you want, and experience it. for yourself. this can be a fast car. a big house. etc.

Practical Steps:

  1. Release attachment to your goals and desires. Imagine them as neutral outcomes rather than something you need to achieve. Approach them as you would a simple task—without urgency or pressure.
  1. Trust the Universe: Instead of forcing things, trust that the Universe will deliver what you need when the time is right. Let go of control, and don't fight against the flow of life.
  1. Stop comparing yourself to others. Your path is unique to you, and comparing your journey to someone else's only creates resistance. Accept yourself as you are.
  1. Focus on your own well-being. Life is not a race. You don’t need to compete with others or constantly strive for external validation. Focus on living your own truth, and let everything else fall into place.
  1. Check your emotional state: If you feel stressed, anxious, or desperate about something, it's a sign that you're raising its importance too much. Re-center yourself and shift your focus to neutrality and ease.

Interpretation:

When you raise the importance of something, you create a polarized energy that disturbs the neutral state of the Universe. The Universe then works to restore balance by sending you to a different path, where your desires cannot be fulfilled. The more you insist on something, the more resistance you create. This is why it often feels like things are getting worse despite your best efforts.

By detaching emotionally and allowing things to flow, you free the Universe from the pressure of your desires. This frees you to receive your goals naturally and effortlessly, without the resistance that comes from emotional dependence.

Key Takeaways:

  • Let go of attachment: Your goal is not your lifeline. If you are too attached to it, you create resistance.
  • Trust in the process: The Universe will provide what you need when you stop forcing things.
  • Live in neutrality: Avoid overloading your desires with emotions and expectations. Instead, let them flow naturally.
  • Avoid comparisons: Everyone has their own journey, and comparison only brings imbalance.
  • Experience life: You are here to experience, not to perform for others. Life is about being, not doing.

In Transurfing, the path to your goal is smoothest when you let go of the emotional attachment to it, live neutrally, and trust that the Universe will bring what you need when the time is right.

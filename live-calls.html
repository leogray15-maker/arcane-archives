Get direct access to Leo for personalized guidance, strategy review, and accountability. Limited to one call per member per week.

What to Expect

  • Duration: 30-45 minutes of focused, 1:1 time
  • Topics: Trading strategy, mindset, business growth, or personal development
  • Format: Video call via Zoom (link sent after booking)
  • Preparation: Come with specific questions or goals for maximum value
  • Recording: Calls are not recorded to maintain privacy and candid discussion

Scheduling Rules

  • Limit: One (1) call per member per 7-day period

  • No-shows: Missing a scheduled call without notice counts as your weekly slot
  • Availability: Slots are limited and filled on a first-come, first-served basis

Abuse of the scheduling system may result in temporary or permanent loss of 1:1 call privileges

Tip: Book early in the week for maximum flexibility and availability

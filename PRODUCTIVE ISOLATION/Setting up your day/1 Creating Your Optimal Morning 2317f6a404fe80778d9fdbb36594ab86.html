Let’s proceed.

When Should You Wake Up?

The answer is… very early.

Listen...

I cannot get work done unless everyone is asleep, and it is dark.

And also… we’re going to talk about a concept called “eat the frog”

Listen to this…

If you had to eat ONE live frog per day…

What time would you eat it?

Of course… the smart people will say first thing in the morning.

Why? Because you can wash it down with everything else.

Now... imagine you left the frog to be eaten at the end of the day.

Your mind will be thinking all day about that frog… just dreading it.

But imagine you literally got it done first thing in the morning? You would actually walk with more confidence knowing you conquered a challenge and you’ve done the worst task in your day.

So... we’ll apply this philosophy in a second.

You will wake up at a VERY early time, and you will “eat the frog”

Now... don’t worry if you’ve not got a specific time yet… let’s first go over what you’re going to do as soon as you wake up.

What To Do When You Wake Up.

First thing first in the day… you will eat the frog.

And what is this “Frog” you may ask…

Listen... you have one main goal in your life.

This “frog” that you’re going to be eating…

It’s the HIGHEST leverage task that you can do to achieve your goal.

Let me explain…

Here’s the 80/20 rule.

20% of the actions bring 80% of the results.

Imagine you’re a writer like me.

Your main 2 goals are :

  • Get more people into your product
  • Improve the product so more people will join.

So... what am I going to do in this “Eat the frog” task?

I’m going to write courses which add value to the network.

And I’m going to write posts which get more people into the network.

Simple as fuck, right?

Well... not really.

the frog is the most demanding task, the most mentally draining task.

That’s why people avoid it.

They go into their “work block” and they start commenting on others twitter posts to try engage, it’s not the “frog” task.

Just remember this…

The progress is ALWAYS in the most mentally draining task.

It will be things like outreach, recording videos, writing content, making your course.

These are the things that actually move the needle.

It’s not the little things like trying to engage on someone else’s twitter post.

So... here’s what you’re going to do.

You’re going to wake up early…

And you will EAT THE FROG.

You will pick out the single most important task for your business/life.

You will use the 80/20 rule and figure out what moves the needle.

Then you will work on JUST THAT.

Nothing else, forget it exists, just do the highest leverage tasks that are the most mentally draining.

WHY CAN WE NOT DO THE HIGH LEVERAGE TASK LATER IN THE DAY?

It’s not just about it “being hard.”

Your focus deteriorates with every task you do.

Let me give you another concept.

BRAIN POINTS.

I love this topic of brain points.

it will make you get ahead of 99.999% of people.

YOU WAKE UP, AND YOU START WITH 100 BRAIN POINTS

now lets say you check your phone, 10 brain points gone

you go to take a shit, reply to messages on snap, 15 brain points gone.

you go downstairs and eat a high carb meal like oats, cereal, porridge. with non raw milk. 30 brain points gone.

you fill up your water bottle with tap water that doesn't have a reverse osmosis filter, 20 brain points gone.

now you have a couple brain points left to put into your work

meanwhile your competition has 120 (which he starts above average because he optimises his health.)

you see how this works?

every time you do anything (even if it’s productive)

you lose brain points.

THE ENTREPRENEUR THAT CAN PUT THE MOST AMOUNT OF BRAIN POINTS INTO HIS WORK…

WINS.

THIS IS WHY WE DONT DO OUR WORK IN THE EVENING OR MIDDAY…

WE RESERVE OUR BRAIN POINTS FOR OUR WORK TASK.

Listen...

When you wake up… it feels like you’ve been factory resetted, fully refreshed.

Why’s this? Because in sleep our brain makes sense of the experiences from the day before… and it turns it into memories.

So.. your conscious mind is clear when you wake up.

But when you do something that isn’t work right after you’ve woke up… you’re wasting the brain points that you have.

This is why we will do our “eat the frog” task when nobody is awake.

You don’t speak to no one, you don’t have to do anything.

You put ALL of your brain points into the most mentally draining task, you get it done.

Then you can go school/work with minimal brain points, because it doesn’t really matter.

HOW TO INCREASE BRAIN POINTS.

You improve your health… which we go over in the “health ascendance” course.

So.. it’s safe to say that you NEED to wake up early and eat the frog.

I’ve ate the frog, and then I’ve washed it down, now all that’s left is me being proud of myself because I started the day off with a win.

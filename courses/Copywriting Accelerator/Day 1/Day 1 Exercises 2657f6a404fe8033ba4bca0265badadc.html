Day 1 Exercises

  1. Buy a notebook dedicated to your copywriting journey. Use it to log your thoughts, breakthroughs and discoveries and to monitor your progress.
    • There's something magical that happens when you physically write it. It HAS to be physical.
  1. Analyse the swipes of the day:
    • What's the main idea behind this piece of copy?
    • Who is it directed to?
    • What emotion does it play on?
    • Why do you think it worked?
  1. Send your idea of the day on the skool
  1. Write a personal letter to your mother or father.
    • Prompt: recommend a movie they would enjoy and persuade them to watch it.
    • Hint (optional): tell them how watching this movie would benefit them, what memories it could evoke, how it would make them feel...
    • Use whatever you think could drive them to watch it.

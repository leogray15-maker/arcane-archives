  1. Awareness ladder
    • Prompt:
      • Choose a product or service
      • Write a series of 5 short ads (50-100 words) each targeting a different awareness level
    • Hints:
      • Unaware: introduce a problem your audience didn't know they had.
      • Problem aware: agitate a problem your audience knows they have.
      • Solution aware: position the product category as the solution.
      • Product aware: introduce the product and it's unique mechanism. Focus on what sets it apart from it's competitors.
      • Most aware: address existing customers or those very familiar with the product. Mention new features, upgrades and special offers to encourage repeat purchases or upgrades.

This exercise will help you see how you can target different prospects by adapting the way you address them first.

Which Books Should I Read?

As I have said, I do NOT recommend you read books right now.

They're theory-heavy and they will clash with your current training, leaving you more confused.

With that said, if you insist on reading books, here are my recommendations.

Copywriting:

  • The One Sentence Persuasion Course - Blair Warren -> Read it whenever you want.
  • Great Leads - Michael Masterson (Mark Ford) & John Forde -> Read it after the lesson on Awareness Levels on Day 4.

Sales:

  • Flip the Script - Oren Klaff -> Read it after the 31 days.

Style:

  • Show, don't tell - Sandra Gerth -> Read it whenever you want.
  • The Emotion Thesaurus - Angela Ackerman & Becca Puglisi -> Read it whenever you want.

Optional (not for beginners):

  • The Adweek Copywriting Handbook - Joseph Sugarman -> Read it after the 31 days.
  • The Copywriter's Handbook - Robert W. Bly -> Read it after the 31 days.
  • Ca$hvertising - Drew Eric Whitman -> Read it after the 31 days.

Remember: Reading books is NOT a substitute for hard work!

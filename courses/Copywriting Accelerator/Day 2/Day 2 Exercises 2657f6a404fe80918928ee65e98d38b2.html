Email To Rewrite:

Subject line: Great Product for You!!!

Hey there,

Hope your doing good. I wanted to reach out to you about our amazing new product that I think you'll love. Its called SuperClean Pro and its the best cleaning solution on the market today!!!

SuperClean Pro can clean anything from floors to windows to countertops and more. It works great and leaves everything super super clean. You won't believe how good it is!!!

Here's some reasons why you should buy SuperClean Pro:

  • Its really effective
  • It smells nice
  • You can use it on lots of things
  • Its not too expensive

Were offering a special deal right now where you can get 2 bottles for the price of 1. This is a limited time offer so you should act fast before it expires!!!

If your interested, just reply to this email and we'll get you set up. Don't miss out on this amazing opportunity to get the best cleaning product ever!!!

Thanks, John

P.S. Did I mention its really good? You'll love it!!!

Areas Of Improvement (Solution)

  1. Poor subject line: the subject line is vague and doesn't motivate the reader to open the email.
  1. Poor structure: the email lacks a clear structure and formatting.
  1. Vague language: many claims are made without specific details or evidence (‘best cleaning solution’, ‘works great’).
  1. No personalisation: the email doesn't address the recipient by name or show any knowledge of their specific needs.
  1. Grammatical errors: there are several grammatical mistakes and incorrect pronoun usage (‘your’ instead of ‘you’re’ and ‘its’ instead of ‘it’s’).
  1. Unprofessional tone: the overall tone is too casual and lacks credibility.
  1. Weak call-to-action: the CTA is vague and doesn't provide clear next steps.
  1. Overuse of exclamation points: the email uses too many exclamation points, which comes across as unprofessional and can get you labelled as spam.
  1. Missing important details: the email doesn't provide essential information like price, size of the product or where it can be purchased.

Why you should not want to work a 9-5 in the long run:

If I never quit my 9-5 I would have never been able to make as much money as I do now.  It allowed me to go all in on my online business & scale it to 7 figures.  Here are 5 reasons you need to do the same:

1) Your boss doesn’t care about you.  All you are to them is a source of work & money.  If you died tomorrow, they’d replace you in a week.  Why would you want to pull all your time & effort into building something for someone who doesn’t care about you?

2) Your income is limited.  Your salary is the minimum amount they can pay you to keep you there.  The skills you have & the work you put in could easily make you double what they’re paying you.  You need to start your own business & unlock unlimited income.

3) Options.  There have never been more options to start your own business & get rich.  I was making less than £20,000 a year working 60 hour weeks when I last had a 9-5. Now I work 5 hours a week doing online Dropshipping which is my main source of income and I bring in about 10k-15k a month just doing this.

4) Freedom.  You’ll never have complete freedom when you have to report to a job at a certain time every day.  You’ll have to miss out on:  •Family time •Travelling •Hobbies  If you quit your 9-5 & start your own business, you can decide where & when you want to work.

5) Burnout. Working 8+ hours every day on someone else's dream is gonna end in burnout.

You’re going to become:

  • Unmotivated
  • Exhausted
  • Angry

Our mental & physical health is going to suffer.  You need to start your own business so you can prioritise yourself.

Summary:

5 reasons you need to quit your 9-5:

  • Your boss doesn’t care about you
  • Your income and time is limited
  • Options
  • Freedom
  • Burnout

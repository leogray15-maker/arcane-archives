Simple relationship advice: 

Cheating is a choice, not a mistake.

If they cheat it’s over no discussions is needed.

Judge by actions not words.

Words have a way of getting people away with things.

Actions mean everything.

When you are angry….

Stay silent, as your angry will pass, but your words will cause permanent damage.

If they put you second

Then put them last, as if they truly loved you they would prioritise you in any situation. Remember that.

If it drains you

If a relationship drains you then this is likely not for you.

True love brings you energy and happiness and fake love continuously drains you of love.

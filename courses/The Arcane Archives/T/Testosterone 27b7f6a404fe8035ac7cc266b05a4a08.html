Testosterone

Testosterone is very important.

It’s important to be strong and it’s important to make money.

Here are 5 everyday things killing your testosterone:

Low testosterone causes:

  • Low sex drive
  • Emotional dysregulation
  • Poor stress management
  • Man boobs (gynecomastia)
  • Decreased reproductive health

Here are 5 things killing your testosterone:

1. Stress

Cortisol (the stress hormone) and testosterone are linked.

They're both made from the same building block - cholesterol.

When you're stressed, your cortisol spikes and your testosterone falls.

Reduce your stress with:

  • Exercise
  • Journaling
  • Meditation

2. Soy products

Soy has phytoestrogens - plant-based estrogens.

Studies have correlated the consumption of these with reduced testosterone.

Beer and foods like tofu, soy milk, and edamame contain large amounts of soy.

3. Lack of sleep

A 2011 study in JAMA restricted subjects' sleep for 1 week.

The result of just 7 days of getting less sleep?

A 10-15% reduction in testosterone.

Get more sleep by:

  • Using less caffeine
  • Getting more exercise
  • Turning off screens 1-2 hours before bed

4. Not exercising

Sitting around all day destroys your health.

But your testosterone is especially affected.

A simple system to boost your testosterone:

  • Walk 3-4 times a day
  • Lift weights 2-5 times a week
  • Push yourself to do more each session

5. Excess body fat

Testosterone is inversely related to waist circumference.

Being overweight causes:

  • Suppressed testicular function
  • Storage of testosterone in body fat
  • Testosterone conversion into estrogen

Dropping body fat quickly boosts testosterone.

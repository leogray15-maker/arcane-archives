Fusion And Equilibrium.

this ones a short one, but I just want to touch on it as we end page one.

jung was interested in 2 things.

science and spirituality.

in between, it kind of makes sense that he chose psychiatry and infiltrating the mind.

science is just making discoveries in life.

whether is be new medicine, or anything of that nature.

and spirituality and "gods world" as he called it, was just an interest.

so he took that science approach to making new discoveries in his REAL interest.

blending them together.

this is a short one, but what does it mean for you?

I don't know your interests, but I know mine.

I want you to keep in mind that you have 2 personalities, and 2 main things you want to pursue.

don't choose just one of them and leave the other, your brain needs completion.

look for a "fusion" between them, that is your purpose.

just like the law of balance...

go too far down, it goes back to the middle.

go too far up, it goes back to the middle.

everything always is in equilibrium.

accept that as a fact of life, and you will find fulfilment.

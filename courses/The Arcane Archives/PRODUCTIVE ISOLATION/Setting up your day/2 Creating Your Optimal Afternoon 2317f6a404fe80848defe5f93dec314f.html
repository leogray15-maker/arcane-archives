NO CARBS UNTIL FINISHED.

Listen… you want to be fasted for as long as possible.

Let’s go into some science.

You see… when you don’t eat carbs your body runs on the fat in your body instead.

And the good thing about that?

Fat consistently will burn for energy, meanwhile carbs will just keep on needing a “re up” where you need to keep eating for energy.

And here’s the thing with carbs…

When you eat them, your brain just goes retarded.

Listen... have you ever ate a bowl of porridge then just get so brain fogged and tired? That’s what it does.

And why does it happen?

it’s because your body is focused on turning the carbohydrates INTO energy.

And it will take a while to digest… that’s why after a carb heavy meal you just want to sleep, and you wake up STILL tired because you was digesting in your sleep/not recovering.

And having that midday is just not good for focus and productivity at all.

But… what can you eat then?

Personally, I don’t even eat anything until everything is done.

But if you are new and you need to eat…

You should eat fats and proteins.

Meat and eggs, that’s it.

The worst thing you can do for your productivity is consume any type of sugar, trust me.

You can have carbs AFTER your daily tasks are done… so it’s kind of like a hunt.

Lions will hunt all day… then they will eat and just lay around and sleep, that’s how you should operate… like a lion.

So, it’s safe to say this….

Don’t eat any carbs UNTIL you have finished all of your daily tasks, which we will go over “resting” and “unwinding” in the evening section.

LETS GET INTO SETTING YOUR PHONE UP FOR SUCCESS.

social media, 100% it's best to delete it all, that's what I did at the start of my journey.

and that's 100% the reason why I got success in it, no distractions, pure stealth mode.

I had my phone in black and white, no social media except from whatsapp.

but most likely you probably will be using social media for business since it has to be used for 99% of things these days.

so this is what you do in short...

this will be a step by step guide.

PHONE NOTIFICATIONS OFF.

now, this doesn't mean "do not disturb"

this means OFF.

go on your settings app, go on snapchat, Instagram, tiktok, everything.

and turn notifications off FULLY.

because do not disturb isn't enough these days, because if you swipe up on lock screen, you can still see notifications, even when on dnd.

unnecessary dopamine.

THE ONLY NOTIFICATIONS I HAVE ON, IS STRIPE, PAYPAL, ANYTHING THAT HAS PAYMENT.

this way the only dopamine I get from my notifications is when I got a sale (which is well deserved)

DATING APPS.

simple, you 100% need to get off dating apps.

as a man, your number 1 weakness is women, which is a beautiful thing.

but... you will see some truth in this.

whenever you're even just messaging even ONE girl.

she takes up some of your brain real estate.

and the guy who has more brain real estate and brain power focused on his goals, is going to win 100/100 times.

and dating apps are shit for your mental health.

SO IT GOES WITHOUT SAYING, DATING APPS GONE.

SCREEN TIMER ON APPS

I don't set normal time limits like "oh I'm allowed 30 mins on tiktok today."

I put all the "bad apps" , the instagram, tiktok to 1 minute each. so that way every time I click it and it says time limit reached.

and then I think about my decision, do I need to get on this app or am I coping?

then if I do need to post something or do something productive.

I type my passcode in and it lets me on.

just a little hack that I learnt to keep yourself off these brain numbing apps.

MUSIC.

this step is extra, but for now its just a personal preference for me.

the only time I listen to real music...

its when I'm in the gym.

when I'm out of the gym, I'll listen to ambient music.

just music that doesn't get rid of your thoughts.

you see... with "real music" with lyrics, etc.

you can't think while listening to it, your brain is just focused on the song.

so... you need something that actually allows you to think to YOURSELF, not focusing on some lyrics.

you can start listening to ambient music, that's what I just grew to love now, especially in the dark mornings... or even in the gym.

BLACK AND WHITE MODE FOR LOWERED DOPAMINES.

experiment with this. go to your Phone... type in colour filters and put in greyscale.

this goes into your primal brain.

when we see colours, our caveman brain gets dopamine.

when we see blue, our caveman brain thinks "oh its a blueberry" and dopamine release.

when you put your phone on no colour.

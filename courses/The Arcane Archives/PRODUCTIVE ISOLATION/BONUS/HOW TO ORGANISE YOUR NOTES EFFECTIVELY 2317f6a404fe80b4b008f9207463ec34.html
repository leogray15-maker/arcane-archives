HOW TO ORGANISE YOUR NOTES EFFECTIVELY.

i listen to podcasts 24/7,
watch these daily quests,
and other youtube videos.
but i always had a problem with organising notes.
i could take them well, but i didn't know where to put them, or how to organise them.
notion was meh, not the best for it at all.
but i just downloaded OneNote for it and it's so good.
use this with every single piece of content you consume.
imagine having notes of EVERY piece of content you've consumed in your whole life, (from this point) in one software and easy to navigate, so OP, and it also motivates you to self educate more.
there were times where i didn't want to do it, just because i didn't know where to put my notes and i knew they'd be messy. it pissed me off.
now, i'm building an information hub, so i WANT to build it up as much as possible, i'll be self educating way more now.
anyway so what i've done, is create different sections for every person i watch, and in each section, i will add a page with the name being the video title.
i'll do this for every single person, and every video, so i can literally go to a specific video for notes instead of scrolling down a massive 15k word notion page trying to find something.
you have unlimited sections and unlimited pages, so you can literally just keep adding the videos you watch and put the notes down on it so easily, and it's cold to quote something from an exact video from an exact person.
it's free to download and easy to work so give it a try.

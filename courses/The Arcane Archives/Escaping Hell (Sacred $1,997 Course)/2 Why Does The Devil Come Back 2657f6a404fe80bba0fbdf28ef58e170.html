2. Why Does The Devil Come Back?

So, you might’ve seen this drawing before, or something similar.

And if you look at it, you actually realise that only the middle of the arrow is pretty fucked up.

And the end just has a straight path…

We’re going to go into why this actually happens.

And it’s not the cute answer of “success just takes time!”

Here’s the short answer…

It’s because all of the people that have made It to the end of the arrow realise this concept I’m about to teach you.

It’s a deep rabbit hole.

It’s about… shedding skin.

Killing your past identity.

Since that is who is dragging your current self down from your goals.

You see, when I used to get into these deep ruts, I would notice I went back to my old identity.

It was a turning point for me when I realised this…

I would start sitting at my desk all day watching YouTube videos that my child self would have enjoyed, or i would start going on games that my child self would have enjoyed + add smoking weed into that mix.

I would go back to playing Minecraft, I’d go back to watch old YouTube "let’s plays" that I watched as a kid.

It is a very odd predicament, you might have experienced this in the “nostalgic YouTube video” form.

Or you might have experienced it through different forms.

Either way, it still becomes a reality.

Why does it become a reality? you may ask.

it’s because it’s your old identity trying to stay alive.

Because deep in your mind your mind doesn’t think your deserve success.

Your old self that hid from everything makes a comeback.

you just want to hide from the world… just like your child self did because life was too traumatic and hard.

by the end of the next module you will understand :

  • Why this happens
  • How this happens

Which ultimately will bring you one step closer to understand how to defeat it.

Since to defeat something, you need to know it’s weak points.

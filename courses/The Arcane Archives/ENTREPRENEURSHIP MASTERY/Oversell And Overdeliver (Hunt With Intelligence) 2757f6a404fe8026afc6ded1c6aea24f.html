Oversell And Overdeliver. (Hunt With Intelligence)

Honestly, business is simple.

Oversell and overdeliver.

What do I mean by that?

Simple.

Your sales page should show a TONE of value.

And your product should have even MORE value INSIDE it.

let’s break this down :

Business is just 2 things

Eyes and a product.

You get eyes on your product, the more eyes you get, the more buy.

The better the sales page, the more buy.

Simple.

But there’s a next step…

The “back end” of selling online.

First, let’s talk about your sales page.

Your sales page is where you direct your “eyes” to

The more eyes on it, the more will buy. That’s a simple rule.

But with a BETTER page (i.e showing more VALUE)

You’ll convert MORE of them eyes.

So.. let’s talk about how to actually show what we call “value”

dream outcome X perceived likelihood of achievement.

So... let’s break this down.

We’ll take a look at a single bullet from my next upcoming sales page for this one :

Dream outcome : success in ANY business

Percieved likelihood of achievement : INEVITABLE

Effort and sacrifice : it will take a while… but this is NECESSARY for your success (deep desire)

Then I go onto actually show that this WORKS, it’s actually INEVITABLE, I show this with a screenshot… proving that it worked for me.

And I can’t fake it, they could scroll down to my account and see that exact tweet.

The more VALUE you show, the more people are “persuaded”

So... let me tell you one thing…

To have more VALUE… you give them more TRANSFORMATIONS.

You will go from x to X.

And if you make that x to X transformation more “inevitable”, you will get more sales

You show this is more “inevitable” by showing that the transformation WORKS.

You can do this 2 ways.

A “mechanism” or “proof”

If you don’t have proof or testimonials yet, the mechanism will work.

The mechanism simply shows HOW this is going to work.

You can tell anyone that you can become effortlessly productive with this “one hack”

But why would they believe you?

This game is guilty until proven otherwise.

You need to show them HOW it works.

But the key is here to give them a transformation, AND a MECHANISM.

Give them a transformation and tell them HOW it’s done.

The more “x to X” transformations with a mechanism/proof in your page, the more it will sell.

A single transformation can make them buy, so you increase your “surface area” for them to buy by adding MORE.

Simple as that.

The more value you seem to provide, the more it sells.

And value is simply an “x to X” transformation with PROOF that it works (mechanism)

That’s it done. Keep that mindset.

Now next… the actual product.

The product should actually “overwhelm” the reader with value BUT with a “step by step” guide to go through it.

When the human brain is overloaded, it does nothing.

When it’s overloaded with value WITH a guide to go through it, it sees it as “value”

Only 2% of people actually finish a course that sells for over $1000, and that’s for a reason.

Value without GUIDE is USELESS.

If you give value, but you don’t guide them through it, the human brain says “this is too much, we shut down”

The goal is to get them into the product by a sales page which shows A LOT of value, and then have them see EVEN MORE value INSIDE the product.

But when they get in, make sure you have an “onboarding” system.

This can be a welcome course, a welcome call if you’re up for it.

You need value + a guide.

So... let’s summarise this..

VALUE in your sales page ‘x to X” transformation, with a mechanism/proof...

ONE TRANSFORMATION CAN SELL THEM. MAXIMISE YOUR CHANCES WITH QUANTITY. BUT DON'T SACRIFICE QUALITY.

make your transformations VALUABLE, but never sacrifice the quality.

one screw loose, the whole building falls... this is key. NEVER forget.

MORE value in your product “shock” the customer with the amount of value.

But with an onboarding mechanism to get them to go through it without being overwhelmed.

Once you build that, your job is to get as much eyes on it as possible.

After this, success is inevitable.

So,  in simple terms..

Value in your sales page (x to X with mechanism, as much as you can)

Overwhelm the reader with VALUE in your product, with a guide.

Get eyes on that.

Do this for long enough, success is really inevitable.

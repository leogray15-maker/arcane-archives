Creating Belief In Your Message And Cult

let's say you've got a message that you preach, and the message shares one end goal and polarises.

now your job is to be the LEADER of that message, and help others that also resonate to REACH that 'goal' that your culture is following, you give them a helping hand.

Help Them Break Their 4-Minute Mile

you've got a message, people who resonate with it are also trying to get there.

but they seem to just not be able to.

they're trying to break a record (the 4 minute mile)

but they can't seem to do it, and here's where you help them.

first, let's talk about the "4 minute mile"

there is a story of Roger Bannister who broke the 4-minute mile on May 6, 1954. Prior to that, everyone thought it was impossible, and after Roger broke it, proving it was possible, many people since then have been able to do it. The certainty people get when they see someone else do something they thought was impossible, gives them the belief they need to also achieve that same goal.

people couldn't break the 4 minute mile because they thought it was impossible.

but after one guy did it, people after him started to believe it... and therefore break it.

this is what you need to do with your cult following.

you need to make them BELIEVE that they CAN do it.

this is simply a leader mentality, a good leader makes everything possible.

as an example, steve jobs was the founder of apple.

everyone that met him said that he had a crazy aura around him that just enabled people to do anything and create anything.

simply being around him made people believe that they could achieve anything.

i got this from a founders podcast on steve jobs, give it a watch if you haven't.

but he was one of the greatest business leaders on the planet. he made apple.

he had a vision : create the most minimalistic tech products in the world

he had belief in himself, therefore that bounced off on others.

he had absolute certainty, remember that from the sub niche module?

absolute certainty, that's what makes people break their 4 minute mile.

after all, just believing that they CAN do it is going to make them take action on it, and make you a leader.

so how can you actually help your cult followers break the 4 minute mile? give them belief.

allay their fears, encourage their goals. remember that?

this makes the transformation possible, and they actually have a glimpse of the vision and end goal you share and they FOLLOW it, that's the most important part. they FOLLOW it... but only as long as you have belief and absolute certainty, know how to use persuasion, allay their fears, encourage goals.

your job as a leader is to get people to get your message, have BELIEF in the shared goal and that they CAN achieve it.

and ultimately, you want their whole life to IDENTIFY with your message.

sounds extreme? it does.

but you know what a cult is? extreme.

i have people that read my emails daily, i have people who buy the snus I buy, do my exact daily routine.

this is how you create true fans, they IDENTIFY with the 2busy movement, which I'm starting to emphasise as I learn here.

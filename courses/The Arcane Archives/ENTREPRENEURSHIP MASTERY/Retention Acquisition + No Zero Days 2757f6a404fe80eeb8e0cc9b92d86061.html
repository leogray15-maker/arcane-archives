Retention > Acquisition + No Zero Days.

this is a lesson from alex hormozi.

retention > acquisition.

don't look to get NEW people in your product.

look to KEEP people in your product.

how do you do this?

well, it's simple.

make your product so good people NEVER leave.

I can talk about this on my side, because my churn is very low

on the other side, something like adonis school (hamzas product)

he went from 2k members, to something like 300.

I'm not shitting on him, just making a comparison.

I've been in his skool for maybe like 8 months in total.

and I've seen what's in there.

i'm not trying to put him down, but I have full conviction that this network is INSANE compared to it, and a lot of other skools.

and this is why my churn is so low, because I KEEP adding.

"it's easy to make money, but insanely hard to retain"

you've heard this a lot of time.

luke belmar says "create, multiply, preserve"

PRESERVE is the hardest part.

but also, the most important part.

everyone can have good marketing, but marketing does nothing when your customers DON'T stay.

you can keep on getting people in, keep giving them your sales page.

but if they leave sooner or later, you lose the money.

so, really what is the way to change this?

KISS.

no, I'm not telling you to kiss.

I'm telling you to KISS (keep it simple, stupid)

JUST GIVE MORE VALUE.

and keep adding.

daily.

that's a lot of "flaws" that i've seen with these big skool communities like adonis school.

they do weekly calls, things like that.

i used to join the adonis skool calls, when it had maybe 2k members.

and only about 50 people would be in the live call.

and plus, just a "call" just doesn't seem valuable, to me atleast.

you need to get people IN your product and KEEP adding, that's how you win in subscription models.

it's as simple as that.

KISS.

keep it simple, stupid.

good sales page, and just KEEP ADDING.

people sell a lot of courses but they just leave it as "one and done"

they make the course, and they leave it for it to sell.

then they go live in thailand on a beach.

it's an easy life.

so what makes you stand out?

KEEP. WORKING.

you keep working, keep adding.

even if it's just one new module a day, one new dataset a day.

it WILL compound.

it's a BIG 80/20

people will stay, and over time you have MORE value in here.

more value, MORE people will join.

more value added daily, MORE people will STAY.

they wake up to another module every day they can read.

they wake up to another video to watch.

they will stay.

but they will not stay if you make a "one and done" course and go live in thailand.

you need to care.

this is why the inbound method is the best business, you simply just work on your passion.

you teach it, you teach it daily.

you keep doing that, people keep getting new info.

and people stay, your product only gets better.

do this for 5 years, your product has it's own market, because it's so valuable.

you're building your "moat" by doing this.

not much people understand it.

they think online business is a "one and done" thing.

but in reality, you just keep adding.

even if it's just one module a day.

never have a "zero-day"

a zero day is a day where you do nothing.

i got this from a very successful entrepreneur, here, I'll paste it :

"Thought I'd share this since it helped me reach 12/K a month doing copy

Progress is exponential, not linear...

I was at £0 for 6 months then on my 7th month I landed a £2000/month client And ONLY two traits helped me get there...

  1. Working without seeing results
  1. Non-Zero Days

If any of you have read atomic habits There's a law called "The Plateau Of Latent Potential" So, what does this mean?

In short, you keep working until you experience that breakthrough moment...

All the work you're doing without the results showing up, It's meant for this very moment.

Now let's move on to the second trait

There's days where we feel lazy, unproductive and sloth-like...

You didn't do anything productive all day and it's 11:58PM?

Write one email

Do one pushup

Read one page of that self-improvement book

Why?

Because one is non-zero Everyday you must do something towards your goals Even if it's one...

Don't use this as an excuse to limit yourself tho Just on days where life gets in the way... "

even if you have a shit day (it happens)

do SOMETHING.

write one email, one module, one pushup, one podcast.

anything.

just don't have a "zero day"

that's it.

let's summarise :

GOOD SALES PAGE

GOOD VALUE

KEEP ADDING VALUE DAILY.

simple as that.

and...

don't have any "zero days"

when life gets in the way, write one email, one post, one module.

just do something.

that's it.

just a mindset shift.

retention > acquisition.

do that, and both will stay.

and remember... KISS.

that'll stay in your mind.

when you're doing anything... KISS.

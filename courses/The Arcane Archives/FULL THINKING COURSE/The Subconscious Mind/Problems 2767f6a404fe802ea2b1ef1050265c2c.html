Problems

Wouldn’t you all agree we have problems?

I’d argue against that.

Problems — in your world — is just something that gets in the way of something we want.

If it did not get in the way, would it really be a problem?

When we have the problem, we have the thought, the belief, the idea, the concept, whatever...

And whatever it is that we have is getting in the way of what we want.

Stay with me now.

That tells us that we are GETTING something from having a problem.

Otherwise why would we keep it? Unless we are getting something from it.

So therefore is a "problem" really a "problem"?

There are no problems, only challenges, obstacles, etc...but no problems.

What do you get from having a problem?

What do you get from having this challenge, from this obstacle, from this being hard?

Let's say you are fat, what do you get from being fat?

If you're getting something from being fat, it's not really a problem is it?

So therefore problems don't really exist. What's more: In the subconscious mind, problems don't exist. Only solutions.

The subconscious mind will only absorb, keep, store, support, encourage, multiply, whatever that is positive, good, happy, keep you safe that it gives something.

That's why we have "conflict" In there it's most likely keeping you from changing identity.

kick the door down and find out nearly everything that's holding you back.

You'll come across things that you haven't seen since you were 5, 8,12, 15 years old.

It's not about removing the problem, it's about losing it.

If you didn't get something from it, you wouldn't have gotten it in the first place, and you wouldn't be keeping it.

Once you do this process, you'll go from having challenges to advantages, obstacles to opportunities, loss to gain, etc…

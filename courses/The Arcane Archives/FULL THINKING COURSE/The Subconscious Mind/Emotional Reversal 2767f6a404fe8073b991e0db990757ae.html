Emotional Reversal

You're always in control, but you don't know it.

And when you forget, you lose control.

Over your emotions, over your energy, over your focus, over your goals, and everything else.

What causes you to lose control?

Emotions.

Here's how to KNOW that you are in control:

Whatever you "feel" is within and thus can be within your control.

You want to switch positions with the emotion.

Here's what I mean: Imagine being fear and scaring you.

Imagine being resistance and scaring you. Imagine being failure and trying to bully you and stop you.

Look at yourself as 'fear', 'resistance' and whatever you're experiencing and actively try to mess yourself up.

Once you do this, it dissipates in the mind.

I mean do it right now, if you're feeling stressed or whatever other emotion you're feeling.

Be the stress and stress yourself out.

You see how you immediately have control now?

It also becomes comical.

Try it and let me know.

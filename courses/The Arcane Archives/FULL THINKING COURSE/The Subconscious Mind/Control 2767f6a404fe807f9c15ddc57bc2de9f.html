Control

What makes you feel really good are two things, permission and control How does the mind know that it’s in control of something?

  • When you can stop something
  • What is that something?
  • Your response to it

If you look at CNN, FoxNews, etc...

  • Their constant undertone of message is "you can't stop it"
  • Which implies "You can't do anything about it":

You can't control your weight? Can you control what you eat? Yes, then you can.

You can't control smoking?

Well, can you stop smoking? No, ok can you stop smoking for 3 hours? Oh, you do it all the time? I guess you can.

"I have to" implies "I have no control"

  • I have to work this job
  • I have to work for this client
  • I have to do this
  • I have to live here
  • I have to think this
  • Etc...

If you can stop, then you don't have to.

If you had to, then you couldn't stop.

So what exactly do you have to do?

Nothing.

That means, you're in full control over everything.

The way you know you're in control is if you can STOP.

Defeating The Destructive Pendulums.

trying to battle with a pendulum is useless.

when you battle with a pendulum, you just end up supplying it with energy.

The stronger your desire to avoid something, the more likely it is that you will encounter it.

and you have experienced that.

the brain doesn’t care about you wanting to “get rid of it”

it simply sees it as “oh, he’s thinking about it”, giving it more energy.

meaning you will NOT get out of it.

the point is, once you let the thing you do not want INTO your life.

and when you associate feelings of resentment and hostility with it.

the pendulum just swings stronger.

the feelings will give it MORE power, and will instantly become a physical reality within your life.

BUT WHY DOES IT DO THIS?

you see, when you associate feelings of hostility with the thing that you DONT want in your life..

you are simply telling your brain that you WANT to get rid of it, but you can’t.

think about it, if you’re angry that something is in your life, doesn’t that just mean you should take it out?

oh, but you can’t, and that’s why you’re angry about it.

each time you get angry that you are a certain type of way, you are simply reinforcing the IDENTITY of yourself that is STUCK with that negative thing in your life.

THE MAIN POINT IS.

maybe it’s your lack of discipline, or your bad habits.

if you associate feelings of resentment and hostility with those things.

it will not go away, however it will strengthen.

REMEMBER THIS.

your mind does not care about what emotion you’re thinking about.

it just sees it as energy.

and since anger is one of the most intense emotions…

if you link anger to the thing that you DON’T want in your life, it will inevitably stay.

since you’re just putting MORE energy into that negative pendulum, it will sway stronger, you understand?

so naturally, what we have to do…

is defeat it.

you either defeat it fully, or it will stay.

which we will discuss in this module.

DEFEATING THE PENDULUM.

the MOST important condition for successfully defeating a pendulum..

is simply to refuse to get into conflict with it.

the more you actively try to fight it off, the more actively it will get you down and pressure you.

you can repeat endlessly “leave me alone! get away from me!” and think you are fighting them off..

but actually, you are just providing them with energy making them cling to you even more.

SECONDLY.

you will act like this from now on.

you do not have the right to judge or change anything.

and here’s what i mean by this.

Everything you see and experience, should be accepted as if they were painting on display.

whether you like them or not, you cannot change them.

there may be many painting in a gallery that are not to your taste…

but it would never occur to you that you DEMAND to get them removed from the gallery.

once you have recognised the pendulums right to exist, you also have the right to leave it behind.

the first step to change is getting rid off all emotion attached to things, this is how you think logically.

we covered this in the stoicism course.

you look at things with a lens of emotion, whether you are conscious about it or not.

which is obviously not what we want, since we don’t see the bigger picture.

and to GET RID of that lens of emotion, you need to start thinking like this :

everything in the universe has a RIGHT to exist.

there is no inherent emotion attached to things.

EVERYTHING is simply happening, just as the paintings in the gallery.

you should NOT demand for the painting to be removed… rather you should just go look at the other paintings.

THE MAIN POINT IS.

do not fight it, do not judge it, do not get angry at it, no matter what it is.

if you’re just relapsed on pornhub or some dumb shit, which your probably past now, but let's use it as an example.

if you get angry at yourself, i promise you, you will do it again.

however, if you just think “oh, I didn’t like that road, let me just go the other way”

or “oh, I didn’t like that script, let me go back to the other one”

once you associate ANY emotion, especially anger with something.

the pendulum starts to gain momentum…

you need to start seeing things as paintings in a gallery, do not get pissed at yourself for relapsing or whatever, it will only do you harm, and the pendulum starts gaining momentum.

the point is, ANGER WILL NEVER DO YOU ANY GOOD, unless it’s an FTE (the point where your life gets so bad, that you have to change, basically the breaking point)

but an FTE is separate, a breaking point will change anyone.

Before You Can Understand What It Means To Choose, You Have To Know How To Decline.

most people have a vague idea of what they want, but know EXACTLY what they do not want. (hence why “anti-visions” work.)

in an effort to get rid of things or events that people don’t want in their life…

people act in a manner that has the OPPOSITE effect, it will stay there longer.

and here’s how it stays there.

THIS IS WHY IT HAPPENS.

to decline something you don’t want.

you have to first accept it’s existence.

the word “accept” in this context does NOT mean to embrace something and let it into your personal space.

it means simply to recognize it’s right to existence and then pass by it, as if it was just a painting, or a path.

to accept and let go of something means to consider the Existence of something, and let it pass.

wave goodbye as it leaves, with NO EMOTION behind it.

this is why getting opposed and angry to something NEVER works.

i mean, when has a time been that you’ve gotten angry at yourself for doing something “bad”, and then moved on to have a productive day as normal.

it will never happen, since you’ve gave the wrong pendulum momentum.

A PRIME EXAMPLE OF THIS?

a woman always goes back to her toxic ex.

or the guy that made it after they broke up.

but why?

they have feelings of anger/jealousy/resentment.

and the pendulum starts to swing.

this is why girls will always be talking about the guy who is toxic, instead of the guy who is always nice and sweet.

a girl never says “he’s so sweet” randomly throughout the day.

but she will say “i fucking hate him” randomly through the day.

infact, it will take up all of her mind, all day.

that’s why you will always go back.

it all comes down to energy, and as we said before, negative emotion like anger and resentment has the most energy.

meaning the pendulum will gain more energy into it, if you think with anger towards something.

but what the person does not realize, is that they could just simply delete apples from their existence.

and the pendulum will be defeated.

It does not matter whether you love or hate.

if you have emotions latched onto a certain part of your life.

the pendulum will swing, because it is just cause and effect.

if you push a swing, it will move.

and the only way it stops moving, is if you stop pushing it.

To Eradicate Something Forever, You Just Have To Stop Thinking About It.

pass by it with indifference to your emotions, and it will disappear.

to eliminate something from your life, do not try and avoid it…

since avoiding it is impossible, just like you can’t avoid looking at a bad painting.

“avoiding it” means you have to let it into your personal space, because you are trying to get rid of it.

but to “ignore” something means not to react to it, and not have to experience it’s presence into your space.

imagine you are a radio, if you wake up every day listening to a radio program you hate…

all you have to do is tune in to a different frequency, you don’t get mad at the radio program you hate, since you have the option to change.

if you are mad at something that is in your life, you are conditioning your brain to think that you cannot change, and that is why you are mad.

You Will Never Be Able To Avoid Destructive Pendulums.

you might think you can protect yourself from unwanted things by putting up boundaries between yourself and everything “bad”

but this is just an illusion, since it will never work.

it’s like saying “i am a blank wall, i see nothing, i hear nothing, I don’t know anything, I won’t talk to anyone, I am mentally unavailable.”

all you can do, is realize that everything that happens has no inherent emotion attached to it.

procrastinating work and watching youtube all day doesn’t have an emotion attached to it, you just create the emotion.

which just feeds BACK into the pendulum, making you procrastinate more.

but on the other hand…

imagine you just thought “oh, I’m procrastinating work, and I’m watching youtube.”

“this script does not align with the place i want to end up at, I should go in a different direction”

instead of thinking “fuck im such a pussy I don’t even want to do the work I said I was going to, blah blah blah”

realise that these moments will always happen, you will procrastinate work, it’s just about how you react to it.

and whatever you do, do not feed back into the pendulum with your anger, since it will only push it forward.

if you want to get rid of it, you NEED to start seeing it as a separate being TO you.

what do I mean by this?

sometimes you can catch yourself on youtube, and it feels like it’s a “part” of yourself.

that’s because you have emotions attached to it.

but imagine the other side.

imagine you switched your identity, and now you’re a hard worker.

imagine you’ve been on monk mode for a whole 30 days, no youtube, no nothing.

and then you catch yourself on youtube again.

you’re not going to say “fucking hell, I’m on youtube, what am i doing? I'm meant to be working, I’m such a pussy.”

you will just say “oh, I just caught myself on youtube by accident, this isn’t what I’m meant to be doing”

you close the tab, and you’re back to work.

it’s easy to be caught up in your head and emotion.

and you need to realize, that’s the pendulum just gaining momentum.

so, to get rid of it gaining momentum, is simply to view it as a thing that you cannot change, therefore, associating no anger or emotion with it.

meaning you have no momentum in this “destructive pendulum” and you get back to work with no friction.

the second you start getting angry at yourself, is the second that you reinforce the identity in yourself that the “one thing” you don’t want in your life, you cannot get rid of.

since we talked about this earlier.

getting angry at something in your life, is just telling your brain that you cannot get rid of it.

because if you was to be able to, you would have gotten rid of it already.

MEANING, as soon as you get angry at something, it means it’ll just happen more, since your brain believes you can’t get rid of it.

The “Bad Thing” Has One Goal.

a dog starts following you and barking loudly.

if you turn around to face the dog, it will bark even louder.

but if you ignore the dog, it will simply switch it’s attention to someone else and will be not even offended that you paid it no attention.

the dog is too absorbed in it’s own goal of sourcing energy to think about anything else.

imagine the “bad habit” or whatever.

the “bad thing” always had one goal, and that goal is to grab your attention, so it can feed off your energy.

a bit like that barking dog.

but if you give it no attention or energy, it will simply find the next person to bark at.

in other words, the pendulum has no momentum, so it will not grab you.

and you’ve got rid of that dog.

but, this wasn’t about dogs.

this was about pendulums, and things you want to get rid of in your life.

they are simply just feeding off energy, which is your emotion.

so, like a cancer, you have to kill it.

and you kill it by starvation, depriving it of it’s life force.

energy, emotional attachment.

  • The Pendulum Does Not Care If You Hate Or Love It, Energy Is Energy, It Will Gain Momentum.

  • Anger Is The Strongest Emotion, Meaning It Will Feed Into The Destructive Habit/Pendulum The Most.
  • Once You Experience Any Resentment/Anger To That Destructive Pendulum, It Just Gives It More Momentum.
  • To Eradicate Something Forever, You Just Have To Stop Thinking About It With Emotion, Recognize That It Just Exists, As A Painting In The Gallery.
  • You Will Never Be Able To Avoid Destructive Pendulums, But You Can Ignore All Emotional Attachment With It.
  • You Kill Pendulums Like A Cancer, Depriving It From It’s Life Force, Which Is Your Emotional Energy.

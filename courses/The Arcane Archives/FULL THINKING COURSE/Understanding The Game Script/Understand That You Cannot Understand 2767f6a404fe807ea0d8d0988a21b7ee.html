Understand That You Cannot Understand.

remember this.

The important thing is to decide from the very beginning...

is to not to torture yourself with questions of why and how exactly this all works.

this would be as productive as a child asking a physicist why gravity attracts.

The scientist would reply: “Because of the law of gravity.”

The child then follows with the next question: “How does the law of gravity work?

"Why do different bodies of mass attract?”

There is no answer to this question, so we will leave the pointless task of trying to explain these things...

for it is not ours to know and understand everything.

We will simply enjoy the results of the alternatives model.

remember that.

don't be too caught up in these "models" or "scripts"

because as humans, we can't comprehend it.

simply enjoy the benefits, and the things that I teach you.

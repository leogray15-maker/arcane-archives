Planting The Seed Of Awareness.

Know yourself thoroughly.

your “emotional self” works on ignorance.

the “higher self” works on awareness.

awareness of your emotions.

The Moment You Become Aware Of How It Operates…

is the moment when it loses the hold onto you.

and you start to become your higher self.

and the “emotional self” can be tamed, and controlled.

Catch Yourself In Emotional Action.

we talked about this earlier.

but let’s amplify it’s importance

You Must Learn How You Act Under Situations.

whenever you feel excitement, how do you act?

do you start wanting to tell people what just happened?

when you feel anger, how do you act?

do you just get pissed off at everything?

What Weaknesses Come Out In These Moments?

this is just a thing to keep in mind.

you will forget about this course module in a couple minutes after reading.

but the beautiful thing is…

Whenever A Situation Pops Up.

you know, when you “feel” the emotion being inflamed.

your brain will flashback to this course, trust me.

you don’t need to worry about “forgetting” as long as you’re reading these words rn.

and you’re understanding what I’m talking about.

It’s like planting a seed...

that will start growing when a situation pops up.

ACTIONABLE STEP.

when a situation pops up when you feel emotion.

whether it be :

  • Excitement
  • Anger
  • Resentment/Hatred towards something or someone.
  • Doubt
  • Fear
  • Stress.
  • Joy.

examine your flaws.

how do you act differently from your rational self.

do you see life through the lens of the emotion?

or do you zoom out and see the bigger picture?

LOOK AT LIFE IN 3RD PERSON.

always ask yourself.

“why am I feeling this emotion?”

“what am I doing that is different from my rational self under this emotion?”

THIS IS A LIFELONG PROCESS.

but it’s good to be aware of it now.

read this module until you understand it.

and you’ve planted the seed.

now you wait for it to sprout.

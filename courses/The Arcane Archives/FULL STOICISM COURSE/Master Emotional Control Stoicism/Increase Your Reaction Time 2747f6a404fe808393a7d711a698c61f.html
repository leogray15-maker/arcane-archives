Increase Your Reaction Time.

the power of all these practices…

they all come from practice and repetition.

just like any skill, any endeavour.

you level it up, with practice, repetition.

just like in a video game.

When Some Decision Pops Up.

you must train yourself to react quickly, and examine your perspective.

are you seeing life through one lens of an emotion, or the bigger picture in that moment?

EXAMPLE.

It’s Time To Sit Down And Work.

you are met with your decision.

and you have a thought of “I’ll just do it later, after gym”

EXAMINE YOUR PERSPECTIVE.

in that moment.

your confirmation bias will look for evidence that it’s the right choice.

which will make you actually procrastinate it.

so you need to…

ZOOM OUT, LOOK AT THE BIGGER PICTURE.

is that your confirmation bias working with the emotion of laziness?

is your brain just confirming it's own beliefs of "this is a good decision" ?

or is it ACTUALLY a good decision to do it after gym?

most of the time, it’s not.

it's just procrastionation.

but you don't think it is, because your confirmation bias in your brain tells you to AVOID PAIN.

all that matters, is that you look at it with a 3rd person perspective.

and not through the lens of one emotion.

AWARENESS IS KEY.

and you become aware with practice and repetition.

this is why I’m drilling this concept into you so often.

your brain will hear a piece of advice so much times.

that it eventually thinks “this information has been said so many times, I must store it, since it might be of good use”

this is also a part of the group bias.

when you hear something so often, your brain will hear it as truth.

ACTIONABLE STEP.

IMPROVE YOUR REACTION TIME TO EMOTION.

practice doing this…

WHEN A DECISION COMES.

ANY decision.

you need to program yourself to instantly zoom out.

look at it from a rational, logical point of view.

look at it from outside, and take into account all of the evidence.

and make a decision based off evidence.

not any confirmation biases.

and not any lens of emotion.

THIS TAKES PRACTICE, IT TAKES REPS.

but that seed is now planted in your head.

when a decision pops up.

your brain will remind you to zoom out, and look at it from a 3rd person perspective.

this is actually magic.

and you’re actually becoming superior, with this information.

MORAL OF REACTION TIME.

  • EVERY TIME A DECISION POPS UP, ZOOM OUT ASAP.
  • PERFECT THIS WITH PRACTICE.
  • UNDERSTAND EVERY PART OF THIS MODULE DEEPLY TO PLANT THAT SEED, YOUR BRAIN WILL REMIND YOU OF THIS WHEN A DECISION POPS UP.

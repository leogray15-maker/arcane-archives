Sudden Wins/Losses Are Dangerous

whether it be a sudden win, or a loss.

these are SO dangerous…

especially, the wins.

Understanding The Science.

when you experience a sudden win, or loss.

powerful chemicals are released in your brain…

these chemicals give a powerful feeling of attention to the emotion.

leading to the desire to repeat this experience.

Reminding You Again…

the reason we will desire to repeat this experience.

even if we’re angry.

is because our brain confirms it’s beliefs.

ik you’re sick of hearing that, but I want to make sure it’s drilled into you.

even when you’re feeling anger, pissed off.

your brain subconsciously will want MORE anger because it wants to confirm it’s biases.

switching back to a happy normal mood after being angry tells the brain “why did you just get angry, are you retarded?”

so of course it doesn’t want that.

This Desire To Repeat This Experience Is Dangerous.

this leads to a manic kind of experience.

just wanting more and more of that emotion.

you forget all the potential negatives to situations when you’re excited.

and you forget all of the potential positives when you’re angry.

When Wins Come Quickly, We Lose Composure.

say you building up a business.

you’re building and building.

it’s hard, you need to keep reminding yourself “remember, just burst that pipe, don’t quit.”

You Finally Get The First Sale.

and you lose all composure.

I remember when this happened to me.

My Experience.

I was just at the start of this business, the writing.

and I’ve just finished my course.

I was working so hard on it.

8 hours a day.

and I finally release it.

So What Do I Do?

I make an announcement.

I post it on twitter.

not expecting any sales.

and so my work for the day is finished.

I go to the gym.

but I don’t go alone.

I bring my friend….

WE FINISH A CHEST DAY.

and we’re walking back, talking.

on a dark night, winter, grind.

we’re pretty quiet, just focused on the grind.

focused on what’s to come next.

locked in, no emotion.

AND SUDDENLY.

I check my phone.

an email notification…

from stripe.

“a payment of £197 has been sent to you by x”

I just say “what the fuck”

“bro, I just got a sale on my course”

and he’s like what? bro, how the fuck.

he didn’t know I’m starting this business, this was the start…

So Guess What I started Doing…

I started to tell him the ins and outs

of how I did it, etc.

I was so excited that I didn’t care.

I was looking through life through the lens of excitement.

you might think this is good, but….

TELLING OTHERS ABOUT YOUR GOALS.

it’s not good. and you know that.

this was a key principle of mine.

“don’t tell anyone about your business”

not even friends.

and at that moment, I was so excited, that all of that went out the window.

Now I’ve Broke A Promise To Myself.

my values are questioned.

and now that friend might tell others, now they might question me.

now they might look at my posts.

and I can’t be bothered with that, it’s long.

After the excitement wore off.

I went back to reality.

I thought “fuck, what if I get evil eye?”

you don’t know who is envious of you.

you don’t tell others your goals, or achievements.

but when you get that notification of a sale.

you might just lose all composure.

Sudden Positives Lead To Negative Decisions.

when you’re in that mood.

the potential negatives disappear.

The Gambler.

he’s been gambling all night, hoping for a jackpot.

in a bad mood, some wins, some losses.

but all of the sudden.

he wins big.

HE HITS THE JACKPOT.

he’s overjoyed.

but he’s met with something…

“double or nothing?”

AND IN AN INSTANT.

his subconscious mind getting rid of all the negatives.

just subconscious thoughts of “fuck, we can become EXTREMELY rich”

“If we lose, we can always go again.”

So He Pulls The Lever.

he forgets these machines are rigged

and…

all his jackpot, is gone.

Now It seeps in.

he starts looking at it from a rational perspective.

and not through the lens of a singular emotion.

now he’s literally having suicidal thoughts.

this is the trap of quick wins.

I Could Talk To You About Quick Losses.

and how that affects you.

but you already know that.

anger makes you see the world in angry.

but the quick wins?

they seem like a good thing.

but they are hidden traps.

don’t fall.

You Are INSANELY Prone To This.

I used a gambling example.

but you need to realise.

if you’re on this path to success.

to business success.

you’re the most prone.

and you know that.

don't think you're better than human nature.

be aware of this, and succeed.

THE SOLUTION.

it’s simple.

when you experience unusual wins or losses.

out of nowhere, you had a guy email you for a refund.

out of nowhere, you get a sale.

You Step Back.

counteract the win/loss with logic.

be wary of sudden success especially.

Sudden Success has no foundation.

you first sale might just be luck.

your video that blows up might be luck.

they are not built on anything that had a solid foundation.

and they have an addictive pull, just like gambling.

And The Fall.

is Always Painful.

MORAL OF QUICK GAINS/LOSSES.

step back, and look at the situation for what it really is.

don’t get caught up in the emotion.

this is how you become rational.

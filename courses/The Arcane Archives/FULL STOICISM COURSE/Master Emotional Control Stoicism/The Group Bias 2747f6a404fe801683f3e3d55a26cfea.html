but…

this isn’t what it’s about.

there’s no more pain than that.

it feels like you’re out of place.

you don’t belong here.

and it feels like you’d just want to escape.

your primal brain thinks "we're about to be kicked from the tribe"

"that means death"

and people are agreeing, you’re gathering tribe members.

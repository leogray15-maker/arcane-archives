Understanding The Law Of Irrationality.

you think you control your actions, your emotions, your goals.

but you’re unaware of how emotions actually dominate you.

they make you go towards ideas that make heighten you ego.

you look for evidence the confirms what you want to believe.

they make you see things that you want to see, depending on your mood.

DON’T BELIEVE ME?

if you’ve decided that your dream car is a Mercedes.

everywhere you go.

you will see a Mercedes.

and you’ll think to yourself “shit, since when was there so much people who have a Mercedes?”

but the reality is, it was always there.

but your brain had no reason to acknowledge them.

but now, you say you want a Mercedes.

so your brain will naturally want to bring you evidence.

that getting a Mercedes is ACTUALLY possible.

and how will it do that?

it will point out all the Mercedes cars that you see.

so you think “there’s so much Mercedes cars everywhere, it must mean it’s easy to get.”

but in reality, it's just blocking out all of the other cars.

which has the illusion that there are more mercedes cars than there actually are.

which is just your brain looking for the evidence of what you WANT to believe.

which makes you think “why does everyone have a Mercedes all of the sudden?”

your brain has a bias.

YOU WILL NOW SEE IT.

next time you go out.

just think of seeing as much Mercedes cars as possible.

you will forget about all the other cars.

and your brain will just scout for a Mercedes.

you’ll start to think “fuck, my brain really does just confirm what I want to see”

it’s all about perspective.

IF YOU HAVE A CERTAIN BELIEF.

for example.

“short guys can’t get girls”

your brain is going to point that out.

EVERY SINGLE TIME you see a tall guy with a girl.

your brain is going to say “see, I told you”

and whenever you DO see a short guy with a girl.

your brain blocks it out, and goes back to focus on the tall guys with the girl.

it’s just finding evidence to support that belief.

that’s the nature of your brain, that’s what it does.

YOU NEED TO BE AWARE OF BELIEFS.

because you’ve probably been living your whole life.

living with these “fake evidences”

you might have the belief of “money is scarce.”

which is stopping you from succeeding in business.

because now you have the belief of “money is scarce, so they won’t buy my product.”

so it goes without saying.

we NEED to reverse that.

THE FIRST STEP TO REVERSING THAT.

is something we like to call.

“rationality”

RATIONALITY.

rationality is just.

the ability to SEE these things.

from a 3rd person perspective.

for example.

I just explained how your brain will always look for evidence correlated with what you believe.

SO NOW.

whenever that thing happens.

whenever you go outside the next time, and you tell yourself “short guys actually do get girls.”

then you go into the town centre.

you will see LOADS of short guys with some absolute stunners.

and your brain will have a flashback to this course.

and you’ll think “wow, my brain really does just confirm beliefs”

REALITY IS JUST PERCEPTION.

everyone views reality through a different lens.

one guy thinks that short guys can get girls.

one guy thinks they can’t.

AND NONE OF THEM ARE IN THE WRONG.

because your brain just confirms what it "believes"

but now you’re aware of that.

this is the first step to understanding human nature.

SUMMARY.

  • YOUR BRAIN JUST CONFIRMS YOUR CURRENT BELIEFS.
  • YOUR BRAIN WILL AMPLIFY YOUR BELIEFS., AND BLOCK OUT EVERYTHING ELSE.
  • BE AWARE OF THIS FACT.
  • REALITY IS JUST A PERCEPTION, EVERYBODY SEES IT DIFFERENTLY

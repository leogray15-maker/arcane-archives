1ST STEP) HITTING THE BALLOON.

you will start on the first step.

you just marketed the fuck out of the idea in the last section.

you need to keep this in mind...

your reader is a retard.

you need to write as if you was writing to a child.

I said this in the inbound method... just pretend you are writing to your friend that cannot keep attention.

keep on making them remember the benefit if they do this.

and the negative of what will happen if they don't do this.

here's why...

PEOPLES ATTENTION SPANS ARE SO SHIT.

listen, this is an analogy I made up myself...

and I'm proud of it.

people's attention spans simply work like a balloon.

you hit it up in the air, and it starts to fall down.

you need to keep hitting it, keep hitting it so it doesn't touch the group.

because as soon as it touches the ground.....

they leave.

HOW DO WE PUT THIS INTO ACTION?

to simply put it, the way I do it.

is before every step.

for example, you're telling them the 5 steps to get rid of brain fog.

you list out the first step.

EXAMPLE.

5 STEPS FOR GETTING RID OF BRAIN FOG.

1. DIET

now you need to grab their attention again, because they heard diet and they got bored.

personally I'd switch just "diet" into something new, for example.

instead of diet, make it sound like a new opportunity.

we know this works from the copywriting course... think of the new/only.

"OPTIMIZING YOUR BODY FUEL" sounds much more interesting than diet.

that's something a child would actually look at and think woah what's this.

then you say some shit that will hit the balloon back up into the sky again.

"now don't click off yet...

you heard diet, now your brain is telling you to click off because you've satiated your curiosity.

this is why you're broke.

success is boring work, and this is part of it.

LISTEN TO ME, DO NOT TRUST YOUR BRAIN

YOUR BRAIN IS THE THING THAT BROUGHT YOU TO THIS POINT

but bro... remember.

do this in your own words, not mine, keep authentic.

WHAT IS HE GONNA THINK NOW?

now after that they'll think fuck he got me there.

maybe I should listen to this guy.

keep hitting the balloon.

this is why I keep adding these Summaries, so you don't get lost and think fuck this I'm gonna click off.

but speaking of summaries.

1ST STEP SUMMARY.

  • START EVERY STEP, METHOD, WAY, HACK WITH SOMETHING "NEW"
  • HIT THE BALLOON BACK UP IN THE AIR.

hit the balloon back up by :

  • REMINDING THEM OF THE BENEFIT
  • REMINDING THEM OF THE NEGATIVE.
  • TELLING THEM WHAT THIS STEP IS GONNA DO FOR YOU.
  • TELLING THEM "IF YOU SKIP THIS STEP THEN _______"

just think of this as you're speaking to your friend who can't pay attention, keep reminding them, remember that.

2ND STEP) THE INFORMATION.

so... you've gave them the promise, etc..

now you need to fulfil the promise.

and there's a specific format to tell it in.

we've covered that you need to hit the balloon back up in the air.

now it's time to speak to the balloon while it's in the air.

YOU NEED TO JUST GIVE, GIVE, GIVE.

as much value as possible.

you got that?

you are speaking to a person who is at the low point, and you are trying to take them to the high point.

you are either solving a problem for them.. or just giving them a benefit to their life.

and here's what you do.

YOU JUST GIVE ALL THE SECRETS.

everything you've learnt about that problem... tell it to them.

you have to tap into the readers emotion, think from the perspective of the reader, just think "what have I missed that I could add more?"

do not think "I'm giving away too much, I won't be able to sell my product to them later"

THAT IS A PUSSY MINDSET.

and here's why...

you actually have to change their lives with your free content if you want them to buy.

and also, people don't buy courses for the sake of information, they buy it for the sake of having ALL the information, in one place.

please bro, just give as much information as possible.

it's meant to be LOADED with info that takes the reader from the low point.. to the high point.

do it in your own words.

3RD STEP) GIVE AN ACTIONABLE STEP.

this is hitting the balloon up once again.

people LOVE clarity.

I've said this many times.

people love straight up getting told what to do.

so you need to tell them what to do, straight up.

this is how we portray clarity.

this is how we get them to take action.

YOU BASICALLY SUMMARISE ALL THE INFO YOU SAID WITH AN ACTIONABLE STEP.

JUST REMEMBER THIS.

PEOPLE LOOOOOVE CLARITY.

if you want, I'll give you an actionable step if you really want to take this proper serious.

write "people love clarity" somewhere, just so you remember.

I cannot stress this enough....

THE ACTIONABLE STEP MUST BE CLEAR.

if you tell them as an actionable step, go hike up a mountain or some shit.

here's a module that summarises how this works.

PICK THE HIGHEST LEVERAGE ACTION

(the action with the lowest amount of effort, but highest amount of result)

and give that to them as an action step.

REMEMBER, you need them to take action because if they change their live from YOUR content, they will become a "true fan"

SUMMARY FOR ACTION STEP :

  • GET THEM TO DO AN ACTION STEP AT THE END OF EVERY STEP
  • MAKE SURE IT IS LOW EFFORT, BUT YEILDS HIGH RESULTS.

Possession Has No Meaning.

so.. this is kind of a "playoff" from it's not yours, until it's yours.

but I'll also want to add one thing to it.

when it's yours, it's STILL not yours.

once you make this mindset switch, you become controlled and realise the reality of life.

this applies to everything, from clients, women, money, etc.

EVEN when it's yours, it's still not yours.

nothing is ever attached to you.

from your personality, your identity, money, etc.

it's never attached.

you can think you have a customer because they give you a good testimonial, you think they'll stay forever.

they can leave the next month.

you think a woman will stay with you forever because she said so.

she can leave the next day.

you think you've got the money because it's in your bank.

the customer can chargeback.

this will save you a LOT of emotional turmoil.

you know what, you should maybe even hang this on your wall, or make this your background :

"possession without meaning, action without expectation"

you should never expect anything from the world.

and even MORE SO you should never expect anything from PEOPLE.

you should never expect anything.

never. never. never.

when you send out a product/email launch, expect it to do NOTHING.

expect NO SALES.

you know why?

this is a quote from ray dalio :

"there are anxious times in every entrepreneurs career, when your expectations of what should be happening aren't aligned with what is happening, and you don't know if what you're looking at are opportunities or catastrophic mistakes" - ray dalio

"when your expectations of what should be happening aren't aligned with what is happening"

"you don't know if what you're looking at are opportunities or catastrophic mistakes"

so, I want you to instill this simple mindset :

act without expectation.

the reason most quit is because their expectations of what should be happening aren't aligned with WHAT is happening.

people expect their first sale in a week, and it doesn't happen, they think they made a mistake, they quit.

but the ones who DONT EXPECT ANYTHING, they see it as a normal day.

they act without expectation.

they put out their BEST WORK with the mindset of "there is no expectation"

alex hormozi talks about this in a unique way.

you shouldn't focus on the results, just focus on emptying your gas tank.

don't look for results, look for work.

focus on the inputs, and the outputs will follow.

DO NOT EVER EXPECT SOMETHING TO HAPPEN.

and when it "does" happen.

don't expect to keep it.

£100K in the bank should be treated like £100 - 1stman

when you land a client, you should not expect them to stay.

rather, you should work as if they're going to leave you tomorrow.

never slack, as you may be stranded.

james dyson said his most biggest driver TO THIS DAY is the fear of failure.

he literally cannot "fail", he has billions in the bank.

but he still acts like he could lose it all tomorrow.

possession has no meaning.

possession of a woman has no meaning

possession of money has no meaning

possession of a client has no meaning

possession of ANYTHING has no meaning.

everything that you own can be gone tomorrow.

act like that, and make the best out of it.

so, let's summarise this :

"possession without meaning, action without expectation"

LIVE BY THAT RULE.

this way, you become emotionally numb to everything that happens, as you expected it anyway.

possession without meaning, action without expectation.

write it down.

once you realise you own nothing, you own everything.

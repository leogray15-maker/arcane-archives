How To Become Patient.

The best character trait you can have in business and life is patience.

After all… you just need discipline and patience to succeed.

That’s literally all it is.

Want to become ripped? Just stay patient and disciplined.

Want to become rich? Just work without results… stay patient and disciplined.

That’s what you do as a man.

But here’s the thing…

What actually Is “patience” and how do you become it?

And to be honest, it’s simple.

It comes down to one sentence :

Patience is simply finding something else to do in the meantime.

When you’re posting videos daily on tiktok hoping to blow up…

You don’t go checking your video performance every hour, you find something to do.

This is what I did when I was on the come up…

I used to post videos on tiktok and IMMEDIATELY I’d force close the app.

I’d then check it later tomorrow when it’s time for my next post.

And when I log on to make my next post… I can just check if the video has blown up or not… if I had “burst the pipe”

But if it didn’t burst the pipe…?

It doesn’t matter because I got on here to post another video, not to check the analytics of the last one.

This is the potential one that will burst the pipe too...

You just keep planting seeds, every piece of content is a seed that is potential to burst the pipe.

Each sales call is a potential to burst the pipe.

But here’s the thing…

You do not want to focus on the seeds sprouting, you want to focus on just planting and planting…

Inevitably they will grow, but if you just watch them and wait for them to grow… it will feel like an eternity.

So instead you should just focus on planting and hoping for no results…

Don’t think “this next video is gonna burst the pipe”

Just think “I’m just planting another seed”

You keep on planting, don’t look at the growth…

And one day you will have a full garden.

That’s how it goes, just do something in the meantime, that’s all patience is.

Focus on the inputs, and not the outputs.

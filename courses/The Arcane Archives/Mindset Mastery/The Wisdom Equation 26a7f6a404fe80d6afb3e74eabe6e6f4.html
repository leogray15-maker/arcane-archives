WHAT IS KNOWLEDGE?

knowledge is simply the accumulation of :

  • facts.
  • data.
  • information.

e.g "eating carbs before your work is bad"

WHAT IS UNDERSTANDING?

understanding is the understanding of the knowledge.

the :

  • WHAT?
  • WHY?
  • HOW?

let's use the carbs example listen above.

e.g "eating carbs spikes insulin, which causes tiredness and fatigue"

why does it equal bad work?

because you cannot focus properly at the important work task.

WHAT IS WISDOM?

wisdom is made through the last 2.

Knowledge, and understanding.

WISDOM IS THE ABILITY TO USE THE KNOWLEDGE IN YOUR OWN LIFE.

let me explain easier.

wisdom is simply :

  • getting that information
  • understanding why it is.
  • applying it to your own life.

the more things you do that with, the more wisdom you have gathered.

THE APPLICATION OF KNOWLEDGE = WISDOM.

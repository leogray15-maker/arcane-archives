Reframe Your Mind To Enjoy Risks.

Excitement is the same as nervousness.

When you feel excited or nervous, your brain is actually feeling the same chemicals being released.

The thing that separates anxiety from excitement is the way you look at the feeling.

But what does this mean…?

This means you can change the way you look at anxiety.

Imagine this…

You’re about to do a big launch for a sale and you’re about to send the first promo email out…

You’re scared because you don’t actually know how much money you’ll make.

You have that feeling in your stomach that you can get nothing or EVERYTHING.

But it’s a mix of between excitement and nervousness…

That’s because your brain registers it as the same thing, neurologically.

So what should you actually do when you feel “nervous” ?

You should reframe it as excitement.

Literally just tell yourself that your body and mind is feeling like “nervous” because it is excited.

And there is no reason you can’t do that… because chemically it’s the same thing.

You just have to program yourself to believe that you’re excited, and you will be an overall more successful person.. since it will make you enjoy taking more risks.

Enjoying more risks means more risks taken…

And more risks taken means more money to be made.

That’s how it goes.

Start reframing your mind to enjoy risks.

There is no such thing as nervousness, only excitement.

(also... if your goals don't make you "nervous", you're doing them wrong)

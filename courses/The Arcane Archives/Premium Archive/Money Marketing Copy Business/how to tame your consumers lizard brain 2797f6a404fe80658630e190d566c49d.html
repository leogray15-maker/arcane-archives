The $50 Million Discovery

at agora financial, we tracked reader behavior across 10,000+ promos.

the pattern was fucking brutal:

  • "better way to invest" = 0.8% conversion
  • "revolutionary trading discovery" = 4.2% conversion

same product. same list. 5x difference.

why? one word: NEW.

The Hope Equation Nobody Talks About

here's what's actually happening in your prospect's brain:

they've been burned before.

  • bought the weight loss program (still fat)
  • tried the business course (still broke)
  • followed the dating advice (still alone)

when you say "better way to lose weight" their brain remembers every failure.

but when you say "japanese enzyme that dissolves fat while you sleep" - their brain lights up.

why? because NEW = HOPE.

if it's new, their past failures don't matter. if it's new, maybe this time will be different. if it's new, there's still a chance.

The Categorization Death Trap

your brain is a lazy fuck. it wants to sort everything into three buckets:

  1. threat (pay attention)
  1. opportunity (investigate)
  1. boring (ignore)

99% of copy lands in bucket #3 because it sounds familiar.

examples that die instantly:

  • "proven system for..."
  • "step-by-step guide to..."
  • "secrets of successful..."

the brain thinks: "heard it, tried it, didn't work, NEXT"

The Power Words That Actually Trigger "New"

forget generic power word lists. these specific words bypass categorization:

  • breakthrough - implies old methods are obsolete
  • discovery - suggests hidden knowledge just found
  • weird trick - pattern interrupt, can't categorize
  • loophole - exclusive advantage others missed
  • glitch - temporary opportunity, act fast

but here's the key - the word means nothing without the angle.

The ONLY Multiplier

new gets attention. ONLY gets action.

new says: "this is different" only says: "you can't get this anywhere else"

together they create urgency through exclusivity.

power combinations:

  • "new discovery, only available here"
  • "breakthrough method, exclusive to members"
  • "weird loophole, closing soon"

The 4-Step NEW Framework

  1. identify the real problem not "losing weight" but "nothing has worked before"
  1. find the unique mechanism not "eat less" but "hormone that changes fat storage"
  1. make it impossible to categorize use specificity that breaks pattern recognition
  1. add urgency through exclusivity new + only + deadline = immediate action

Case Study: $47M Promo Breakdown

"a world-changing new liquid could make you $38,000 richer"

why this worked:

  • "world-changing" = massive scope (not just another stock tip)
  • "new liquid" = wtf is that? (impossible to categorize)
  • "$38,000 richer" = specific believable number

the reader literally cannot compare this to anything they've seen.

THE IRRATIONAL MATH PROBLEM

rational math: product → if/then → outcome ONE step.

irrational math: product → if/then → if/then → if/then → if/then → outcome MULTIPLE steps.

rational: "take pill → lose weight"

irrational: "become copywriter → get clients → clients pay → make money → quit job → freedom"

each "if/then" = uncertainty

uncertainty = objection

more steps = more objections = lower conversion

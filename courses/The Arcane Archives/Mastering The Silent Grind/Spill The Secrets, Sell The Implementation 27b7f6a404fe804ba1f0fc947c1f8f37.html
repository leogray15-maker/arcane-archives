Spill The Secrets, Sell The Implementation

Simple fix

You don’t want to “leak too much” in your free content, and that’s reasonable, because what would you be able to sell?

Dan Koe talks about this.

You drizzle information here and there in your free content

And your course is simply the place where ALL of it is.

Plus, it’s paid.

And when it’s paid, people actually act on the information.

If you tell people something valuable for free, they likely won’t take action on it.

But when they’ve actually paid for it. Made an investment. They will act on it.

For example I drizzle mindsets like “burst the pipe” and analogies from my network in my free content.

People will skim over it, and they will think not too much of it, because it is free after all.

But if they’ve paid for it and actually internalised it. They will start saying that it changed their life.

In your free content, you drizzle out things to help people.

But your course/community is simply a collection of EVERYTHING you learnt in one place.

The value in exchange from money comes from the time saved, they don’t have to scour the internet for the information you put out there, it’s just a place where they can learn everything in one place.

Teach all the secrets you want.

Alex hormozi said “tell the secrets, sell the implementation”

The implementation in this case is simply everything in one place.

Hamza has a made a skool group called adonis library, and he labels it “Netflix for self improvement”

But if you go and watch all of his videos, you’ll know everything that he teaches anyway.

The difference is that it is all in one place.

Same reason why people pay for Netflix, you can get all that shit for free online.

But why do they pay? Convenience.

Spill the secrets, sell the implementation.

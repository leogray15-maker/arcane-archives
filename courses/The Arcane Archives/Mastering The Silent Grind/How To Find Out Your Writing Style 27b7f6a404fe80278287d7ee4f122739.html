How To Find Out Your Writing Style.

This one is from c.e, 14 year old active grafter, big respect.

Now this will cover writing…

and the simple answer it?

Test and iterate.

Finding yourself switching up your style is good, because it just means you’re testing different things.

BUT here’s the thing.

Once you find one that you “like”, then you should stick to it.

For a business example, it’d be this.

There is no correct “niche”, just the niche that you have the most experience in.

Really you can find a style that you fuck with, but after a while of each style you will fuck with ALL of them.

So really the question is…

What type of style will be nice for you?

Is it the late night deep writing about rewiring your brain?

For me, that’s what it is.

I started this off with a late night tweet just expressing my thoughts, that’s what I like doing.

And if you do too, you should do it.

Or you can do a “daytime” type of writing style where you’re giving life advice, it’s really your choice.

I can’t help you find a style that you like, but I can give you a direction.

And the answer to this one is simple.

Find out what you like.

It’s not necessarily finding the “right writing style”, it’s about finding what you like writing about.

And the thing that you like writing about?

Your brain will simply give it to you.

When you have a thought in mind to write about, do it.

Your brain provides you with the answers, you have the possibility in your subconscious for infinite intelligence.

People have created planes, bluetooth, radios.

Your brain has all the answers you need, you just need to let it work.

Go on a walk with nothing, and just think about what you want to do, how you want to write, why you want to write.

And there comes an answer.

The correct writing style will come from the style that you find a flow state in, which will be the style that your brain just sticks it out.

The point of writing is to have no set “style”

As long as your writing has as flow, it will work.

So really, you should just find the “flow” that you want to go in.

AND the most important thing is the topic you’re writing about.

If you write about something you’re passionate about, the writing will flow, you won’t have to think of a “style”

And that’s because your writing IS the style.

And if you don’t understand this, you will understand it sooner or later.

So what do you really need to do?

Just write, the correct way of writing will stick out.

Try new shit, since you’re new in writing and this journey.

Writing is a difficult one to master because you can’t actually “master” it.

Writing is just writing.

So, test different shit, and understand that your “Style” of writing IS your writing.

Do whichever one feels the best for you, and that’s it.

You’ll find your style with experience, just like how a boxer finds out if he’s southpaw or “peekaboo” mike tyson style.

I say this because I just watched the Tyson fight and lost £5 on a bet, peak. I'm going broke.

But yeah, you’ll find your style with just pure experience in the game.

you'll learn to love writing... and when you start "loving it", that's when you start to find the style.

it's not an instant process, just keep that in mind.

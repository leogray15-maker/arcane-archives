Proof By Process Or Device

This one is interesting, it's crazy. (Known as quiz funnels)

If you go on to any popular skincare or weight loss website, they'll make you do a quiz, they'll tell you what your skin type is and offer you a product to help you.

You have no choice but to believe it because of the initial commitment you put yourself through to going through that process.

In diet, sometimes a diet is built this way. There's some kind of diagnostic thing. Eat by your body type for example.

There's blood type too. There's a body type deal; there's a chart where you're a whatever, and now you decide what to eat.

Process and devices flip switches in people's heads.

The very fact that you are plugging data into this thing and it is lighting up, it is spinning, it is calculating, it is doing something; the very fact that you have a process that you go through to arrive at your recommendation flips switches in people's heads.

It does things to them.

Again, they've been hard conditioned to accept this.

The Rules Of Nature.

the rules of nature.

you see those who show weakness?

they get eaten.

nature's law. not mine.

when you share your struggles... your pain... your fears...

you become prey. not a predator. Simple nature.

remember... lions don't announce their hunger.

they hunt in silence instead. complaining does nothing.

your vulnerabilities? keep them locked away.

not because you're weak. but to build a perception of yourself to others.

Become the guy who CAN be trusted.

your word. it must be steel. unbreakable.

when you say "tomorrow at 5am" it's not a maybe. it's law.

nature doesn't "try" to make the sun rise. it just does. you must become like nature.

composure. watch a tiger when threatened.

it doesn't rage. it calculates. waits. strikes. that's power.

emotions are like waves. they rise. they fall.

make moves when the ocean is still.

not during the storm. not during the calm. but in between. that's where clarity lives.

look at nature again. does a bird build a nest after laying eggs?

no. foundation first. then life.

you must be selfish and make an abundance of money first. say no to everything. THEN you can be selfless.

that's not cruel. that's wisdom.

power. real power. comes from independence. when others hold your leash... you're not free. you're owned.

minimize these chains. break what you can. endure what you must.

but never forget… true power is in self-reliance.

routine.

it's boring, I agree.

but look at the trees.

same thing every day.

photosynthesis. growth. survival.

nature doesn't seek excitement. it seeks continuation.

your boring routine? that's your power source.

perception. even the weakest wolf stands tall. carries itself with pride.

because in nature... appearance is survival.

if you act like a success, in due time the universe will catch up and give you the success you "attract".

build a good foundation...

do this by learning and studying the 0.001%

not the modern day "entrepreneurs", even though they do help.

study people like carl jung, psychology, natural law.

it's like soil to a seed. when you know natures laws, that's a good foundation.

rich soil = strong growth.

poor soil = weak roots.

choose carefully. nature doesn't grow roses in desert sand.

and overthinking. seen a cheetah hesitate before the chase?

analysis is manmade. action is natural. choose your path.

remember, most cages are in your mind. most limits are imaginary. most fears are learned.

but nature... nature knows no limits. no ceilings. no boundaries.

empty your tank. every day. without celebration. without pride. like the sun doesn't celebrate setting. it just does.

that's power. that's masculinity. that's truth.

everything else? manmade illusions. societal lies. comfortable deceptions.

align with nature. become unstoppable.

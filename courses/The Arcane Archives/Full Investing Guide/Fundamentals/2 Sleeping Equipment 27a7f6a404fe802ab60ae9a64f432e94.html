2. Sleeping Equipment.

Sleep equipment is number 2, you should buy these things after you’ve locked in your perfect diet.

Diet comes first, then sleep optimisation comes second.

But what actually do you need for sleep optimisation?

It’s simple.

  • Blackout Curtains (Good For Work too)
  • Mouth And Nose Strips.
  • Earplugs/Noise Cancelling AirPods (Amazing For Work Too)
  • Lock On Your Door (Peace of Mind)
  • Sleep Mask (Optional)
  • Some Type Of Cooling (Fan, etc)

And also you could consider getting LED lights, switch them to red at night (increases natural melatonin)

And you could also consider blue light blocking glasses.

But the main things you need are good breathing, no light, no noise, right temperatures and also peace of mind for an optimal nights sleep.

These are very cheap investments, but insanely high ROI.

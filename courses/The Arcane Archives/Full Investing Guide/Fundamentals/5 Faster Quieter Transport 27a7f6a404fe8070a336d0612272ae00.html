5. Faster/Quieter Transport.

We’ve got health secured.

We’ve got your environment secured.

Now it’s time to get your time secured.

Now what do I actually mean by “faster transport”?

To simply put it, Ubers.

You may be going to places by walking or with a bus.

Imagine how much time you could actually save by ubering everywhere.

Obviously I don’t know your location… or how far you’re from gym… or school/work.

But I know one thing.

I uber everywhere.

When you uber everywhere, it’s honestly like teleporting.

You hop in the back of a car with AirPods in… maybe doing some work on your phone.

10 mins later you’re dropped off right outside your gym, it’s like you’ve teleported there.

And imagine that in comparison to walking or a bus…

You hop on a bus, you have to talk to the bus driver… scan your ticket… find a seat… deal with the people talking… it smells like piss on the bus… and the cherry on top which is taking WAY longer than it should be to reach your destination.

Compare that to hopping in the back of a car… getting driven privately in the back with no brain power wasted.

I mean… why do you think all the extremely successful have chauffeurs?

It’s to conserve brain power.

You don’t have to think about driving, you don’t have to think about when to put your foot on the brake, you’re just in the back doing shallow work on your phone.

There is no better investment than this honestly.

And if you think this is “expensive” and you’d rather take the long transport, you don’t value your time enough.

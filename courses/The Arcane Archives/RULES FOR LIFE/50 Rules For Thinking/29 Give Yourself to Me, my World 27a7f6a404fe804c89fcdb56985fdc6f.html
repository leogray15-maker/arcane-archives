29. Give Yourself to Me, my World

let’s unpack this.

Imagine you’re standing at the edge of your world, hands outstretched, waiting for it to come to you. You’ve got all these desires, dreams, and wants, and you’re expecting the world to come rushing to meet you, to deliver exactly what you want. But here's the thing—if you’re standing there just waiting, all you're doing is sending out an energy of desperation. You're essentially telling the world, "I don't have what I want, I need it," and guess what? The world reflects that. The more you try to force it, the more the world reflects your sense of lack. The universe mirrors your energy, and if you're vibrating desperation, that's exactly what you'll get back: more desperation, more longing, more chasing. The world doesn't hand over what you want simply because you're standing there and demanding it.

So, what’s the solution? It’s simple, but not always easy to implement. The shift comes when you stop waiting for the world to give to you and instead, take the first step toward your world. You have to move toward your desires—not as a passive receiver, but as an active participant. This is about taking ownership of your experience and your energy. When you walk toward the world with intention, the world starts walking toward you. You stop waiting, and you start acting—not to force an outcome, but to align yourself with it.

The key is to shift your focus from wanting to giving. Let go of the desperate need for what you think you should receive, and focus on giving the very thing you're hoping to receive. It’s a kind of energetic exchange: If you want respect, start by respecting others. If you want love, show love to yourself first. If you desire compassion or understanding, start by offering that to others. When you give, you set in motion the energy that will come back to you, and it will come back multiplied.

This is how the Universe works: it reflects the energy you put out. If you constantly feel like you’re owed something, that the world “owes” you for your suffering, or that you’re somehow entitled to receive without giving, you're blocking the flow of abundance. The Universe doesn’t respond to demands; it responds to alignment. If you’re vibrating with a sense of lack or desperation, that’s the energy you’ll attract.

But if you start with yourself—treating yourself with respect, kindness, love, and compassion—you change your vibration. You’re no longer sitting in a space of wanting but instead in a space of being. And when you occupy that space, the world reflects that. People will start treating you with the same respect, love, and compassion you’ve been offering to yourself.

So, it’s not about asking the Universe to hand you what you want—it’s about giving what you want, and letting the Universe respond. You create your own reality by giving to the world in the same way you want to receive. It’s all about aligning your inner energy with your desires, and trusting that the Universe will mirror that back to you.

when you walk towards your goal, it walks back to you.

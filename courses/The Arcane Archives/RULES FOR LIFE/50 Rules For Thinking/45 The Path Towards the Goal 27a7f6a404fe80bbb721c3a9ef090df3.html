45. The Path Towards the Goal

The Path Towards the Goal principle in Transurfing is about moving towards your goal with determination and a clear focus, without emotional attachment or excessive worry about how things will unfold. The key idea is to eliminate raised importance, letting go of unnecessary emotional energy, and focusing solely on the pure intention behind your goal. By doing so, you allow the external forces (the Universe) to guide you without resistance.

you see the decision to make to align yourself to that "already achieved" goal, and you make those decisions.

sooner or later, you will end up there. you walk towards it.

Declaration:

The essence is to approach your goal with neutrality and determination—like you’re walking to the newsstand to buy a newspaper. There’s no need for intense emotion or worry about failure. The only obstacles in your way are too much responsibility, a desire to be perfect, and fear of failure. Keep your attention solely on your final goal, not the steps, processes, or scenarios that might unfold in between.

people are often worried about making 10k a month in the next couple months.

don't think of that, you're thinking in a mindset of scarcity.

you don't set a timeframe, you just walk towards it.

that's how you know these "gurus" aren't really living it, you don't set a goal and a timeframe.

you just walk.

Key principles of this approach:

  1. Release emotional attachment: Remove all emotional intensity (raised importance) around your goal. Approach it with calm and clarity, like a simple task, remove time from the goal, you don't tell yourself "I've got 10 minutes to run to the shop)
  1. Pure intention: Focus solely on the intention of reaching your goal, not on the how or the journey. You don’t need to believe that you can achieve it—just keep sliding into the mental image of your goal.
  1. Don’t worry about the process: Avoid including detailed scenarios or stressing over the exact process. Concentrate on the outcome you desire, and trust that the external intention will guide you there.
  1. Eliminate doubt: If you feel doubt or think your goal is too far-fetched, that doubt will block your progress. Stop thinking about belief—just slide into your goal again and again. Success comes from consistent focus and trusting the process.
  1. Stay persistent: Even if you don’t see evidence of progress or results, keep sliding and maintaining the mental image of your goal. Many successful people claim that they never expected to reach their level of achievement, yet they continued to pursue their goals persistently.

Interpretation:

  • Release attachment to the outcome: If you treat your goal as a casual, unimportant task, you will take the pressure off yourself. This helps reduce fear of failure and prevents you from getting bogged down by uncertainty.
  • Stay focused: The only thing you need is focus on the final result. and taking steps towards it, day by day, minute by minute. and breaks are also important, don't think you should be grinding every minute, that raises the importance of the goal. How it happens is not your concern. The Universe will orchestrate events to move you in the right direction, as long as you consistently slide into your goal with pure intention.
  • Persistence over perfection: Don’t strive for perfection in the process. Allow yourself to make mistakes or adjust along the way. The more you try to be perfect, the more you’re investing emotional importance, which can create resistance. don't force yourself to work when you are burnt out. this removes a lot of "weight" from your goal, knowing that you will achieve it sooner or later, you don't have to grind every minute of the day.

Practical steps:

  • Visualize the goal consistently, without adding unnecessary details or worries about the process.
  • Detach emotionally from your goal. Let it be a simple task you are moving towards, with no extra weight.
  • Stay persistent: Even if things don’t seem to line up, keep visualizing and moving in the direction of your goal. steps will lead you there. inevitably.

Summary:

To achieve your goal with ease, remove emotional intensity and approach it with a calm, determined attitude. The path to success lies in pure intention and persistence, not in worrying about how or when things will happen. Trust that the universe will guide you, as long as you focus on the end result and maintain flexibility and small steps throughout the process.

23. Determination to Act

Declaration:

When you desire something, do not waste your time hesitating or overthinking. Approach your goals with the same certainty and determination as if you were going to buy a newspaper—without doubt, without second-guessing. Whether it's a parking spot, a new house, a new car, or anything else you desire, don’t get caught up in wondering "how" or "when." Just act, and take what you want. There will be a waiting period, but do not view this time as "waiting." Instead, understand that during this period, you are moving through different lifelines in the vast time-space continuum. Let go of fear, doubt, and hope—embrace only determination. This unwavering focus will bring you what you desire. Zeland shares how he never wonders whether the bus will come or how long it will take to find parking; he simply knows that his intention will be fulfilled. So, adopt the same mindset—feel the unshakable belief that you are going to receive what you desire without conditions or hesitation.

Interpretation:

Often, when we want something, our mind begins to ask questions: "Will it happen? Should I do more? How is it going to happen?" These doubts and worries only serve to undermine our confidence. Get rid of this habit. The truth is, whatever you want will come to you if you have the determination to act and give enough time for the Universe to work. Wishes are fleeting, dreams are materialized. Pure intention is always fulfilled.

In every action you take, whether small or large, do it with confidence. Don’t second-guess yourself. You may face setbacks along the way, but understand that success is more likely when you act without hesitation. And if you fail? So what? Failure is simply part of the process, and it doesn't diminish your chances of success. The key is the coordination of your intention. When your intention is clear, unwavering, and acted upon with confidence, the Universe will align to make it a reality.

The more certain you are about your desires, the more the Universe will mirror that confidence and bring those desires into your life. So, commit to your actions with full determination. Know.

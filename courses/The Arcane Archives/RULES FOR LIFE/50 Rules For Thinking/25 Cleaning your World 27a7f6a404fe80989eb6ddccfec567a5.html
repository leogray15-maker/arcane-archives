25. Cleaning your World

Declaration:

When you find yourself feeling depressed or down, take a simple yet powerful action: put order in your physical environment. Clean your house, rearrange the furniture, or change the interior. Get rid of old items that carry emotional baggage and replace them with fresh, new things. This shift in your environment will immediately uplift your mood. Just like clearing your space, clear your mind of all negative thoughts. Fear, doubt, hatred, blame, bad anticipation, and criticism—all these negative thought patterns should be discarded. The only feeling that should remain is DETERMINATION to achieve your goals. The one emotion you should choose to follow is pure, unpolarized love.

Interpretation:

The best remedy for depression is creation. In fact, creation is an incredibly broad concept—it’s anything that involves active engagement and production. For instance, cleaning your living space is an act of creation, as it turns a chaotic space into one that is orderly and inviting. Going to the gym is another form of creation, as it builds a healthier body for you to live in. Whether it’s cleaning, organizing, or working on something you’re passionate about, engaging in creation helps release negative energy. This energy, once released, moves you along a more positive lifeline. Instead of being trapped in the negative cycle of depression, your act of creation propels you toward new, more beneficial paths.

As you engage in creation, you begin to reshape your thought patterns. Your reality is a reflection of what you think and feel. When you approach life from a place of love—seeing the world as a nurturing and caring place that supports you—you invite positive energy into your life. The world responds to your feelings, not necessarily your thoughts. If you feel loved by the Universe, then the Universe will mirror that love back to you. However, if you think the world is cold or unkind, then that too becomes your reality.

The truth is, the Universe is a mirror. It reflects back the energy and emotions you put into it. If you believe that success, wealth, and happiness are reserved for a few, then that belief will shape your experience. But if you open yourself up to the possibility that everything you desire is within your reach, the Universe will reflect that possibility right back at you. Your feelings are the driving force behind your external reality—so choose to feel loved, abundant, and supported. Let that feeling guide you through every aspect of your life.

44. The Slide

The Slide principle in Transurfing is about mentally shifting to a life where your goals are already realized. Rather than focusing on the physical actions required to achieve your goals, you focus your attention on creating a vivid mental image of the life you want and living in that image as though it is already true. The idea is that when you fully embody the feeling and experience of your desired outcome, you start attracting it into your physical reality.

once you see yourself inside your goal, you start to act as if you was already there.

you operate from a lens of abundance, not desperation.

Declaration: The slide works when you align your mind with your desired outcome, visualizing it consistently and feeling the emotions of having already achieved it. The key is to not focus on how it will happen, but only on the end result. Trust that the external forces will guide you toward your goal when the timing is right.

you see yourself at the end goal, and you take steps towards it.

it will walk towards you, because you have already attracted it and called it's name.

now all that's left to do is walk towards it, and it will walk towards you.

each time you see yourself in your "goal"

your daily decisions are influenced, and they all align to that life that you "are" living.

each decision is aligned with your goal, as you're basically "already there"

now you just wait for the universe to catch up, as you walk towards it.

Key points about the Slide:

  • Focus on the end result, and take small steps towards it. (alchemy of self)
  • Live in the vision: As you mentally picture your goal being realized, experience it fully with all your senses. Feel the 5 senses that comes with it.
  • Gratitude is essential: As you visualize, express gratitude for the image of your goal, as if it's already in your life.
  • When to slide: Only slide when you're in a positive emotional state. Negative emotions will only distort the vision and reinforce the current negative reality. it is simply a multiplier, if you feel negative and you visualise your goal, you will attach negative emotions, same applies.
  • Balance: While sliding, it's also important to live in the present reality. If you are constantly sliding into a future that doesn't yet exist, it may indicate a resistance to accepting your current reality. This can cause more resistance and negativity to arise in your life. hence why you should do it, 1-2x a day, as said in the alchemy of self.

How the Slide works:

  • Mental shift: When you slide, you move from your current line of life to a new line of life that contains your desired goal. This shift occurs mentally, and when the divine timing is right, external intention will guide you to the experiences and opportunities needed to make that goal a reality.
  • Emotion is key: Your emotions are powerful in this process. Positive emotions reinforce the slide, while negative emotions create more resistance. Therefore, it's important to approach the slide with optimism and happiness, or even passionlessness, living as though you're already experiencing your desired reality.

Practical advice:

  • Be mindful of your emotional state before sliding—only do it when you're feeling good and open.
  • Don’t overdo it: Sliding isn’t something you need to do constantly. Use it as a tool to shift when necessary, but don't try to escape from your present reality all the time.
  • Trust the process: Have faith that when you focus on the end result and live in that vision, the universe will align with it. The transition to your desired life happens naturally over time.

In essence:

The Slide is about using the power of visualization and positive emotion to shift your vibrational frequency to align with your desired outcome. It’s not about forcing action, but rather, allowing the universe to move you toward your goal as you mentally and emotionally align with it. By practicing this technique with gratitude and balance, you invite your goal into your life with ease, as you walk towards it.

you intend to take it, and you do, soon enough.

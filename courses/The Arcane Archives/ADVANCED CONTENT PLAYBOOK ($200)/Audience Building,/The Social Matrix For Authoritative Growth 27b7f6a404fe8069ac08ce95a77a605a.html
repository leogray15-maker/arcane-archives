The Social Matrix For Authoritative Growth.

There are 3 crucial aspects of growing an audience:

  1. Authority
  1. Growth (obviously)
  1. Authenticity

If one of these falls, you become a commodity. We do NOT want this. We want our account to be an asset.

You need a balanced mixture of this content to make it work.

  • 60% growth
  • 20% authenticity
  • 20% authority

If you post 5 times a day (recommended on X - not on other social platforms) you would have:

  • 3 growth X posts
  • 1 authenticity post
  • 1 authority post

This creates leverage. What does this mean?

It means nobody can replace you. You become your own niche. Your expertise + unreplicable experiences + attention = leverage.

You eliminate all competition and gain loyal followers (on X that is).

Growth

There are many things that attribute to growth (authority and authenticity HELP), but you need content to be repostable. How do you make content repostable?

  • Post about topics that are already doing well
  • Turn your personal experiences into relatable insights
  • Simple advice based on your content pillars. Write at a 5th grade level.
  • General health, wealth, and relationship advice.

The easiest way to understand what I'm saying is by understanding the format of viral blog posts. Posts are just mini blog posts.

Headlines = short posts, list posts (9 signs a person is really happy with their life), and you can turn each point into posts.

Repostable content leads to growth and intertwines with authority - the more followers and engagement you get, the more people respect you. Weird, but true.

Growth posts will get the most engagement. Don't get discouraged if the others don't get much engagement.

Authenticity

Authenticity shows people that you are a human. You display this by:

  • Posting about your losses and how you plan to overcome them (don't pity post, tell them what you are doing to be better).
  • Giving advice contrary to what people are used to in your niche.
  • Documentation: show people what you are doing in real life (photos and videos encouraged but optional)
    • What are you working on? What does your lifestyle look like? What did you eat? What does your niche audience do that you also do?
  • Post about your likes and interests.
    • What kind of music do you listen to? How can you display your personality?

Post once a day for authenticity to help nurture the followers you gained with growth.

Authority

Authority posts help prove your expertise - these start warming people up for sales.

You can either post direct concepts from your current products or other actionable information to your target audience.

These should be very specific meaning you should call out who you are targeting, their pain points, and (optional) how to fix them.

  • Example: Entrepreneurs are good at making money, but at a sacrifice to their health. Your quality of work suffers and revenue is limited in growth. Health is wealth.
  • Step-by-step posts to solving this pain point work as well (see: blog post style posts)
  • SOCIAL PROOF: This does not necessarily mean posting a screenshot of a review. This means telling a story of where they were before and what they accomplished working with you.
    • Having a screenshot of a review will make it seem promotional and get less engagement.
    • Telling a story will get a ton of engagement because it inspires people. They will be in the comments congratulating them.
    • Then, all you have to do is plug a link or ask them to DM you.
    • (Remember to mention your specific target audience here.)

Authority posts are what will make you the most sales. Promote once a day - whether it’s a free or paid product/service.

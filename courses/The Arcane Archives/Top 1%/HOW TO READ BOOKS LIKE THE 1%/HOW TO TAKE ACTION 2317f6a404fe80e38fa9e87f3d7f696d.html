HOW TO TAKE ACTION.

you now know that books are about short "aha" moments.

but what do we actually do with those moments?

THIS IS THE PROCESS.

STEP 1) NOTICE YOU JUST GOT THE "AHA" MOMENT.

simple, just notice it.

STEP 2) PUT IT IN "DISSECT LATER"

straight away.

as soon as you got that moment, you need to catch it.

keep hold of it, like a fish tryna get away.

YOU CATCH IT.

PUT IT IN THE NET.

you do this with our famous notion template.

The Queue Notion Template

click that link, click the top right, and duplicate it to your notion if you haven't already.

YOU CAN SEE "DISSECT LATER"

you may also notice that there one called "book notes"

I personally just use that for general book notes, something I've already known.

BUT THESE "AHA" MOMENTS.

they should be :

  • things you can implement
  • things you want to know more about
  • things that will make a change in your life

SHIT THAT CAN CHANGE SOMETHING IN YOUR LIFE.

because that's the main point.

we're learning new things to acquire change.

STEP 3) SET A TIME TO DISSECT IT.

sounds pretty straightforward.

because it is lol.

AFTER YOUR READING SESSION/BLOCK.

set a time to go through your "dissect later"

which we will go over time structuring more in the later modules.

STEP 4) DISSECT IT

BUT HOW DO WE "DISSECT" IT?

we run it through a process.

a process of thoughts.

LOOK AT THE STATEMENT THAT YOU WROTE DOWN.

THINK :

  • WHEN IS THE NEXT SITUATION I CAN USE THIS IN? (practical advice)
  • WHEN IS A TIME I COULD HAVE USED THIS?

for example.

you might be reading a book on social skills.

and a point that said "say their name more, people love hearing their name"

you put that into dissect later.

then later you think "ahhh fuck I could've used this on that one girl yesterday."

and now guess what?

YOU WILL KEEP THAT IN MIND THE NEXT TIME.

next time a similar situation pops up.

your subconscious brain.

it has now integrated that piece of info into itself.

because you invisioned a situation where you could have used it.

now unconsciously, it will look for more situations where you can use that info.

NOW YOU'RE WAY MORE LIKELY TO TAKE ACTION.

YOU ACTUALLY LEARN SOMETHING THIS WAY.

in books, just reading.

you will get that "aha" moment.

but you will never actually think of when you could have used it.

so your subconscious didn't feel the need to integrate it into your brain.

PEOPLE NEVER CONNECT THE DOTS.

so they never learn.

but now.

you have a system to remember everything you read.

THIS IS WHY TEACHING IS THE BEST LEARNING.

like I do.

when you are learning something just so you could teach it later.

everything sticks with you.

because you're thinking about the conscious way that you're going to use this info.

your conscious brain tells the subconscious "remember this for later"

BUT WHEN YOU READ NORMALLY

it doesn't do that.

do this, and your subconscious will remind you.

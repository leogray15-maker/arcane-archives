Work Until You've Finished "X"

So... here’s a quick one I learnt from Alex hormozi and other top achievers.

None of them set out a specific “work timer” for their work block.

What they do is say “I will work until I’ve finished x”

So for example…

When you sit down to work… don’t put a 90 minute timer…

Just tell yourself that you will work until you’ve finished “x”

For me it will be something like “work until you’ve finished an email, telegram message, tiktok post, a course module and a tweet”

then my second work block of the day can be something like "work until course is finished"

since imagine this...

I'm working on a course... I finished a module and can think to myself "how much more do I actually do?" since I didn't give myself a proper goal to work towards.

but when I've just outlined a clear thing I want to achieve... I will just go forward without even thinking.

Then after you’ve done this task and completed it.. you can actually rest and shut off.

because you had a clear goal… and you’ve accomplished it.

Now for your NEXT work block, you can set yourself another task to complete.

This is just a little tool that will give you some clarity when you’re actually working.

Since when you “Eat the frog” and then you start working… you don’t even know what you’re actually working towards IN that work block.

So you should instead set a goal inside that work block for something you’re going to achieve.. and then you can move on with your day.

This will increase productivity because you actually know what needs to be done… and it will make you more efficient since working with an end goal always brings more speed.

AND it will give you some peace of mind when you’ve finished that activity.

Keep it in mind, just a little mental model from what I’ve learnt studying high performers

The Outreach Formula, Consistently Land Clients.

We've talked about the longer / more time consuming method of outreach in other articles.

But there comes a time when you can be more strategic (and less time consuming).

Here is how you craft the perfect outreach message. Whether it's DMs, cold email, or any other medium.

The Hook

Dive into your prospect's content and find something that they would be proud of.

  • A deep post that didn't get much engagement
  • An insight from a podcast episode
  • A specific part of a blog post
  • Any other post on social media that breaks their current content strategy (they may be testing something new and could use a compliment)

Then, let them know what you enjoyed about it or how it resonated with you.

Take a look at the first line from the screenshot above and make it better through deeper research.

Example: I wrote a blog post about focus a few months back. You could read that article and comment on one of the most complex parts and why you liked it (because I like to feel smart, and not many people saw it).

3 Different Options For Authority

Social Proof And Name DroppingEXACTLY how Shreya did in the screenshot above. Mention someone you have worked with (that the other prospect may know) and tell them the results you got them.

These results will make the other prospect want the same results.

Actionable Tips

Simple, give 2-3 ACTIONABLE tips that can help them immediately.

This mainly works for freelancing services because you can SEE what they are currently doing. You can look through their landing page, copy, emails, or whatever else you specialize in.

This shouldn't be used for coaching offers UNTIL you know what they are struggling with.

Free Work

There are two ways to send free "work."

  1. Offering to audit their current state
  1. Offering to send a "sample" of your work that they can use

For the audit: you are offering to give them 2-3 actionable tips on something that they've already done in video form. You can use Loom to record your screen and voice while doing this.

For the sample: Do what you are best at and send it to them for free (i.e. tweets, landing page, social posts, emails, copy, etc.)

THE KEY WITH THIS: only offer to send the free work. Don't send it at first. Simply ask, "Can I send you an audit/work I made for you? You can use it or trash it." Once they say "yes," THEN do the work and send them a link.

The Call To Action

The call to action needs to be plain and simple.

"Do you have time for a call this week to see if we can get you the same results?"

Or, "Do you have time for a call this week?"

Or however fits your natural voice.

The Vessel

There are 3 ways to send these messages. I encourage you to try them all.

If they don't respond via one channel, try another. Email them or DM them on Instagram/LinkedIn if they don't respond on X.

The 3 Vessels:

  1. Video DM - write out your outreach message from above and record a video of yourself to send to them.
  1. Regular DM - just send the DM and try different platforms.
  1. Cold email - send your outreach message in the form of an email.

P.S. You can still follow the script in the previous module - and I encourage AT LEAST prequalifying a bit and having them tell you where they want to take their project (in terms of money and lifestyle).

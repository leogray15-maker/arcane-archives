How to keep your prospects eyes glued to your copy

Next to my desk are things I consider to be very important to my success.

The most important item attached to the side of my desk is a sheet of paper filled with transition sentences.

A transition sentence are little sentences, phrases and words that help "connect" paragraphs and ideas together

And what they do is guide your readers to wherever you want them to go by making it easier for their brains to follow along.

Sometimes they take your reader from one idea to another. Sometimes they guide your reader from one thought to a related thought. Sometimes they simply ask a question.

There are hundreds (probably thousands) of transition sentences you can use.

But there are only about 60 or 70 I use regularly.

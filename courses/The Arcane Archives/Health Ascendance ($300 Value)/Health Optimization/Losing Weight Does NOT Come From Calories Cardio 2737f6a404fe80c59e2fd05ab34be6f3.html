Losing Weight Does NOT Come From Calories/Cardio.

Why you struggle to lose weight?

And its not because your eating too much or not doing enough cardio its because :

  • Impaired thyroid function
  • Consumption of polyunsaturated fats (think seed oils)
  • Vitamin and mineral deficiencies
  • Excess estrogen
  • Inadequate light environment (lack of sun exposure, excess blue light)
  • Chronic stress
  • Digestive issues, excess serotonin
  • Inflammation
  • Environmental stressors toxins

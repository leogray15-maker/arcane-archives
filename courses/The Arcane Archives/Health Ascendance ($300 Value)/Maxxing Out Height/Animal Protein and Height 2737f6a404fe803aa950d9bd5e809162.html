Animal Protein and Height.

the biggest influence on height is animal protein intake.

one of the biggest animal protein consumers in the whole world is Argentine if you visit the place you'll be shocked by the sheer amount of meat they consume.

another thing you'll see is that even tho Argentinians consume a load of animal protein, they are still short.

this is because of the Calcium: Phosphate balance in the diet in Argentine is fucked.

something they got right in Netherlands where animal protein intake and Calcium : Phosphate ratio are top notch creating an incredibly tall population.

when the Calcium:Phosphate ratio is low (much more Phosphate than Calicum) deterioration of bone will occur, which messes with your height growth.

moral of the story, eat more animal protein.

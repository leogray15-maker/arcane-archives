The Simple 3 Point Online Business Model

Business is one of the most simple things out there, but most of us never touched a business book during our childhood.

We think that building a business is reserved for people with startup capital, a top 1% skillset, and/or a huge following + paid ads.

Let’s take a step back and see what you really need to hit the $5K, $10K, and even $50K per month mark.

The 3 point online business model is as follows:

  1. Social media account
  1. Email List
  1. Offer

They all interconnect, so let me explain it to you so you see the bigger picture

Point 1) Social Media Account

Your social media account is your way to get potential customers.

Your content displays your writing, videos, whatever you like to create.

And with paid ads becoming less viable and worse...

your social accounts are a free organic traffic source that you can send to your offers (what you are selling).

Most people see social media growth as daunting.

It is.

But there are proven methods for growth and monetization. It would be silly to start growing an account if you have no idea what you are doing… that’s why it’s so daunting in the first place.

Side note: you don’t need a lot of followers to make 6 figures+. Yes, they help, A LOT, but you don’t need them. You can grow to build leverage while pulling in a decent income.

as i said before... $50x200 Per Month is literally 10K Per Month.

All of this is like learning to play a game (because that’s what it is).

At first, a game is boring because you don’t know how to play.

But you play anyway, learn the rules, the fundamentals, how to win, and eventually it becomes fun.

The same goes for social media. The rules are the platforms terms of service. There are ways to play the game.

Your skills of writing/speaking reflect in your content.

In the case of Twitter, the mechanics lie in your ability to write easy to read content that give value.

this is all you need...

You Craft A Followable Profile

Email List

The unfortunate truth is that you do not own your social media account, the higher ups do.

It also may not be the best place to sell ALL THE TIME, because it will kind of just ruin trust...

that's where this email list comes in.

this is where people will be sent emails from you where you sell your "offer" and print money.

and also..

It will come in handy just in case you get canceled. You own your list, no one can take it away.

When you have your offer (product or service ready) you can create email sequences to sell them on autopilot. Then, all you have to do is post content to your social account, plug your email list, and make sales without doing any of the work.

this is how it's going to work...

you will gather an audience from social media...

send it to your email list to sign up.

now you've got their email, here's what you will do next..

Start with a welcome sequence. Have a welcome email that tells everyone what you talk about, your vision for your brand, and what they can expect in future emails.

With a social media account and email list alone, you can pull in a hefty income over time, we're talking up to maybe 50K/Month if you have the right way of doing it.

This is an ultra-lean one-person business model. 3-4 hours a day (possibly less) of work — IF you have a solid offer / offer stack.

which we will go through now..

but let me recap how this will work.

  • Social Media Account To Find An Audience That Share The Same Interests.
  • Email List Which They Will Get Emails From You In.

which all leads to the next thing...

Your Offer

this is what you sell... which in this case is a skool community which I taught you about how we're going to do that.

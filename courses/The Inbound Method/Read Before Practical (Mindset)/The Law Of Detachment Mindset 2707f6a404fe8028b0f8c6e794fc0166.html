The Law Of Detachment Mindset.

Here’s the mindset you NEED and is the SOLE mindset that crafts a winner.

Imagine this...

You will keep on posting these HIGH QUALITY pieces of content that solves problems... OR just gives your viewers a "wow, that's something new"

You will get no results for a long while...

But Think about this…

You’ve been uploading high quality guides to problems on your tiktok/youtube for literally months and no views…

Then one video pops off to 1 million views and everyone just literally starts going back to your old content and think “how tf doesn’t this guy have more followers? He drops gems”

Then you're literally nurturing your audience already just by your old content.

This is literally why everyone is an "overnight success", it all comes down to bursting the pipe.

You have to be the underdog, you have the be the guy who was grinding in the dark… until you burst the pipe.

Just proceed with the mindset of this…

Imagine you posted 1000 high quality tiktoks/youtube videos/whatever like this...

it is virtually impossible for you to not blow up and get rich… literally it is input output.

See it as “planting seeds” for your future audience, they can get to know you from your one post that "burst the pipe" then go back through all of your old posts with minimal views... and that way you're "nurturing" them at the same time...

You could even just send emails daily STILL when you don't even have anyone in your email list.

The posts will be there inside your substack for your new audience to go back to.

you could even teach your audience this mindset...

You kept on grinding because you knew it was virtually impossible to fail if you uploaded 1000 tiktoks.

You was literally sending emails to NO ONE just to build the habit and knowing that you will have a future audience that will go back to this.

The key to this is forgetting about outside results and just focusing on the work you’re putting in.

Success is hard because you don’t see results for a while when you start.

So you just need to see it as if you’re doing this for yourself.

This is why I’ve made the inbound method as “solving your own problems then giving the solution to your audience"

It’s kind of the law of detachment, you are doing it for yourself so it doesn’t matter if you get views or not… but they will come eventually and you will get money off that.

This game is just a game of not quitting, and you will succeed, so here’s the mindset you need.

YOU NEED TO SEE THIS AS PLANTING SEEDS.

THERE IS NO CHANCE THAT YOU DO NOT SUCCEED IF YOU POSTED 1000 HIGH QUALITY TIKTOKS/YOUTUBE VIDEOS/WHATEVER IT IS.

Just think about that.

This is the same thing with other businesses too..

Imagine you made 1000 shopify stores, one is due to be a winning store.

Imagine you made 1000 sales calls, one is due to convert for a 2K/Month retainer.

It’s all just a number game.

remember that.

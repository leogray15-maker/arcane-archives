The Philosophy Of Posting.

So... posting content.

Posting content is to build up an audience that loves your content, relates to it, and gets good lessons from it that they can apply to their life (gives them a benefit)

The main point here is that it gives them a benefit…

That’s all you need to really focus on, and you will grow.

You will give them free content that gives them a benefit… then you will direct them to your email list which contains more free content that gives them MORE benefits…

Then you direct them to your offer/product in your emails which give them the ultimate benefit. (With a price tag)

Really… it’s easy.

The specific "method" or "style" of posting does NOT matter, as long as they get a benefit from your content.

So, this is the first stage of the ladder.

it’s getting the audience that loves you.

In an upcoming section in this course I will go through different ways to write to gain an audience.

You will choose the one that you really resonate with and that will be the most fun for you.

OR you can obviously come up with a different content style (because there are infinite)

And if you do find a different way to gain an audience writing… you can let me know and I’ll put it in this course.

BUT HERE’S THE IMPORTANT PART.

You shouldn’t switch too much.

You should try out different formats, but you should find one that you will stick with.

Since the one you stick with the most… will be the one you are best at.

So, here’s why I made this module.

It’s to tell you how this is gonna work…

Let me summarise it for you.

SUMMARY.

HOW AUDIENCE BUILDING WORKS.

  • If They See A Benefit In Your Content, They Will Click Follow To Get More Benefits In Their Life Through Your Content.
  • Then You Can Tell Them “Subscribe To The Email List For Even MORE Benefits”
  • Then This Will Lead Onto Selling Them Into Your Offer Which Has The Ultimate Benefits. (For a price)

WHICH CONTENT STYLE SHOULD YOU CHOOSE?

  • Test Out Many, But Stick To One.

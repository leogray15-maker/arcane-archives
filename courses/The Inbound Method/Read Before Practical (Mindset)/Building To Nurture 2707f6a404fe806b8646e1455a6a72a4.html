Building To Nurture.

So... just a quick note.

This part of the course and your journey is about “building” an audience.

Which is pretty simple…

It’s just :

  • Getting Followers. (Basically)

But later on comes “nurturing”

Here’s what nurturing is.

The Art Of Nurturing.

So... once you have got them as a follower (which we will do in this section)

You will get them in your email list, and you will “nurture” them.

So now… what Is nurturing?

Nurturing is just helping someone develop, that’s it.

So... having them in your email list means that you have ONE GOAL.

It’s getting them to read your emails constantly and having them take action on it.

That way they will :

  • take action on your words
  • See a benefit to your words
  • Think “this guy actually gives good advice”
  • Repeat

Now after a while of this nurturing…

They will start to trust you as an authority figure, you have improved their lives.

Now when you tell them about your skool community which is the ULTIMATE product, they will buy inevitably.

But without that nurturing? Some may buy, but a majority won’t.

So right now I need you to understand the vision.

You are getting followers so later on you can “nurture them” in your email list.

And then those “nurtured” followers will become buyers.

That’s how it works.

So... keep that in mind.

We are building to nurture later down the road.

And this will become automated soon.

You will keep on posting content to social media for new followers, and you will also be sending emails at the same time for your already existing followers.

It will just become an ecosystem and an endless loop later on…

The money printing mechanism… it’s beautiful.

But anyway, I hope you understand, just needed to tell you this so I know you get it.

just remember... you're just trying to get them in your email list so you can "nurture" them.

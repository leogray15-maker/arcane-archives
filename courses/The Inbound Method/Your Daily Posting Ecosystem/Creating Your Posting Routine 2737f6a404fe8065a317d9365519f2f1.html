Creating Your Posting Routine.

So.. now we’ve covered EVERYTHING, and you’ve got one more thing left to do.

That’s to create your daily routine.

Lucky for you… I’ve already made a full course on routines, things of that nature.

Productive isolation, you need to read it.

If you’ve read it already, reread it again.

Put it into action.

After you've done that, you'll see that your day has 3 segments.

  • eat the frog
  • do the work thats needed
  • rest

in your "Eat the frog" section this is where you will do the following :

  • see what topic interests you today (can be anything)
  • pick out a resource (you know how)
  • listen, read, until you hit a lightbulb moment that you think you could make a post on.
  • make a post, teach your reader.
  • make that into an email. send it out at a certain time EVEN if you don't have any subscribers, get into the habit.
  • go back to the topic you want to learn. set out a timeblock for 1-2 hours.
  • get your community/course open. teach(if you're a beginner a notion document, a notes app, whatever.)
  • learn, lightbulb moment, teach, repeat, don't lose the notes tab you teach in.

that's your "eat the frog" done, that's your "day of work" done.

after 365 days of doing this set routine

the routine of :

learning, and posting

making an email. (promoting at the end when you have a "book of secrets" ready)

ADDING to your book of secrets for 1-2 hours.

that's it, that's your work day.

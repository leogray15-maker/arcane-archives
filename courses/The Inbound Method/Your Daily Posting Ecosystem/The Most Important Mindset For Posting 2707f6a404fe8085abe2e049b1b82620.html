The Most Important Mindset For Posting.

Now, there are not too much different formats for posting…

But there are a couple.

There are 2 main ones that solve a big problem (and are longer to write)

And there is one main one that is just a quick post that can pop off

But let me give you a quick mentality on why the “longer” posts are better, even though they will get less views.

Listen to this…

Of course when you write longer posts, you will get less views.

They can still pop off, don’t forget that… but as a rule of thumb they will get less views.

But this doesn’t matter.

Why?

Because it’s not about the view/follower count at the end of the day…

It’s about the people that will actually buy from you.

You can have 200 people that read your longform content and actually get their life changed because of it, resulting them to buy.

Or you can have 2 million people that saw one of your short posts that is just some “motivation”, they will not buy.

OR you can have 2 million people that saw your longform content and a lot of them buy… now you’re financially free.

So... honestly the “long form” is kind of the new meta, the thing you should be focusing on.

And you should NOT care about views, you should NOT optimise for views…

However, you should be optimising for money instead.

Your views don’t matter, just remember that.

Do not chase the dragon of “views”, as they will not get you rich.

There is this concept of “1000 true fans” which is absolutely amazing And has changed my life… I read this in the book expert secrets by Russel Brunson.

In short… what this “1000 true fans” concept is… I’ll tell you now.

1000 True Fans.

Basically, imagine you had 1000 people buying every product you release…

If you released a skool community for $50 a month… and 1000 people bought it… that’s 50k per month.

In theory, you could make 50k a month with 1000 followers.

That is why there is NO point optimising for followers or likes or views.

You are optimising to change someone’s life from your content…

And that comes from Longform posts, not short motivation posts.

So... how do you actually acquire one of these “true fans”?

Basically, just keep on posting content that will resonates with the audience.

in our way, we just teach the things we're interested in and that we've learnt that day... we just teach as we learn.

and weirdly... you will start to find that you're interested in a couple main topics.

for example. mine.

I like psychology, nature of things, sales, business, etc.

and there will be CERTAIN GUYS that like the exact same thing.

my account is "perfect" for them. they become a true fan.

And after that… he’s read every single one of your posts, every single one of your emails.

He loves you because you actually changed his life with the content you put out... but it wasn't even your "ideas" which is the funny part.

He will just see that every single day…

He thinks “if his free content changed me… imagine what’s in here” little does he know the book of secrets is in there.

And he will join your skool sooner or later because he just sees your emails reminding him every day… and will NEVER leave, as long as you keep giving value.

Now... you just need 1000 of those people and you’re on 50-100k a month.

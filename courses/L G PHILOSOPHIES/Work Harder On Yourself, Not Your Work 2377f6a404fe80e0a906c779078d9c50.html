Work Harder On Yourself, Not Your Work.

I don't think I've ever gave real "business" advice.

i will often get comments that ask me where the money making advice lies.

but the reality is that success is 95% mindset and 5% work.

there is a quote that sums this up perfectly.

“Work hard at your job and you can make a living.

Work hard on yourself and you can make a fortune.”

― Jim Rohn

ever since I heard this, it stuck with me.

since you've probably heard that "99% of businesses fail"

but have you ever questioned why they fail?

it's because people focus on working, instead of developing.

there is another quote that i'd like to add.

"I was educated once. It took me years to get over it."

  • Mark Twain.

this quotes resonates deeply.

everybody may have their interpretation of it.

but here's what it means.

the most educated man on the planet knows that he is not educated.

nobody can be "educated" unless they believe they are not "educated"

since true intelligence comes from knowing that you can never be educated.

there is infinite knowledge that no man could ever comprehend, as no living being that is mortal can ever be omniscient.

when you make this realisation, it's like a whole world opens up.

you see, dopamine is seen as a bad thing inherently because it mostly links to bad antics in the modern space.

but why was it created?

dopamine is the drive to learn.

whenever you learn something new, your mind lights up.

you feel a hit of bliss.

dopamine drives you to learn more.

just like it drives you to scroll more.

it is a neurotransmitter that can make you into a great man.

but it can also destroy you into a deep depression.

i believe dopamine was created by god with the intent for us to get it from learning.

as there is infinite knowledge in this world, and we get dopamine spikes which are based off novelty.

everything else in this world is finite, but knowledge...

knowledge is infinite.

and it is the only thing that is infinite.

which leads me back to this point.

man was made to explore, and man was made to learn as much as possible before death.

even if we know that we will never know everything.

it is a nice journey to have the mission to know everything.

it is a mission that ascends yourself.

this is how you become a man of wisdom.

and wisdom is what breeds success.

but let's question why does wisdom breed success?

firstly, what is entrepreneurship? answer.

entrepreneurship is simply solving problems.

and a man full of wisdom is simply a man who knows the answers to the problems of the 99%.

"Business is all about solving people's problems - at a profit."

  • Zig Ziglar

which comes back to a singular quote.

"success is 95% mindset, and 5% work"

work harder on yourself than you do on your job.

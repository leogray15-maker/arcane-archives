When Should You Wake Up?

don't hit the snooze, here's why...

i always get asked "what time do you wake up?"

the answer is always going to be a time when nobody else is awake.

most people will do this lifestyle of "digital nomads" and "wifi money" so they can do whatever they want.

they can wake up at 1pm... write a couple tweets and be free making 10k/Month magically...

well, that's not the case.

but let's be real, you're probably past that stage now, you know that this life is a grind.

so, let me tell you my philosophy on times you should arise.

here are the 3 non negotiables for me.

  • wake up when everyone is sleeping.
  • eat the frog.
  • wake up when it's dark (optional, but not optional for me)

and the main one here is "eat the frog"

you see, here's a philosophy.

imagine you had to eat one living frog in your day.

what time would you eat it?

the smart people would have said first thing in the morning, since you can wash it down and get it over with.

the retards will leave it to the end of their day.

after school, after the 9-5.

then they'll spend their whole day dreading that frog.

eating breakfast, almost gagging at the thought of having to eat that frog later.

whereas, the smart people have got it over and done with first thing in the morning.

and now they walk through the day happy... and proud.

they know they've done the hardest task that they had to do in their day already.

and this applies to your work.

it's not about "when should I wake up"

it's "when should I work?"

and my answer to that is?

before you brush your teeth, get it done.

wake up before anybody else.

don't hit snooze, even if you think your muscle hypertrophy is gonna suffer.

it's pretty hard to argue against a smart mind.

it'll come up with excuses like "I'm not maximising muscle growth when I don't sleep until rested"

but after enough times, you start to question it.

and when you find an answer to that question, wisdom is bred.

what's more important?

0.2 extra inches on your bicep or enough money to retire your wife, mother and sister?

that's the thought that I'm passing down to you.

so, here's a little summary.

wake up before everybody else (reduce all friction)

work immediately after your eyes open.

don't brush your teeth, don't press the power button on your phone.

sit on your crusty kitchen chair, and work.

there is no other way.

after all, why would you brush your teeth before eating a frog?

it's common sense.

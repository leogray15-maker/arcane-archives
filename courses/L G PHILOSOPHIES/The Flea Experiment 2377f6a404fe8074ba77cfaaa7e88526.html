The Flea Experiment.

“We are already rich, just short of money”

you’ve probably heard of this experiment before.

this life is just a constant game of topping yourself up.

not one upping yourself, topping yourself up.

to win, all you have to do is act like he who has already won for a long enough amount of time.

visualise this.

you put some fleas in a jar, and you put a top lid on it.

the fleas will keep on jumping, and they will not stop jumping because their number one desire is to get out.

but they are limited by the top lid of the jar.

they keep on trying to jump out for days…

but they can’t they’re just limited by the top.

but something weird happens…

the experimenter removes the lid, and they’re still jumping up to the same height where the lid was.

they have freedom right in front of them, but they wont jump out.

they’ve been programmed…

so, how does this link into what I was talking about?

it all comes back to the point that you can never rise above you identity

you will never fall below your standards, and you will do just enough to get by your standards.

so you have to make success a standard.

this is human nature, the law of deadlines.

so, the common sense thing to do is make success your standard.

but how do we actually do that?

you have to believe that you are the person who has already achieved the goals.

and then your “standard” will be the goals you are on the path to achieving.

and because you see yourself as the person who has already achieved, your character will align your actions towards the goal that has already been achieved by your 6th sense.

the 5 senses are touch, smell, hearing etc.

but the 6th sense is the mind, the infinite intelligence.

the place that we call “imagination”…

and that crazy thing about this “imagination”?

it’s that we cannot tell imagination from reality.

the placebo effect is real.

why do you think you can get aroused by watching pixels on a screen?

the whole point of arousal happens because your brain perceives it as a chance to complete your lifes mission, to have children.

but obviously, you’re not having kids with this onlyfans girl on the screen, you’re just watching her with all of the other guys in the comments like a monkey.

it’s not real, but it still happens.

so, we need to use this to our advantage…

we need to do 2 things.

  1. think about our goals as if we have ALREADY achieved them.
  1. when you think about said goals, you need to remove any doubt associated with them.

I got this gem of a tactic from a 70K/Month copywriter, so you know it works.

let me leak the secrets of the top 1% to you.

firstly. identify clearly what you want.

sit down, and think.

what is your purpose right now?

money, women, status?

probably money, so you need to set a money goal.

firstly, you need to identify how much money.

not “i will be rich”

but “i will reach $10,000 a Month”

specific.

now next, you get a card and you write that onto it.

“I will reach $10,000 a month”

and you look at that card often.

but here’s the thing.

when you look at that card, your mind will come up with reasons why you can’t do it.

you have the ability to reject those thoughts and beliefs with your conscious mind, kick it out.

I will talk about how to do this in a second.

but you need to understand that when the emotion of doubt wakes up, you cannot tap into the goal, it is impossible.

you will do your craft with the lens of “doubt”

whether it be trading, writing, whatever.

you will do your work through the lens of doubt.

so how do you reverse this?

every time you look at that card often and your brain starts to think why it isn’t possible, start thinking endlessly.

think until you start to believe that you can.

FORCE your mind to think positive thoughts about the goal.

why? because positivity is abundance, when you are angry or doubtful you are letting thoughts of scarcity enter your mind, since scarcity is what brings the negative emotions.

so when you FORCE your mind to think positive thoughts, you force it into a state of thinking in abundance.

and wealth by definition means abundance, abundance by definition means to have more than you need.

“my cup runneth over”

once you have beliefs of abundance, you are unstoppable.

he who has become he who has achieved, has achieved.

take action, write the card.

carry it everywhere.

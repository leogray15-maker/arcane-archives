so, where are you?

or in other words... why have you not became the person who you've always wanted to be?

only be friends with people who bring a net positive to your life.

this is a harsh lesson that you will learn sooner or later.

but this is how an entrepreneur thinks, and I'm learning these things as I go.

if it's not about money or progress, do not reach out.

value is exchanged for value.

imagine you are friends with somebody because they are funny.

you're just being friends with them because they bring humour to your life.

why would I associate with someone that does not have the single goal to make money?

since my only thing that is on my mind is money and progress.

it's just the truth.

if i'd go out with someone that isn't as money focused as I am, I'd just think "why am I even here"

being lonely in a room full of people.

what is the point of hanging out with people that don't even align with your thought frequency?

you want to talk about philosophy, money, progress.

while next man will be talking about some next up up celebrity doing something.

tell me, what is the point?

why does anyone focus on others people lives more than their own?

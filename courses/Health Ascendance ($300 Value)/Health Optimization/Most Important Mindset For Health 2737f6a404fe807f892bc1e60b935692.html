Most Important Mindset For Health.

Enjoy life

Anorexic mainstream fitness demonizes anything that has to do with fun.

Late nights, some red wine, cigars.

Which couldn't be more backwards.

The point of health is to actually enjoy these things, be resilient enough that they don't result in a negative.

Doesn't mean to binge every week. But you'll get to point where you can indulge and just go on normal the next day. Or even include it and don't hinder any progress.

Which is truly superhuman status.

Sipping red wine and smoking cigar late at night while chilling is much healthier than gymcel preworkout life which i used to do and i heavily regret it.

the body is meant to go through hardship.

When you eat nutrient dense and raw foods, you won't have any negative impact from these things, i actually started enjoying life when i changed my diet.

you can be drinking red wine at night and the next day be stronger at gym.

remember...

Enjoy life, don't follow man made routines.

work hard, play hard.

THE DAILY TESTOSTERONE DIET.

Breakfast.

  • 200 grams of ground beef fried with butter in a stainless steel pan.
  • 4 “pasture-raised” or "free range" eggs, boiled in water or fried with butter.
  • 3 dates.
  • top everything with Himalayan sea salt or Celtic sea salt.

Lunch

  • 1 ribeye steak 250 grams fried in butter with Himalayan/Celtic sea salt.

(if too expensive, you can use 80/20 or 75/25 ground beef instead.)

  • 50 grams of cow liver

(even 25 grams of liver contains a lot of nutritional values, hence 50 grams, liver is also very cheap)

  • 300-400 millilitres of raw milk/raw kefir (if you do not have access to raw milk/kefir, you can drink normal kefir, but do not drink normal milk like the blue cap/red cap etc)
  • 1 mango
  • top everything with 50-100g of raw cheese.

    raw cheese can be probably found at your local store, for example in aldi they have "parmigiano reggiano" which is made from raw cows milk.

  • 1 banana with 1 spoon of raw honey on it (you can buy raw honey online for surprisingly cheap, I got 1kg for under £20)

Evening meal

  • 250 grams of minced beef or 250 grams of ribeye steak fried in butter with Himalayan/Celtic sea salt
  • 3 eggs with self-made tomato sauce and onions (if you prefer)
  • 1 avocado
  • 2 oranges

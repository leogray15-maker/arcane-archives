A Breakdown Of A X Ghostwriting Agency Offer.

A Breakdown Of Dan Koes Previous X Ghostwriting Agency Offer

For those that don't know (and I don't expect many to know), I created an X management / growth agency a while back. It ended up falling apart because my previous partner and I had different interests, we decided to just abandon the idea altogether after 4 months.

But, we landed around 6 clients in our first month.

The Offer

Here were the components of our offer:

  • 4 posts a day
  • 1 thread a week
  • $750 monthly budget for paid reposts (guaranteed growth)
  • DM management
  • Engage with people in the replies

Our guarantee : we make them their money back or we refund them what the difference (if they made $2500, we refund $1000)

Our target market : personal brands with top selling courses (makes it easy to make their money back)

Our USP : My partner and I are both great writers, the people we reached out to can see that. We have "celebrity" credibility.

How We Delivered

First, we understood how to grow X accounts and make sales because we had already done it ourselves.

(Remember that learning how to deliver your service is extremely important. This shouldn't have to be said but it seems like too many people want everything give to them, like in this post, and just expect it to work.)

In reality, people have already done this work for you. It’s called an X course. You can take an X course, systemize it, and turn it into the backbone of your account management offer. This is also how you learn how to grow on X.

Other skills that are required:

  • Understanding of personal brands sales process
  • Light understanding of copywriting for promotional content
  • Proficiency in writing, specifically short form content (X posts) and blog style content (threads)

How we came up with content ideas:

  • We got access to their courses and turned it into authority content
  • We studied competitors in their niche on Instagram and YouTube and used it as content inspiration
  • We had the client fill out an extensive questionnaire asking for their content pillars, topics, tone of voice, likes, dislikes, accomplishments, etc
  • We created a swipe file from their top tweets in analytics and reworded a lot of them

We would send them a week’s worth of posts to refine and approve. This would help us understand their voice better over time. We would do the same with threads.

We would schedule the posts in Hypefury and plug their course once a day under a post that we knew would do well and was relevant to their course.

How We Landed Clients

We closed 80% of the people we reached out to because our offer was very specific, had a great guarantee, and we targeted people that we knew had money (top selling course creators).

You can find these by searching for the best sellers on Gumroad or finding coaches / creators that you vibe with on Instagram or YouTube and offer to increase their course sales and build their authority on X.

We followed this outreach method to a T and used a personal video. We recorded ourselves going over the outreach message and talked like normal human beings.

All clients except one closed without a sales call because our offer was that good.

How Can You Do This?

  • Study as much X content / courses as possible (like in other posts in this community)
  • Practice by growing an account of your own, preferably in the niche / interest area that you would enjoy writing about and has profitable creators in
  • Systemize your process for content creation, sales, engagement, and replies
  • Write content for others or get on a call with them to give them strategies to get more out of their account
  • If you aren't extremely confident in your ability, start out at a low cost and increase it as you get results / testimonials
  • Create smaller offers (like $500 for 20 tweets and a thread)

One last thing to note:

Stop trying to make money extremely quickly. All it will do is burn you out. Take your time, have fun with the process, and GET GOOD at what you do.

The financial advice you need to know about to be better than 99% of people:

  • Avoid lifestyle creep
  • Society will convince you that you need to possess certain things to be cool.
  • Do not fall into the trap of overconsumption.
  • All these big companies spend billions to study you so that you buy their products.
  • Live simply and build your future.
  • Do not chase social status
  • Many people confuse wealth with social status.
  • Wealth is
  • – Freedom
  • – Choices
  • – Options
  • – Assets
  • It is not
  • – Cars
  • – Big houses
  • – Luxurious lifestyle
  • – Material possessions
  • Marry wisely
  • Never rush into marriage if you're not ready.
  • This is the greatest financial decision that you will ever make.
  • Vet your future spouse for as long as you can.
  • Make sure that your choice is a good one, or else it will be costly for you.
  • Avoid credit card debt
  • It is a trap designed to keep you dependent and a predatory tactic that takes your money.
  • Avoid it at all costs. And if you have any, pay them off as soon as possible.
  • Debt impedes your investments and savings goals.
  • Set goals
  • Don't let life pass you by without having objectives and a plan for the future.
  • Set both short-term and long-term goals.
  • There is no way to know if you are making progress unless you have goals.
  • Create an emergency fund
  • Too many people resort to debt when they can't afford an unexpected accident or expense.
  • Set aside 3 to 6 months worth of expenses.
  • Be prepared, since life throws us curve balls when we least expect them.
  • Play the long game
  • Everything with a solid foundation lasts a long time.
  • Wealth creation is not a get-rich-quick scheme. It takes time, sacrifice, and a lot of patience.
  • Maintain your strategy and avoid being swayed by market movements.
  • Surround yourself with great people
  • Do not be with people who are about partying and the night life.
  • Be with ambitious people who force you to be the best version of yourself.
  • Success, just like failure, is very contagious.
  • Never stop learning
  • Never hesitate to invest in yourself.
  • Invest in:
  • Yourself
  • Your financial security
  • Your health
  • Your relationships
  • The most valuable resource on earth is you. Before making any other investments, first invest in yourself.

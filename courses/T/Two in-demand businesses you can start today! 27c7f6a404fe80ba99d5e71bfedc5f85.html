Two in-demand businesses you can start today!

Two in-demand businesses you can start today with just a laptop that nobody talks about

These business models require:

  • No capital
  • No degree
  • No tech knowledge

Literally, the only thing you need to start any of the businesses below is a laptop and a work ethic.

TikTok UGC Creation

This one is huge and very hot at the moment.

”UGC” means user-generated content.

And you know it as TikTok videos. Literally.

UGC agencies charge around £5,000 for a set of 8-12 UGC videos.

You literally only need ONE client to be able to quit your job.

How it works:

  • A brand needs content for their marketing so they send you their products
  • You create the content and edit it
  • The brand then gets your permission to use the content (ownership rights)
  • You get paid

And if you’re good at doing UGC, you can scale it into a sizeable business by hiring other creators and building a real company.

Onlyfans Management Agency

This may be a bit controversial for some people but there’s big money to be made.

I’m not recommending this to everyone, do your due diligence before picking any business model.

The reason this is a hot business opportunity right now is that there’s never been more Onlyfans models ever.

These models need someone to help them with growing their account and monetising the audience.

In fact, 90% of the top 10% of Onlyfans models have management teams/agencies behind them.

How it works:

  • A model with good content but low engagement signs up with you
  • You handle their promotion on Twitter, Instagram,…
  • The model makes more money as a result and pays you a % of the profits

I personally know a few Onlyfans agency owners from Twitter and some of them make over £100K/month.

Yes, you read that correctly.

Over £1,000,000 a year. A million Pounds.

How to build confidence:

1) Start with small accomplishments:  Set yourself up for success by starting with goals that you know you can achieve. As you check things off of your list, your confidence will start to grow.

2) Make a list of your positive qualities:  Sometimes it can be easy to focus on our flaws and forget all of the things that make us unique and special. Write down a list of your positive qualities and refer to it when you need a confidence boost.

3) Challenge your negative thoughts:  When you catch yourself thinking negative thoughts about yourself, take a moment to question why you believe those things. Chances are, they’re not true!

4) Spend time with people who make you feel good about yourself:  When we’re around people who make us feel good about ourselves, it’s easier to see our own worth. Surround yourself with positive people as much as possible.

5) Fake it until you make it:  If you’re having trouble mustering up some self-confidence, fake it until you make it! Act confident and eventually, it will become second nature

6) Do something that scares you:  One of the best ways to overcome our fears is to face them head-on. Do something that scares you on a regular basis and watch your confidence skyrocket.

7) Focus on your strengths:  Instead of dwelling on your weaknesses, focus on your strengths. When you start to see yourself as a valuable and capable person, your confidence will follow.

8) Be kind to yourself:  We’re often our own worst critics. Cut yourself some slack and try to be understanding and compassionate with yourself.

9) Make healthy choices:  When we take care of our bodies and make healthy choices, we feel better about ourselves. Eating right, exercising, and getting enough sleep are all great ways to boost your confidence.

10)Accept yourself unconditionally:  One of the most important things you can do for your self-confidence is to accept yourself unconditionally. Just as you are, right now, is good enough.

The Mentality That Made Charlie Munger “Weird”

Charlie munger was seen as an “odd” man.

Like nobody has ever met someone like Charlie munger.

And this main part comes from his one mental model.

Sam ovens uses this also.

It’s called “inversion”

Instead of thinking how to be happy in life, he thinks on how to be miserable in life, and he REVERSES that.

To build a successful business, he doesn’t think how to BUILD a successful business, he thinks “how would I build a failing business”

Then he REVERSES that.

It’s simple.

An anti vision is strong, only strong because you know EXACTLY what you DON’T want, but finding out what you do want may take years.

So, this is a quick one.

I want you to start practicing changing your thinking from now on.

Start using inversion, you will slowly start to think like Charlie munger.

To actually “start” this, just manually remind yourself everytime you make a decision.

Tell yourself “use inversion”

And after enough time, you start to adopt it as a habit, and then you’ll be seen as “odd” too. And that’s the goal.

So, just a quick one.

Invert everything.

I’ll give you some examples.

“How do I have a miserable day?”

Don’t get any work done, slack off the gym, eat shit food

“How do I stay broke?”

Don’t take any risks, don’t start a business, keep delaying it.

“How do I repel women away from me?”

Be too needy, focus on them more than your business, don’t take control, be scared to look them in the eyes, be insecure.

INVERT EVERYTHING.

Abundance Mindset.

this is the mindset that basically is the "I don't give a fuck if I lose this, I can get a better one."

this mindset can be applied to every area in life, women, business, gym.

ABUNDANCE IN BUSINESS.

this depends what business you are in.

IN SALES/SMMA

it could be the mindset when you get rejected on a sales offer

you should think to yourself "they are just not the right fit, I will find someone that will ne"

instead of "shit they rejected me that means my offer is shit and should quit rn"

be aware of it, because it very well may be your offer that is shit.

always have a good mentor.

IN ANY SOCIAL MEDIA BUSINESS.

this will happen to you, so prepare.

when once in a while, you get a viral video... 100k views wow...

and you might start thinking this :

I'm getting so much views but I don't even have a product, or I'm not even promoting my product so nobody is buying it.

or in dropshipping you'll think, shit I don't even have 1k followers yet I can't put a link in bio

or that "I'm getting so much views but no one is buying, I've wasted the views by having a shit sales page"

that's FOMO, fear of missing out.

you think you are missing out eyes on your product.

you are scared because you are missing out on sales...

and you didn't create a product in time, or you aren't promoting, or you don't have enough followers yet, or you don't think your sales page is the best and it doesn't convert much people into sales.

you have to have the mindset of, okay, i'm getting new followers, that means there will be more eyes on my product in the future.

not i need sales rn blah blah blah, because what's the worst case scenario.

the worst case scenario is that you get new followers to see your content, therefore in the future they are likely to buy your product.

longevity mindset, you have to think :

"if I done it once, I can do it again"

composure is key.

ABUNDANCE IN WOMEN

this one is so simple.

you will also get more women when you start thinking like this.

because the aura will radiate of you.

if you get rejected by a woman, you get broken up...

genuinely you can go to any town centre, where a lot of people are.

don't stare but just notice how many good women there are, there's infinite.

there is no such thing as the one.

SUMMARY

"THERE IS ALWAYS A BETTER OPPORTUNITY."

"they was just not the right for for me"

"if i have done it once, i can do it again and better"

Just Spam The 80/20 Until Success.

success is sitting at the spawn of zombies with your raygun.

you just sit there and spray until you're at your desired income.

don't work smart, or hard.

do both.

find out the task that moves the needle the most. (think the 80/20 rule from productive isolation)

it will be writing, outreach, recording... something of that nature... it's your "eat the frog" task.

cut out all noise, just put hours in that task none stop.

the more hours you put into that task, the more success you will get.

it's simple really... now you just wait for the universe to bring you your gifts.

in the meantime on your journey to success, just focus on the journey.

cherish the early mornings, cherish the late nights.

cherish them because they will be gone... it's all a part of the process.

enjoy the present moment as you do it, because you will get no results for the first couple months, so you need something to distract your mind with.

focus on the input and not the output, because the output simply will not be there until you've put enough input, that's how business works.

it's not a "pay per hour" thing, it's more like bursting a pipe.

you keep on applying pressure... and one day it bursts and you finally get that video that blows up, or that one sale that gives you a 2K/Month retainer.

just keep telling yourself that it due to blow up, because it will.

I mean... if you put 1000 hours into a business there is not a chance you wouldn't be rich.

it's really logical... just work so much that it would no longer be justified for you to be broke.

the reason people stay broke is because they switch business models or some bullshit.

there is no perfect "niche", just a "niche" that you have the most time spent in.

so... just camp there with the raygun and spray, you'll get to round 100 eventually.

that's the game.

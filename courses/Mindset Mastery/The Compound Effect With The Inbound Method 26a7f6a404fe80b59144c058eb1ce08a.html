The Compound Effect With The Inbound Method.

I’m going to show you exactly why the inbound method is the best business you could start, or any “community”

And it all comes down to one thing..

The compound effect.

Chances are, you know what this is.

If not, you’ll know by the end of this.

But listen up…

With every other business, you have to send cold emails… do cold calls… new dropshipping stores.

They don’t “add up” and become an “asset”

But with your community?

You add new things DAILY.

So what does this mean?

With each day that goes along, let’s say you put even one new module into your community courses.

After a year, you have 365 modules of pure value.

Let’s say it takes you an hour of writing to write one value PACKED module.

After a year, you have 365 modules that are absolutely stacked to the brim with value…

And the thing with this?

It just makes your community and course better.. and better.

That means overtime it will sell more… it will get better, more testimonials.. more EVERYTHING.

Ive been seeing this with my network.

I recently found a photo of the early days of the network, I’ll show it here now :

This was maybe in January/February.

I had a little bit of courses, and each of these courses were not even half the size of one course of mine now.

There has been days where I’ve created a whole course in one sitting, but that’s a hell of a grind and it gets tiring quickly.

You start writing.. the course is looking good… but after 4 hours of writing you just want to get it over and done with, so the course starts with a bang… you’re hyping it up in the early modules… then it goes to shit, and the value isn’t there because your brains cognition isn’t even at 50%

But when you write one HUGE value packed module a day?

Each module has quality, each module is a beautiful read.

It’s been about a year since I started this network, It’s January right now…

And honestly, the value is insane.

But it’s only insane for one reason.

The compound effect.

Each day I put something new in, always adding something new, each day is a new daily quests.

Now there’s an abundance of modules, abundance of videos, abundance of value…

And as this goes on, it just grows more and more… and with that you get more customers.

Just imagine if you started and never quit…

In 5 years time you could have 1000+ modules of very high quality modules that surprises the reader.

They’ll have never ending sauce, that means they will keep on paying you monthly because you keep on bringing life changing value to their life, it only makes sense.

Same thing with daily emails..

There’s been countless people that pop up on my email notifications and it just says “x liked your post” 24 times in a row…

This is because once you find a “true fan”, they just start to go through ALL of your content.

And the best part with the emails?

All of them will have a link at the end for them to buy your product.

That seed is planted in their heads again and again.

They’ll read your sales page dozens of times.

And soon enough, they’ll buy from you.

And when they buy, you’ve been adding modules consistently.

Your content, your course.

It becomes an asset that just makes you money.

The best asset is not property, it’s not stocks.

It’s simply the value you bring.

In this case, it’s your community/course.

After a while, it will start printing just because of the sheer value you have inside.

It’s impossible NOT to get rich when you don’t quit and you just keep going…

Do this for a year, you have 365 modules that are packed of value. 365 emails with seeds that are planted in the readers heads.

Top it off with a good sales page, and you’re printing, literally.

with any other business, this doesn't work, you don't build "assets", you just build clients that may leave you sooner or later.

This is the game of the compound effect.

Once you understand this, you’ll never go broke again.

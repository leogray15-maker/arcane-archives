No Zero Days (Eliminate This Mentality)

never have a "zero-day"

a zero day is a day where you do nothing.

i got this from a very successful entrepreneur, here, I'll paste it :

"Thought I'd share this since it helped me reach 12/K a month doing copy

Progress is exponential, not linear...

I was at £0 for 6 months then on my 7th month I landed a £2000/month client And ONLY two traits helped me get there...

  1. Working without seeing results
  1. Non-Zero Days

If any of you have read atomic habits There's a law called "The Plateau Of Latent Potential" So, what does this mean?

In short, you keep working until you experience that breakthrough moment...

All the work you're doing without the results showing up, It's meant for this very moment.

Now let's move on to the second trait

There's days where we feel lazy, unproductive and sloth-like...

You didn't do anything productive all day and it's 11:58PM?

Write one email

Do one pushup

Read one page of that self-improvement book

Why?

Because one is non-zero Everyday you must do something towards your goals Even if it's one...

Don't use this as an excuse to limit yourself tho Just on days where life gets in the way... "

you know...

sometimes you get caught up, life just gets in the way...

it's easy to say "I'll just get back on it tomorrow"

it's like the weirdos that count fap streaks saying "i failed, might aswell go 10 more times"

DON'T throw away the WHOLE day.

and even if you did throw away your whole day because you just started off with shit momentum...

one email.

one module.

one post.

one page.

end the day off right.

you're way more likely to slack off the NEXT day if you have a complete "zero day"

so next time you find yourself having a "zero day"

and there's 5 mins left of the day..

do SOMETHING.

write one email, one module, one pushup, one podcast.

anything.

just don't have a "zero day"

it's more important if it's at the END of a day, weirdly.

your brain is most malleable in the morning, and night.

this is why listening to terminate should be done, morning and night.

same with your alchemy of self.

never have a zero day, instill that in your mental.

11:55pm... and you've done nothing...

one page of a book.

one module.

one post.

anything. just do something.

it gets you in the right "headspace" to end the day off.

trust me, slow motion is better than no motion.

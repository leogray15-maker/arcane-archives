Mentality On Routines.

Listen, I don't advise you to follow a man made strict "routine"

I don't think you should be stressing every second of the day timing your "blocks"

I used to think like that... then I started smoking cigars and appreciating nature.

you should not follow a routine made by man, because it will be flawed.

rather you should just follow a structure to your day as we will.

you will do your important high leverage task.

then you will do all the things you need to do that you have written out the day before.

then you will plan and rest.

live like a hunting lion, that's the way.

that's it, you don't need to block out each second of your day, but you should have a basic "routine" that we will go through.

you should build habits that you see have a benefit to your life.

e.g meditate if you enjoy it and it brings a benefit, cold plunge if you like it.

you shouldn't let these things hold power over you, rather you should hold power over it.

after all, having to succumb to a routine is just feminine, you should own your "routine"

Meditation Guide

  1. Choose a quiet place: Find a comfortable spot where you won't be interrupted.
  1. Set a timer (optional): If you're new to meditation, consider setting a timer for 1-5 minutes to avoid feeling overwhelmed. You can gradually increase the duration as you get more comfortable.
  1. Focus on your breath: Sit there, close your eyes, focus on your breathing and you’ll get deep in thought.
  1. Observe your wandering mind: Your mind will inevitably wander and you will indulge in thought. At one point, you’ll realise that you was no longer being mindful of your breath.
  1. Refocus on breath: The moment you realise you’re not being mindful, slowly guide your attention to your breath again. Every realisation of unmindfulness and focus onto breath = 1 rep.
  1. Repeat: This isn't about achieving a perfect state of mental stillness. It's about creating a space of awareness by repeatedly noticing your breath and returning your focus. The more you practice, the more effortless this cycle will feel.

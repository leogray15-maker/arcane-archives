7. Illusion of Reflection

Imagine standing in front of a giant mirror. You look into it, and what you see is your reflection, right? Now, imagine that the Universe is like this mirror, and everything you see in it is a reflection of your own thoughts, feelings, and beliefs. If you look at the mirror and see only negativity, that's what you’ll get back from the Universe.

this way, you may get rejected by approaching a girl, because you feel like you WILL get rejected.

You might feel stuck, like obstacles are controlling your life and you're unable to change your destiny. But here’s the trick: this is an illusion. It’s not real. The truth is that you have the power to change what you see in the mirror.

Here’s how it works: you start with your thoughts. When you think negatively or feel like things aren’t going your way, the Universe reflects that back at you. It WILL happen.

It's like sending out a message, and the Universe responds by giving you more of what you don't want. You get caught in this loop: negative thoughts → more negativity in life → more negative thoughts. It feels like you have no control.

But the key to breaking this cycle is to realize that you are standing in front of a mirror. Instead of seeing obstacles, you need to focus on what you want to see. If you want a positive, successful life, start thinking about that. Start creating the image in your mind of what you want your life to look like.

and here's the key...

THINK AS IF YOU ALREADY HAVE THAT LIFE.

This is how you can turn the vicious cycle around. You need to look at your reflection and consciously decide what you want to reflect back at you.

even if it's the small things...

look in the mirror, point out what you do like.

this builds confidence, some say you become "narcissistic"

but weirdly... the"narcissistic" people always get what they want.

it's like going to a girls mirror and flexing in it, she will say you're too confident...

but you end up sleeping with her that night, because of that trait.

When you focus on the things you want, not on the things you dislike, the Universe starts to reflect that back to you. The obstacles you once felt controlled by will start to shift, and you’ll see opportunities instead. You have the power to control your reality by changing your thoughts. When you do this, the Universe responds with the energy you’ve sent out. The world starts to walk towards you because you’re walking towards it with intention.

This is the exit to the vicious cycle of negativity. Once you start controlling your thoughts and focusing on your desires, you tap into your external intention . When you align your thoughts with what you want, there’s nothing that’s impossible for you.

YOU ONLY SEE GOOD. IN YOURSELF AND THE WORLD.

The mirror of the Universe will show you a world that reflects your new, positive image. And just like that, your world changes.

it flips, once you flip your thoughts.

remember, it's simply a mirror.

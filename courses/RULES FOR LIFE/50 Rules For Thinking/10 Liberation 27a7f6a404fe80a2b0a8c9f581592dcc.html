10. Liberation

Imagine you're walking through life with a big weight on your shoulders. This weight is your worry, fear, and the pressure you put on yourself to achieve things. You think you have to push hard to make things happen, but in reality, all this extra stress is actually holding you back.

The key to Liberation is to lower the importance of everything you want. That sounds strange, right? But hear me out: when you place too much importance on something—like desperately needing to succeed or fearing failure—you create a block . This “excess potential” takes up your precious energy, and instead of feeling free and powerful, you feel stuck and drained.

Think of it this way: when you stop worrying about whether something will happen or not, you suddenly feel light and free. When you just allow your goal to come to you, without fighting or forcing it, everything starts to fall into place.

Once you see your "goal" as unimportant, it will FLOW to you.

just like when you're not looking for girls, for some reason you get a dm.

that's called synchronicity. it's never a coincidence.

when you're focused on yourself at the gym, some guy pressing 50kg dumbbells will come and ask for a spot.

it's happened, i can tell you from experience.

but when you're thinking "is that girl looking at me?", you will never have her approach you.

rather, if you lower the importance, she FEELS it, weirdly. and you may just get a convo out of nowhere.

she doesn't know why she did it, but you do.

it's literally quite magic. you will see it happen.

Here’s how you can start:

  1. Fear : How do you stop being afraid? By realising that fear is not real, it's just an illusion that comes from your past experiences, or thoughts, and they are all NOT real.
  1. Worry : How do you stop worrying? By taking action. Movement in the physical world scatters the negative energy and gets you out of your head.
  1. Desire : How do you stop hoping and desiring something constantly? By accepting that it will come to you sooner or later, as long as you take steps towards it. it's easy to compare yourself, especially with the internet. but it's not "running away", you're just stepping towards it, in your own pace, you dictate your reality, if you walk to the world, the world walks back. You’re not attached to the outcome anymore—you’ll keep moving forward no matter what. and you will get it. just like you don't sprint towards a girl when you go and approach her, you walk slowly. she's not going to run away if you walk towards her. but if you run? she might just run too.
  1. Importance : How do you stop giving too much importance to things? By realizing that nothing is as important as you think. Everything is part of you, and you don’t need to give special power to anything. there is no such thing as importance. everything just IS. if a girl looks good, she just has facial features in the right spot, and she may have some curves on her body. it's nothing special, she just looks the way she looks because of cause and effect. everything just IS, it is not good or bad, beautiful or ugly, it just IS.
  1. Irritation : How do you stop getting irritated by things or people? Play with the "pendulum" (see the thinking course). Don’t engage with it. When you act illogically or don’t react as expected, you break free from its grip. you "starve it" from energy.
  1. Guilt : How do you get rid of guilt? By not justifying yourself to others. You’re the creator of your own world. Your choices are yours, and they don’t need to be explained. there is no such thing as guilt, just things that don't align with "common" people's rules. do not feel guilt. as you're never guilty, you're just playing by your minds rules (and... there are no rules)
  1. Pain : How do you deal with emotional pain or hurt? By stopping the fight with others. You don’t need to engage in battles with people or situations. Instead, keep moving with your current flow in life. (dont feed the pendulum)
  1. Unable to deal with pain? Sometimes, it’s okay to just allow yourself to realise that thought . You don’t have to fight it. when you fight it. you feed the "pendulum" Let yourself feel what you feel, then move on. your thoughts are not YOU. it's just an illusion. focus on the good.
  1. Problems pressing in on you? Don’t buckle under pressure. Use coordination of intention —stay focused on your goal and trust that it will come to you as long as you take it one step at a time.

In short, Liberation is about letting go of the pressure, releasing the need to control, and just allowing things to happen naturally. The Universe is ready to deliver everything you want—it’s your birthright. But first, you need to free yourself from the weight of worry, and move forward with ease, trust, and a calm mind. When you stop giving everything so much importance, you’ll find that everything falls into place, effortlessly.

don't stress about anything. just realise that it's all a step. a step towards your goal.

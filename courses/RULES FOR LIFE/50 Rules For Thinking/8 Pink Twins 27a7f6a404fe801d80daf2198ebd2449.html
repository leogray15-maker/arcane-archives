8. Pink Twins

Imagine wearing a special pair of glasses, let’s call them your "pink glasses." These glasses are magical because they help you see the beauty and happiness in the world around you, even when others can only see the negative. People might try to tell you that you need to be more "realistic" or focus on the problems and challenges, but when you wear your "pink glasses," you’re choosing to see life in a different way.

Now, when something wonderful happens to you, like a little win or something that makes you smile, catch that feeling . Feel how good it is, and then, carry that feeling with you. Keep focusing on the good things, no matter how small. And before you know it, you’ll start to see more and more "pink twins" in your world—places, moments, and people that bring happiness, magic, and miracles. The more you focus on this positive energy, the more it will flow into your life, and soon your world will change in a beautiful, dramatic way.

Here’s the secret: You are the one who colors your world. Your thoughts are like paintbrushes, and the way you think shapes the world you live in. If your thoughts are mostly negative, that’s what your world will feel like—full of obstacles and sadness. But if you choose to focus on the good things, even the little moments of happiness, your world will start to feel more like heaven.

Heaven and Hell aren’t some faraway places. They are here, in our everyday world, and you’re choosing between them with each thought. Sure, there are things like accidents, crimes, or natural disasters in the world, but you don’t need to focus on them.

the news is designed to bring you down.

showing floods, tornados, negativity.

they will happen anyway, it's just a part of nature.

If you pay too much attention to those things, they’ll start to show up in your life. Instead, focus on the beauty, the kindness, the love, and the joy that’s also here. Evil and negativity may exist in the world, but by choosing to focus on the good, you can create your own little layer of peace and happiness, where these negative things won’t affect you as much. Your "pink twins" will keep showing up, and soon, your world will glow with positivity.

you will start to make more money, more muscle, beat prs.

You hold the key to how you see the world. Choose wisely, and let the magic happen.

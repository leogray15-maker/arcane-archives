6. Boomerang

The Universe is like a giant boomerang that always returns what you throw at it. Imagine you have a boomerang in your hand, and whatever energy—positive or negative—you throw out into the world, it comes back to you. If you throw out negativity, like anger, hate, or frustration, it will circle back and hit you. If you throw out love, joy, and gratitude, that’s exactly what you’ll get back. It’s simple: what you give out comes back to you, just like a boomerang.

Let’s say you really hate something—maybe you hate traffic, or a certain situation, or even a person. You put all your energy into that hate. You think about it a lot, you get frustrated about it, and the Universe says, “Okay, this is what you’re focusing on, so let me give you more of this.” The more you focus on what you don’t like, the more of it you will see in your life. It’s like you’re creating a world full of things you don’t want just because you’re thinking about them.

But here’s the key: you can change that. Instead of throwing out negativity, start throwing out positivity. Think about the things you love, the things that make you happy. Focus on the good in your life, even if it’s small things. Maybe you love your pet, or a sunny day, or a good meal. When you focus on these positive things, the Universe starts to send more of that good stuff back to you. It’s like the boomerang comes back with more love, more joy, and more things to be grateful for.

this way, you trick your mind into thinking you ALREADY have everything that you want.

the universe then has no choice but to give you everything that you do want.

you "trick" your mind into thinking that you already ARE abundant, therefore everything you have will be in abundance.

This isn’t just about thinking happy thoughts once in a while—it’s about making it a regular habit. Every day, start by focusing on what you enjoy, what makes you smile, what you appreciate. Even if things are tough, look for the positive. Start declaring to yourself, “My life is getting better and better,” and you’ll see how quickly the Universe starts to respond. The more you focus on love and positivity, the more you’ll find it everywhere you go.

The best part? Love is also a boomerang. When you send out love, whether it’s to people, to yourself, or to the world, it will come back to you multiplied. So, think of the Universe like a mirror that reflects whatever energy you send out. If you send out negativity, you’ll see more negativity. But if you send out positivity, you’ll see more positivity. Your thoughts are powerful—they shape the world around you. Choose carefully what you send out, because it’s coming back.

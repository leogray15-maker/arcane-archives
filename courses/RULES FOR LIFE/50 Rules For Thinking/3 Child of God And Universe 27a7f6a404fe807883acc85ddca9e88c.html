3. Child of God And Universe

no matter your religious beliefs, this applies.

Declaration:

You are a part of something really, really big. You are a child of God, and your life is a special part of His dream. Imagine you’re playing in a huge game, and the game is God’s dream. Every time you make a decision, it's like you're following the rules of the game, but also helping to shape it!

When you want something, you don’t have to beg or pray and ask for it like you're waiting for a present. Instead, just decide what you want and believe it’s already yours. Think about it like when you want to build something with Legos. You don’t beg the Legos to come together. You just know how to put them together, right?

God is always with you, in you, helping you put everything together. So, you don’t need to ask Him for what you want, just intend it. Like setting your mind on a goal. You already know what you want, now just allow it to happen. It’s like when you already know you’re going to win the game, you don’t need to keep asking, "Am I going to win?" You just know.

Interpretation:

Think of life like a play in a big theater. God is the director, the actor, the person making everything happen. He is in everything and everyone, including you. Every choice you make, every feeling you have—it’s all part of God’s bigger plan, like Him playing a game through you.

But when we come to Earth, we forget how powerful we are. We forget that we are here not just to play by the rules, but to help create the game too. You are not just a player—you’re also the creator!

Imagine God sends you into this game, this world, and gives you freedom to make your own choices. But sometimes, we forget. We start thinking we’re just here to play the game, and we forget that we can make the rules, too. The truth is, God wants to play with you and through you. You are not here to beg for things or wish for things to happen. You are here to create with Him, like two artists painting a picture together.

The secret is that you are not just a player; you are a co-creator. You and God are making this world together.

You are making your OWN world in this life, there are no rules.

remember...

rule 1 : there are no rules.

rule 2 : don't forget the first rule.

So, stop doubting! Stop asking for permission or waiting. Just intend what you want, and believe it will happen. Take steps towards it, and you will get there.

You’re part of the biggest dream ever, and you already have the power to make it come true, you are awake, and you realise you can create anything.

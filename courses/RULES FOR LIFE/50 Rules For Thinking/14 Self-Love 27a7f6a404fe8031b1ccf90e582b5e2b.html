14. Self-Love

Self-Love

The key to happiness and fulfillment in life begins with loving yourself . Without self-love, no one else can truly love you, and you’ll never be able to experience true joy. When you don’t love yourself, there’s a constant conflict between your mind and your soul, and this conflict will show up in your physical appearance and character. This negative energy pulls you into a life where things just seem to get worse. You’re stuck on the wrong paths, the wrong lifelines

by self love, I don't mean the bullshit that people tell you.

I mean self love as in CONSTANTLY improving.

when you're constantly improving, in money, business, gym, prs.

there is no choice BUT to love yourself.

Instead of focusing on what’s wrong with others or comparing yourself to them, the secret is to improve yourself first . We are often pressured by pendulums —the social forces around us that push us to reject our uniqueness and make us feel like we need to be like everyone else. These pendulums get us caught in a cycle where we constantly compare ourselves to others, and the more we do this, the more we dislike ourselves. And the result? We lose our true power.

Here's the truth: The more you stop comparing yourself to others, the more you embrace your uniqueness. When you stop trying to measure up to someone else’s standards, you stop giving away your energy to things that don’t serve you. You don’t need to be perfect to love yourself—you just have to progress.

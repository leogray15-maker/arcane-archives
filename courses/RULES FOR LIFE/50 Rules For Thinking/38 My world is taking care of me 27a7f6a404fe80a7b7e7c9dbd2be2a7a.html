38. My world is taking care of me.

The “My world is taking care of me” declaration is a powerful affirmation to center your mindset and realign yourself with positive outcomes. Zeland emphasizes starting your day by telling yourself this phrase, reinforcing the belief that no matter what happens, the universe or your world is working in your favor. It’s about setting your intention and shifting your perspective to one of trust, rather than resistance or fear.

When negative events or challenges arise, it’s natural to react with frustration or doubt. However, Zeland suggests that you counteract this tendency by using the declaration as a way to reframe the situation. By affirming that your world is taking care of you, even in times of hardship, you can shift your energy and mindset back to a more positive state. This affirmation becomes a reminder that setbacks or obstacles are not the end of the road, but rather a part of the process that ultimately serves you, even if it doesn’t seem that way at first.

this way, you allow synchronicities to come in.

your car breaks down, you react positive, you open up your email and you got a sale, it covers the cost.

it may sound bullshit, but you will believe it when you see it.

The Universe responds to the energy you put out. If you respond to negativity with negative energy, you're simply adding fuel to that fire. However, by affirming that your world is taking care of you, you're sending out a vibration of trust, gratitude, and surrender to the flow of life. This creates a ripple effect, helping you to stay aligned with better life lines, keeping your energy high, and ensuring that you're on the right path to getting your desires.

This phrase is not just a surface-level affirmation—it’s a reminder that your attitude and energy are in your control, and by declaring that your world is supportive, you start to see and experience the support the Universe is already offering you. The technique, though simple, is extremely powerful in shaping your perception and reality. Try it out for a few days, and observe how it influences your energy and outcomes.

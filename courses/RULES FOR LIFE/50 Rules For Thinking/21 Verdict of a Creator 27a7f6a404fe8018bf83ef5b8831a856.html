21. Verdict of a Creator

Declaration:

Throughout your life, you’ve been told how to live, what to believe, what is right, what is wrong, and even how to behave. From childhood, society, family, and external influences have shaped your understanding of morality, success, and purpose. But now, you have the power to break free. You have the birthright to create your own rules within the layer of your world. You have the right to declare what is right and wrong for you, without needing anyone’s approval, as long as your actions do not harm others. By declaring your own Verdict, you free yourself from the oppressive circumstances that hold you back. This declaration empowers you to confidently believe that everything in your life is unfolding as it should be, because you’ve chosen it, and the Universe will mirror that belief and respond accordingly.

Interpretation:

Everyone has their own opinion on what is good or bad. What one person might perceive as a blessing, another might see as a curse. It’s easy to lose sight of your own internal compass, especially when societal pressures or mass opinions try to define what is right for you. But, as a child of the Universe, you have the same creative power as anyone else. You are the ruler of your own life, and you can define what is positive and negative for you. The Universe is a mirror—it reflects the beliefs and attitudes you hold, no matter how others might perceive them.

However, there is a key condition: when you make a declaration about your life, you must be willing to take action in the physical world. Simply changing your beliefs or making metaphysical shifts is not enough. If you feel fear, self-doubt, or a sense of unworthiness when it comes to pursuing your goals, your Verdict loses its power. Your goal then becomes a mere desire—something intangible that lacks the energy of intention to manifest. Remember, desires do not come to fruition, only pure, focused intention does. It’s not about whether your thoughts are right or wrong—it’s about how deeply you believe that your thoughts and actions are correct for you.

Your Verdict becomes powerful only when your mind and soul align. When you are united within, there is nothing standing in your way. The Universe will echo your belief and provide the opportunities needed to create your reality. Your Verdict is the key to stepping into your true power, where you no longer follow the rules set by others, but create your own path with conviction and purpose.

Key Insights:

  • Create your own rules: You have the power to define what is right and wrong for you. Your choices are yours to make, as long as they do not harm others.
  • The Universe mirrors your beliefs: When you declare something to be true, the Universe will reflect that belief back to you. The world around you will shift to align with your intentions.
  • Action is essential: It’s not enough to declare your intentions metaphysically; you must act on them in the physical world. Without action, your intentions are just dreams.
  • Mind and Soul alignment: Your Verdict is powerful only when your mind and soul are in harmony. When you are united, you are unstoppable.
  • No one’s opinion matters but yours: Stop seeking approval from others. Your truth is your own, and it holds the power to shape your reality.

In conclusion, the Verdict of a Creator is about reclaiming your power and choosing the path that aligns with your true self. When you declare your intentions with full conviction and align your actions with your beliefs, you begin to see the Universe unfold in ways that reflect your deepest desires. This is the magic of creation—owning your life and confidently walking the path that you choose.

there is no right and wrong in the world.

there is only right and wrong decisions.

and the right decision is a decision aligned to your goal.

the rest are wrong.

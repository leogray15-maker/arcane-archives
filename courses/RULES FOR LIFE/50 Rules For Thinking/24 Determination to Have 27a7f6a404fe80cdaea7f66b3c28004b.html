24. Determination to Have

Declaration:

When you let go of all doubt and fear, and you are left with nothing but pure determination, the impossible becomes possible. You can pass an exam without studying the material, win a court case when everything is stacked against you, and achieve goals that once seemed unreachable. The key is to release the desperate desire to obtain what you want at all costs . This desperate desire only creates resistance. Instead, embrace a mindset of acceptance . Act as though you have already achieved your goal. This mindset removes resistance, as you are no longer grasping or struggling for the outcome. Avoid emotional attachment to the goal, acting without passion or urgency, as if what you want is already yours. When you set your intention, trust that the Universe will fulfill it. There is nothing to worry about; there is no reason to hope. Only your determination to have it is needed.

Interpretation:

In the metaphysical world, a wish is simply focused attention on the goal itself, while an intention is focused on the process of moving towards that goal. A wish is like staring at a life path that contains your desired outcome, but without making any effort to move towards it. It’s not the wish itself that brings the goal to you—it’s you who must travel through the time-space continuum, aligning with the lifeline that already contains your goal.

Intention comes in two forms: external and internal. External intention is the process of deliberately choosing a lifeline that already contains your goal. Internal intention, on the other hand, attempts to change the world around you to bring the goal to you on your current path. However, you cannot make the goal come to you through internal intention. You must release enough energy to allow the Universe to move you to a lifeline where your goal exists. This is why external intention is more powerful—it involves setting your intention and trusting the Universe to move you accordingly, allowing time for the transition.

when you walk towards the world, it walks towards you.

when you walk towards your goal, it walk towards you.

the world is a mirror, remember?

To effectively use external intention, you must learn to let go. This means releasing any resistance—doubt, fear, hope, or desperation. When you remove these emotional obstacles, you free up energy to move in the direction of your goal. Even without advanced techniques, if you can maintain an emotion-free state, without any attachment or expectation, your intention will manifest.

Remember that for external intention to work, there must be unity between the mind and the soul. Without this alignment, nothing is possible. Your potential is only as limited as your belief. If you believe with unwavering conviction that your goal is achievable, there are no limits to what you can create. The Universe responds to your confidence and alignment, and it will bring you to the lifeline where your desires are already waiting for you.

as long as you walk towards your goal, no matter how far.

it will walk towards you.

and if you walk for long enough, you two will meet. and you act passionlessly when you do...

because you expected it to happen.

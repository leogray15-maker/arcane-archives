4. Rising of a Star

You’ve probably heard people say things like, “Success is for the chosen ones,” or, “You have to be a certain way to make it big.” Society tells you over and over again that you have to fit a mold—that you have to look a certain way, act a certain way, or even come from a certain background. But here’s the truth: those rules don’t apply to you. The moment you stop trying to fit into someone else’s mold, you begin to see that you can create your own reality .

True success doesn’t come from following other people’s steps ; it comes from stepping out on your own, setting your own standards, and creating a new path for yourself. The people we look up to, the ones who seem to have it all—their fame, their success, their money—those people didn’t get there by copying others. They got there because they did something different , something no one else had done before. They didn’t just play the game—they changed the game.

Now, here’s where it gets interesting: The pendulums —the invisible forces that control how society thinks and acts— don’t like individuality . These pendulums want everyone to follow the same routine, follow the same path, so they can control the flow of things. When someone breaks free and does something that others haven’t, the pendulums try to bring them down . But here’s the twist: The pendulums are forced to make that person a star because they can’t stop someone who chooses to be different.

Think about it: the moment you decide to be yourself fully , you start shining in a way that others notice. Your uniqueness starts to create a new standard , and others begin to look to you, follow you, and imitate you. You become a star not because you were chosen or because you followed someone else’s path, but because you chose yourself . You became the one who didn’t need approval. You became the one who chose to set your own rules.

It’s all about allowing yourself to be who you really are. This isn’t about waiting for permission. This isn’t about praying for the right opportunities or asking for someone to give you a shot. It’s about knowing that you have the power to create your own path, and by doing so, you’ll attract everything you want into your life. The pendulums might try to control you, but once you’ve set your own standards, they have no choice but to support you .

The key to success is understanding that you are the one who sets the rules . You don’t need to play by anyone else’s game. You just need to start your own, and the universe will make you shine like a star .

The Habit to Remember focuses on the power of awareness and perspective in handling life's challenges. The declaration suggests that all problems arise from raised importance , meaning that when you place too much significance or emotional attachment to something, it creates obstacles and struggles. The key to overcoming these problems is not by fighting them head-on but by shifting your attitude towards them.

The essence of this concept is that your attitude and perception of challenges play a far greater role in determining your success than the actual circumstances themselves. Zeland emphasizes the importance of waking up to the realization that life is like a dream—fluid and under your control. By recognizing that you have the power to shape your reality , you can free yourself from being dominated by external events and allow yourself to move through life with a more balanced and conscious perspective.

Pendulums, in this context, are metaphors for external forces or societal expectations that feed off your negative energy. When you react emotionally to something, you give your energy away to these pendulums, which only leads to more negative outcomes. To break free from this cycle, Zeland suggests becoming more conscious and aware of your thoughts and reactions. By practicing awareness, you can begin to choose your thoughts and not let negative ones dominate.

The practice involves setting aside specific moments each day to observe your surroundings and thoughts without reacting. Start with just remember where you simply observe what’s happening around you and how you’re thinking. just do this a couple times in your day, manually, eventually it will become a habit, and your norm.

This practice helps build the habit of remembering that life is just a dream—a malleable experience that you can control. Over time, this habit will help you shift your energy from reactive to conscious, allowing you to navigate life’s challenges with more ease and less emotional turmoil.

as you can control dreams and what happens in it, you can control reality, because all is just a dream.

In summary, this technique encourages the development of self-awareness , emotional detachment , and conscious control of your thoughts to break free from external pressures and obstacles. By practicing this habit, you can gradually reshape your reality into one that aligns more with your desires.

40. Going alongside the Current

The "Going Alongside the Current" concept builds on the idea of trusting the flow of life and finding equilibrium with the surrounding world. Rather than trying to force things or control outcomes, the approach here is to let go and trust that things will unfold in their own time and way.

The declaration encourages you to release the urge to control and instead allow the natural course of events to happen without resistance. It emphasizes that nothing is as important as it seems —meaning that the weight you place on any situation or outcome is largely subjective, and often exaggerated by your own attachment and expectations.

By becoming an observer and approaching situations from a neutral perspective, you can see where you might be placing excess potential (which is essentially overvaluing something) and learn to let go . This involves not rejecting advice or opposing viewpoints immediately, but considering them with an open mind. Similarly, if someone says or does something that doesn't align with your views, instead of criticizing them, you can simply allow them to follow their path , just as you are allowed to follow yours.

The idea of trusting the current of life is about learning to flow with life’s natural rhythm rather than trying to control or direct it. When we attempt to control everything, we inadvertently create obstacles and conflict. By releasing the need for control and allowing life to flow naturally, you are much more likely to find yourself on the optimal lines of life , where things unfold smoothly and effortlessly.

This is a powerful way to align yourself with the Universe —by releasing resistance and trusting that life will provide what is necessary for your highest good, at the right time. The more you let go and follow this flow, the more you will experience a sense of ease and synchronicity in life.

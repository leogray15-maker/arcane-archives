Writing For Deep Dives Into Your Unconscious.

Journalling wasn’t created by “success gurus”, they don't even explain it right.

they just tell you "journal" or "get a piece of paper and write"

what even is that? that's how you know they're not in the game.

You see… since ancient times, there have been poets, there have been writers… shit like that, and that’s pretty obvious.

For some reason, this quote stuck out to me.

“He was utilising automatic writing as a method of psychological investigation”

And this is cold, let me tell you why.

Automatic writing is simply writing without thinking.

Or in other word… letting your subconscious mind write for you.

And my subconscious mind came up with a reason why this is actually such a tool for success :

When I was in my “grind” stage, nothing happening, no followers, no nothing.

Every night I had a ritual before I go to bed…

This ritual was simply to “refresh” my brain and kind of brainwash myself.

I did this by the thing that I call “reassurance journalling”

You may have seen this in the productive isolation course, but really… I just realised how powerful this is, and how I discovered how to really master this grind and how to achieve anything you want in life.

It’s simple, and it’s controlling your mind, not letting it control you…

I’m a normal person, so I had anxiety, doubts, and fears.

But that’s what this “reassurance” journalling combatted.

Without it, I don’t think I’d actually be here.

But here’s what I did :

First, I dove into my mind.

My mind was always focused on success, I couldn’t think about anything BUT that.

But it wasn’t just “thoughts” about success, rather it was negative AND positive thoughts.

Some thoughts of “you’re gonna make it”, some thoughts of “you’re not going to make it, just stop”

And I knew that a tree will fall whichever way it leans.

And in this case… negativity always powers over positivity in the human brain.

So the only way to get rid of this was to get rid of negativity entirely.

Or in my words, “stop feeding that pendulum”, and it will stop swinging.

Obviously, I didn’t know shit back then.

I’ve done it once, so I don’t have any doubt that I can start over again and be back at my exact point.

But for you? It may be different.

And that’s when this reassurance journalling comes in… or as carl Jung calls it “automatic writing”

I want to tell you how you can use this.

First, the mindset.

This will be your armour through the trenches of success.

Like in call of duty… or any game.

You get shot, you hide and heal.

When you experience doubt, you start automatic writing.

I want you to develop and “if x, then y” relationship with this.

If you get shot, you hide and heal.

If you experience doubt, you automatic write and reassure.

I’d do this every night and morning to start your day with assurance, and end it….

This is because your mind and identity is the most malleable when you’re coming from a waking state, or going into a sleeping state, you’re still kind of in that subconscious “sleep” programming mode, these are the moments where there are the highest amount of theta waves in your brain, meaning that you can program yourself.

That’s why you do the vision board after waking, and before sleeping… that’s why you listen to terminate, that’s why you will do this reassurance journalling.

I only did it before bed, so I’m not expecting you to do it in the morning, but if you’re locked and serious, I’d say it’s a high roi.

But here, let me explain to you the practical steps.

You can read over this a couple times, I’m just going to tell you :

You need to think of your subconscious brain as like an ai chatbot.

You give it a prompt, and it will answer.

The conscious brain gives a question, the subconscious gives an answer, because that’s where all the “answers” are stored.

You know when you read a book then you feel like you’ve forgetten what you learnt?

But a couple weeks later you remember that one little thing from the book randomly?

That’s because you stored it in your “subconscious library”, and your conscious mind found it and pulled that “book” out when it was needed, that’s one reason self education is so powerful… you’re arming yourself with literal “books”

Your subconscious holds everything, and I mean everything, you just can’t see it.

So... with this in mind.

What prompt do you want to ask your subconscious brain?

you know your problems better than me.

You simply give a prompt, and let your subconscious speak.

An example could be “what are your thoughts of me succeeding”

Then you let your brain write, don’t hold back. nobody is going to see it.

See it as if you’re mentoring someone.

Jordan peterson said this, act as if your own self is responsible for you.

Act as if your job is to be a therapist to yourself, literally.

Any question you have about your own behaviour, it’s simple to answer, just do the prompt.

Get a piece of paper, go into a dark room, light a candle, write the prompt, let your subconscious brain write.

“Automatic” writing is powerful, extremely powerful.

Use it as a tool, it’s a tool to uncover the side of yourself that is bringing you down even if you don’t know why it’s bringing you down, or how.

Get to the root, and rip it out, this can only be done with automatic writing.

Connor mcgregor used to do this before a fight, he’d dive in to what he called “Narnia” and reversed whatever wasn’t benefitting him.

Once you realise what is dragging you down, you can kick it off.

So that’s the point of this module.

I’d like you to try automatic writing right after reading this, or anytime tonight.

Put in a prompt, then tap into automatic writing.

Note : at the start, you will not feel as if this is right, but soon enough you’ll find yourself into a flow state writing.

Let it write, and you will unveil your true self… or in other words.

Your personality NO.2, the real one.

Unlock true freedom and peace.

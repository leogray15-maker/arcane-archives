The Niche Of "The Program"

if you're not doing the "program" and you're doing the book of secrets, you can skip this.

you can read it, but don't get confused.

so.. let's get into it.

This is where things get unique.

Everyone wants financial freedom, that's not hard to see.

But there are hundreds if not thousands of ways to get there.

What is YOUR way?

What are the steps you've taken to achieve ANY goal within one or multiple of those domains?

Is it budgeting? Meditation to have a better relationship with money? Starting an affiliate marketing business, learning about human psychology and human nature to understand humans to sell better? using the subconscious mind to align yourself with the actions of money? using your dreams to align your past trauma to money?

What about getting in shape?

Is it intermittent fasting? Low-volume weight training? Running? Boxing? Crosstfit?

You are helping people achieve one of their base desires with one of your interests in the form of a program that gives them a transformation from A-B.

To make it more unique, you are going to sell to the person you know best:

Yourself.

WHY are you learning about the things that you learn?

So, let's turn ourselves into our customer avatar.

What's the importance of this?

This will determine the frame from which you write, market, and create your products.

This is the specific person you are trying to help.

This is what you remind yourself of any time you write content, write promotions, launch a product, send an email, or anything else of that nature.

You are attempting to help someone achieve a transformation you've made in your life.

You are targeting and attracting people who want that same transformation. That's your niche.

And if you haven't made a transformation in your life, You need to LEARN.

This transformation does not have to be dramatic, it can be quite simple as long as it's desirable.

think of how you can get an interest to make a "transformation" to a person.

for example, how a productivity course can make someone from a lazy person into a machine.

to help the "transformation" think of this...

1) Where Was Your Starting Point?

Dig deep and think about what your life used to look like related to your topic, skill, or interest you plan to monetize.

  • Did you hate working long hours?
  • Did you hate the thought of an unlived life?
  • Did you hate being locked up inside all day?
  • Did you hate being unhealthy and overweight?
  • Did you hate being unhappy and negative all the time?
  • Did you hate procrastinating and feeling shit about it?

Start a list of things you didn't like in your past.

Your past isn't just a long time ago. It could be yesterday for all I care.

You may be wondering, "What if I don't have the experience to teach this?"

That's a common trap for a lot of people.

Let me ask you, how do you gain experience?

Yes, by doing it yourself… but what matters more are the results you get for other people.

BY LEARNING.

A tricky question:

Can you get results for other people if you never help them?

How do freelancers gain experience? They do work for clients. They just don't charge as much or set high expectations.

The same goes for anything else you sell.

A planner, a course, e book, anything.

You need people to buy it in order to (1) get results (2) be able to improve the product and (3) become an expert yourself.

You will never become an expert if the only person you help is yourself. You have to help other people with different situations and perspectives to refine your craft.

You can't and won't have this 100% clear right now. You should come back to this when ideas come to mind as you experience life.

2) What Made You Want To Change?

this is WHY do you want to teach and learn the things you want to learn?

what impact did learning your loved topic have on your life?

WHAT PROBLEMS DID IT SOLVE?

In order to get across to our potential customers, we need to guide them to realize what made you change.

We need to amplify their problems and make them understand how it is truly impacting their present and future.

  • What was your mindset then?
  • Where would your life end up if you kept that mindset?
  • What made you interested in your main topic or interest?
  • What problem of yours were you trying to solve?
  • What parts did you struggle with?
  • What were your biggest "aha" moments?
  • What were your objections before you decided to start?
  • What do you wish you had done differently?

If you can accurately convey the transformation people will make with your marketing, you are set.

With content, this happens over months and months sprinkled throughout your writing.

On a sales page, this happens all at once.

IN THE SALES PAGE, YOU LIST THE DIRECT TRANSFORMATION YOUR CUSTOMER WILL GET BEHIND THAT PAYWALL, THAT IS VALUE.

3) What Does Your Ideal Day Look Like?

Think about what your life looks like now.

What does your ideal day look like?

As an example, I want to:

  • Wake up around 5
  • Write books and content for the first few hours of the day
  • Go to the gym and have a high intensity heavy workout.
  • Manage my product, systems.
  • Go on multiple walks, smoke cigars without the desire to check my phone (just be at peace)
  • Study my interests and dive down interesting rabbit holes
  • Have nice dinners with friends and family
  • Go on dates with hot girls

The important thing here:

This is relatively unique to you, but it is enticing to people who share your same goals. That's who you are marketing your product to.

This will help shape the transformation you are marketing for your products.

People don't buy products just to buy them, they buy them because they want to see a change in their life.

and here's the thing that a lot of people get wrong and try to "fake it"...

you DON'T sell a MONEY MAKING COURSE if you HAVEN'T MADE MONEY.

this is why you sell a "book of secrets" that will help people in each area of life, think of the 4 eternal markets we talked about... OR you sell a program that is not related to money. BUT IT STILL OFFERS A TRANSFORMATION.

THEN you can teach how to make money based off how YOU MADE MONEY OF TEACHING YOUR INTERESTS.

TEACH PEOPLE HOW TO DO SOMETHING THAT HELPS THEM IN LIFE.

this could be anything, as I said before... learn a topic... go down rabbitholes... get info.. MAKE IT INTO A "TRANSFORMATION PROGRAM"

you just have to know this topic very deep, and then you make a "transformation" program on it.

4) What Goals Have You Achieved That Others Desire?

People don't know what you know.

No matter how "normal" or underwhelming they seem to you now, what goals have you achieved?

Have you learned to manage your emotions? Make a lot of money? Design a website? Get a better job? Write a better resume? Understand bitcoin? Get lean? Lift a lot of weight? Not burn in the sun? (Yes, that's a thing based on specific diets and habits).

There's literally a course on how to get your dog to stop barking at night, there's UNLIMITED opportunities.

You have skills, interests, and experience – what have they helped you achieve in life?

5) How Does Your Product Topic/Idea Fit Into That Equation?

Think of:

  1. Where you started.
  1. Where you are now and where you're going.
  1. The skills, knowledge, habits, and education required to get from number 1 to number 2.

this can be from undisciplined to disciplined through using manifestation and hacking your subconscious. that's just one example.

How does the topic of your product help bridge the gap between the two?

Your product should be like a taxi, people go from point A TO B, but they pay a taxi fare.

Why do people take ubers and taxis? because it takes them there QUICK.

people always say "you can just get everything for free online"

sure, you can... no one denies that.

but you're saving TIME, that's the real key.

just how you can walk to your destination but you choose to take a taxi, because it saves time and effort.

As we will learn in our marketing strategy, you will help people reach one desirable goal by giving them the education and habits in an easy-to-implement way.

As an example:

Where I was : unproductive, lazy, and numbed by cheap dopamine so my work suffers.

Where I am now : I wake up and get straight to work.

Where I want to be : To build my own business or get a promotion at a job.

Skills, education, & habits : mindset, self-discipline, and a productivity routine.

Why that matters : because your career, market value, and business depend on it.

Now you can see how this is starting to play out.

I have a transformation that I can help people achieve. I have topics I'm able to educate people in based on my own experience and practice.

6) Why Should They Care About Your Product?

This is where your marketing truly takes shape.

People don't care about your skill, interest, or knowledge.

People care about why it benefits their lives.

this is important, remember it.

Let's go back to point number 3 and what my ideal day looks like.

Walking is a massive part of my day because it's not as simple as "going on a walk."

  • Walking clears my mind for my creative work
  • Walking is a time block for creativity without distractions
  • Walking keeps me lean, tan, and active
  • Walking is where I do a lot of my ideation and writing
  • Walking is where I listen to audiobooks and lectures

I guarantee I could create a walking digital product with a walking routine to help people become lean and money making individuals.

So, write out the benefits of the product's topic/idea so you're able to amplify your marketing.

Get as specific as you can with all of these points, hit their DEEP DESIRES.

remember that, this will play into how we "market" our product.

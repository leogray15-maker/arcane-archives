Stage 1) The “Shut Off”

before we get into the supplements, we have to shut off our brain.

the book “deep work” talked about this.

you should have a ritual which signifies “the work is done”

your brain can freely switch from work mode, to rest mode.

the best one i’ve tested goes as follows :

  1. do all the work that you planned out to do (close all opens loops in your head)
  1. declare that all the work for today is done in your mind, write down the date, followed by “my work for today is done” in a handwritten journal
  1. shut off every “work” tab, so nothing reminds you of work.
  1. IMPORTANT : plan tomorrow, plan every task that you’re going to do (i do this on a whiteboard on my wall)
  1. organise your room/workspace, prime your room for tomorrow, this reminds you that the work for today is done
  1. go for a warm shower as your final stage of the ritual (knowing that you’re clean helps you sleep)
  1. stretch for a couple mins

note that number 4 is highlighted as important.

this is because planning your day before the day has actually started ensures that there’s no emotion correlated with the next day.

when you plan out your day the next morning, you’re rushing, and you’re planning with emotion.

when you plan your day the NIGHT before, there is no emotion, pure logic on what will progress you the next day. that’s why it’s so important.

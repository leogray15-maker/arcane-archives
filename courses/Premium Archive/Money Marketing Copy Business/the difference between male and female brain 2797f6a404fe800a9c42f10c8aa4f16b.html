MALE BRAIN TRIGGERS

dominance activation:

write: "while others struggle, you'll dominate" conversion: 8.3%

write: "join thousands of customers" conversion: 1.2%

always position as conquest, never community.

territorial triggers:

"only 47 spots available" = scarcity "beat 97% of men" = competition "exclusive access" = status "first to know" = priority

male brain cannot resist being first/best/only.

the power words:

  • crush (7x more effective than "succeed")
  • dominate (5x more than "achieve")
  • master (4x more than "learn")
  • destroy (6x more than "change")
  • own (3x more than "have")

the comparison trap:

never: "join other successful men" always: "leave other men behind"

never: "our community of achievers" always: "the 1% who actually win"

instant gratification sequence:

"get instant access" (not "lifetime access") "download now" (not "member portal") "start dominating today" (not "begin your journey")

male brain wants NOW, not LATER.

visual triggers:

  • product alone (87% preference)
  • technical specs visible
  • performance metrics
  • before/after contrast
  • competition losing

never show groups. always show victory.

FEMALE BRAIN TRIGGERS

belonging activation:

write: "sarah from austin just joined 5 minutes ago" conversion: 6.7%

write: "limited spots available" conversion: 2.1%

always position as community, never isolation.

oxytocin triggers:

"12,847 women trust us" = safety "moms like you choose" = identity "join our sisterhood" = belonging "we're in this together" = support

female brain needs group validation.

the trust words:

  • together (9x more effective than "alone")
  • share (7x more than "keep")
  • support (6x more than "help")
  • nurture (5x more than "build")
  • connect (8x more than "achieve")

the validation sequence:

show: "verified buyer: jennifer, mom of 2" then: "real results from real women" then: "see what other women say" then: "read their full stories"

female brain needs 4-7 validations before purchase.

social proof stacking:

  • faces of other women (mandatory)
  • emotional testimonials
  • group success stories
  • community celebrations
  • transformation journeys

never show just product. always show people using it.

THE STRESS TRIGGERS

stress him right:

"price increases in 2 hours" "only 3 left at this price" "offer expires at midnight"

urgency = testosterone spike = purchase

stress her wrong:

"hurry before it's gone" = abandonment "don't miss out" = anxiety "last chance" = shutdown

comfort her instead: "secure your spot" "guarantee your price" "reserve your access"

As many of you know, I am a HUGE fan of emulation.

That is - picking something that works on the funnel level and differentiating yourself on the brand level (mostly personal brands in our case).

Funnel = content, copy, landing pages, free products, paid products, services, and how you get people to buy. There is somebody in your industry who has already figured it out - steal from them.

Brand = why they choose to buy from you . Usually based on authenticity or authority (we’ll cover that in the next lesson).

We will be discussing the "growth" factor that fuels both of these. Your organic content - free lead generation.

How To Generate Unlimited Viral Content Ideas

The secret: DO WHAT WORKS.

You probably already follow brands that are similar to you. They have done something right somewhere along the way. Your job is to find that content and recreate it under your brand.

Where do you find content ideas?

  • Most popular YouTube videos from creators in your niche
  • Top-ranking blog posts found through Google search
  • The featured articles on Medium based on your interests
  • Top X posts from creators in your niche
    • You can type "from:thedankoe min_faves:500" in the X search bar to find posts of mine that have gotten over 500 likes. You can do this for any creator, just replace the handle after "from:"
  • Pinterest, Instagram, TikTok - I have not figured out how to find the best posts, but if they get high engagement you can take them

How do you use these content ideas?

The content you find will be tried and true (especially the YouTube videos, they work wonders for idea generation).

YouTube videos, podcasts, and articles can be turned into threads. You can also turn ideas from those videos into individual posts.

The main thing you are looking for here is the headline or video title.

Your job is to take that big idea and turn it into content that falls under your brand.

A headline can be turned into:

  • Short form content (X post, Instagram post, another headline)
  • An idea for your own expansion on that idea

The content in a video can be turned into:

  • List style X posts / social posts (if the original content is structured / numbered)
  • Individual posts from a random "knowledge bomb"
  • Threads that are already structured for you

YOU ARE NOT PLAGIARIZING. YOU ARE EMULATING WHAT WORKS. YOU NEED EXPERIENCE, SKILL, AND KNOWLEDGE IN THAT SPECIFIC AREA TO REWRITE IT AS YOUR OWN. I am assuming you all have this and will use this ethically.

This is what everyone does. We all just steal from each other and our buyers are determined by our branding + marketing + getting in front of their face with the right offer.

There is only so much you can talk about for a specific topic - so why not talk about the ideas that work?

We all have our own unique biases that will make these unique. This is an art.

The Natural Evolution

The longer you are in the game (through more experience and iteration) the more you will gravitate towards deeper content that SCREAMS insert your brand here .

When you are just starting out - you are somewhat forced to create basic content that is proven to work. Nobody knows who you are or what you are about - you need time, repetition, and an audience for that.

Grow by creating content that is proven to work. Slowly introduce your authentic voice, and make sure to establish authority along the way.

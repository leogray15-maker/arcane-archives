X Profile Optimization.

Right now is the time to switch from consumer to producer.

To do this correctly, you must:

1) Have an eye-catching and clickable profile picture - you want to set yourself apart from the sea of consumers, your profile pic should stop their scroll.

2) Have a professional banner with a clear image that gives off your "vibe" or a well-designed banner that tells what you do, avoid:

  • Group photos
  • Pics of yourself
  • Anything that isn't aesthetic
  • There are exceptions to this - but only for those who can display their personality very well in their content

3) Have a bio that converts viewers into followers:

  • A condensed version of your story
  • How you take your target audience from where they are now to where they want to be
  • Your accomplishments
  • What they are going to learn from you and what it will help them achieve
  • Have a pinned post that is either extremely valuable information specific to your target audience or your story

In essence, we are optimizing everything that is above the fold - what people can see when they click on your profile.

We will discuss this in-depth in a bit.

Don't overcomplicate this because I guarantee you will change this 50 times in your X journey... I did.

Who, What, Where, When, How

WWWWH (lol) is a simplified marketing tactic - we will be using this to get the first iteration of your profile down. Having this will start to attract your ideal customer.

This will allow us some quick wins until we dive deep into marketing.

Who, what, and where are priority.

The others are a bonus.

Remember that this is solely to help with clarity in creating your bio. If you already have one, don’t feel like you have to conform to these guidelines.

Who - who you help.

Example - creators

What - what you help them accomplish (pain points or benefits focused).

Example - make more product sales

Where - where they go next.

Example - Link in bio or pinned post (newsletter, low ticket product, lead magnet, etc)

When - when will they get results (the timeframe, this should be tangible)

Example - 30 days

How - how you help them accomplish what they want.

Example - X and funnels

Example for fitness:

  • Who - entrepreneurs
  • What - be more productive
  • Where - application to work with you
  • When - 60 days
  • How - fat loss through coaching

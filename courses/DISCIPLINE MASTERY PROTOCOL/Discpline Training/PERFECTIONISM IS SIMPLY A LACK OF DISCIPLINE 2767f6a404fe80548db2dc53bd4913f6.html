PERFECTIONISM IS SIMPLY A LACK OF DISCIPLINE.

imagine the fat guy saying.

I will only go to the gym when I find the perfect workout routine.

would that be better than just going to the gym,

doing 10 mins on the treadmill and going away?

action is always better than overthinking.

DONT BE A PERFECTIONIST.

"I won't post on YouTube or twitter because I don't have the best idea for a post"

you are relatable and similar to a fat man who needs the "perfect" workout routine.

your first post will always be shit.

imagine your future kid says to you.

"dad but I don't want to do this because i'm not good at it"

what would you say to him?

"son we do these things we are bad at to get better at them"

THERE WILL NEVER BE A RIGHT TIME TO START.

AND WHEN THE "RIGHT TIME" COMES, IT IS ALREADY TOO LATE.

start now and don't wait for the perfect moment.

you don't want to be like the fat woman who says "January 1st I'm starting the gym journey new years resolution yay."

as men we take action, not speak about it.

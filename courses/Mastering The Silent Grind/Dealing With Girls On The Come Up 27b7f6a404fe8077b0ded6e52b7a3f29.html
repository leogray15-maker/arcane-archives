but how do you balance this?

you can get lost in the sauce, of course, as with all things.

but whats more important? staying moving forward.

so, balance...

you should have a life which is full of money/fitness/girls/hobbies, anything else is a distraction.

but the main 2 that are the "foundation" is money and fitness.

without them, there's no point doing hobbies, and you'll get less bitches, inevitably.

the way to balance is a mindset, you have to see the money and health as the "basic attack"

everything else is a addition, it will come and go, but the money and fitness grind will always stay.

it's more of a mindset, enjoy the girls as much as you want, as long as it doesn't get in the way of money and fitness.

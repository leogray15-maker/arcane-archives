Certainty

There's one very important question to ask yourself when it comes to growth and expansion, and that is "What are you really in control of?"

As the subconscious' mind number 1 job is to keep you safe, it does so by keeping everything in equilibrium, balance and perfect harmony.

Any type of change is a potential threat, and that's why the mechanism of awareness is one that anything that moves, we notice quite easily.

Change is dangerous to the mind, and then we look for gain or loss and evaluate the position.

Any good safety system works by increasing certainty.

When we're selling stuff, we're just increasing the certainty.

Lack of certainty = unsafe = problem state.

If you are in control of anything, then you can have certainty.

But because we are not in control of ANYTHING, even MOST of our mind, that means we can not have certainty.

Stress comes from the mind wanting certainty but not having it.

Because of this, growth is very hard.

Stop seeking certainty, because certainty does not exist.

Now here's your exercise for the day: What can you control exercise? - List it out and then challenge it, and ask yourself "Do I really have control over this?"

I do not control anything, so therefore there is no certainty.

It's imagined.

If it is imagined, then you are in control of that imagination.

To the degree that you are in control of certainty is to the degree that you can imagine it.

And thus holds you back.

There are probabilities, but no certainty.

When you seek certainty, you are feeding fear, and when you are feeding fear

Whenever you're seeking something, it is FEEDING something within you.

You are afraid and when you are afraid you do not go there.

I "WANT" certainty = I LACK certainty = I have fear I WANT certainty > feeds fear

Fear prevents trying new and bigger things because of the lack of certainty of those.

Asking for advice, to the questions that you already know, is often seeking certainty.

Powerful Mindset Shift

Powerful Mindset Shift That Makes Your Emails Have More Oomph

If you'd like to multiply the chances of your email pitches being opened, read and acted on... then this quick tip will show you how.

Here's what you do:

Simply pretend you are not sending an email... but a regular piece of “snail mail” instead.

In other words:

You're not sending out 10,000 emails that “cost” nothing to send.

You are sending out 10,000 real letters - each with a real, first class stamp on it that costs real money to send out.

And so, instead of these emails costing you nothing to send... they are now going to put a $4,100 dent in your bank account.

When you factor in printing and envelopes, you're looking at even more.

Now, how will this make your email promotions more profitable?

Well, think about it this way:

If it was going to cost you $4,100 (or more) to send out your emails, would you send the same email pitches you are sending now?

For most of us, the answer is no.

We'd put way more time, energy and consideration into every aspect of the pitch. From the headlines (subject lines) all the way down to the lowly “P.S.”

Changes the entire complexion of the process, doesn't it?

And that's why if you can put yourself in this mindset - where it's going to cost you money to send your email, and getting sales is an absolute necessity - you are going to write your pitch and conduct your entire email campaign in an entirely different way than you would otherwise.

This isn't the “sexiest” email tip in the world.

But it can save you from making a lot of expensive mistakes.

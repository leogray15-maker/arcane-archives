The powerful four word phrase.

The powerful four word phrase, when done right, can make any claim you make believable.

I was debating whether or not I should teach this because it's that powerful.

Anyways, this powerful four word phrase is...

"As we both know"

Now let me explain why this works and in what context you should use it in.

One of the reasons it works is because it establishes common ground. It implies that you and the reader are on the same page.

This sense of alignment helps the reader feel like they’re part of an "in-group," which is psychologically comforting and makes the reader more open to the message.

This phrase works especially well when people already like you. It works well when people already know you and trust in you.

Note: If you're a guru or writing for a guru - it's the perfect phrase for you.

You'd need to build rapport for this to have effect.

The second reason it works is because it's subtle flattery. We all like flattery, I don't care who you are. It makes us feel good. It makes us feel appreciated. It makes use feel like we have value. It's hardwired into us.

It implies you respect the readers intelligence and knowledge. It invites a sense of trust. They don't feel like they're being educated but rather included into a conversation. Conversational copy is the highest type of converting copy.

Now the last reason why it's so effective is because it reduces resistance. We all put our guard up when we're getting sold to. It's natural.

In fact, one of the aspects of selling is informing them or making them aware but in this case, when you use the phrase correctly, it's like a gentle reminder which follows the path of least resistance.

I'll be transparent here, I couldn't find any examples of old sales copy with this specific phrase so do what you will with this information.

I've given you the logical reasoning on why it's effective so there's that.

Anyways, here are some examples of the top of my head:

You can probably "see it" here.

  • As we both know, investing in crypto is probably one of the smartest choices you can do today.
  • As we both know, educating yourself has become a rare behaviour in a world of endless short term dopamine.
  • As we both know, if you don't utilise the power of your subconscious mind, you'll be at a huge disadvantage.

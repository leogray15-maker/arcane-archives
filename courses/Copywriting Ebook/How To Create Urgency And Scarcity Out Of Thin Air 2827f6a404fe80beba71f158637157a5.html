How To Create Urgency And Scarcity Out Of Thin Air

Many of you will write copy for digital products where the offer is evergreen. There is no deadline and there's no scarcity...

So injecting urgency/scarcity becomes quite a bit of a challenge.

On that note, let's get started.

I'm going to show you an email from Kyle Milligan and we'll dissect the urgency/scarcity part together.

Here goes:

Subject: "Insurance Return Checks"

Body:

Even though "Insurance Return Checks" could help turn America's retirement crisis, they are not advertised.

Yet this potential "second source of income" has existed for years...

Collectively, the companies issuing them have been returning millions of dollars a year to investors.

Yes, thousands of investors have collected "Insurance Return Checks" (or "IRCs" for short)

Emma was declared $513,365 from her IRC plan last year in one payment.

Kirk's plan paid out an estimated $14,362 to him in early 2023. If he gets nothing else during the year, that averages out to $1,196 a month.

Mike went on a lot bigger, taking in an estimated $35,354 in one payment from his IRC plan.

And those who started early in this plan have noticed a nice pattern...The amount of money they started collecting keeps going up and up.

To learn more about what we've dubbed "IRC" and to find out how to set up your own plan with them starting today. I urge you to see this special report when you click here now!

"I urge you to". The word "Urge" is urgent, it's pretty self-explanatory but it seems not many people "see" it the first time.

Subject: AI "wealth window" is closing soon

Body:

Hello. I'm James Altucher.

I've ben called a "genius investor" by my fans...

And an "eccentric millionaire" by some others.

I think it's because I make big predictions...

That tend to come true.

Today, I'm making my boldest prediction ever.

Next-generation AI technology will create the first $100 TRILLION industry.

And there could be trillions available to those investors who get in early.

I put together this personal video (HERE)...

Revealing the AI stocks I believe...

Could turn as little as $10,000...

Into $1 MILLION over the next few years.

If you get in early, this one-time opportunity could...

Potentially change your financial circumstances...

For you, your family and your heirs.

Today, I want to show you how I believe...

AI 2.0 will open a brief "wealth window"...

That will slam shut soon.

If you've missed out on new tech opportunities before...

I urge you, do not ignore this message.

HERE is everything you need now

To your good fortune,

James Altucher

I'm hoping you would see at least one urgency trigger after reading this email

And it should be "I urge you"

But there's a lot more going on behind the scenes.

The one you probably missed out on is "If you get in early" and "who get in early"

You're not outright saying you need to "Act now" or else you'll miss this opportunity...

You're implying it. If you want to make big money, you should probably act now. That's what it's implying.

Now the other urgency trigger is "Slam shut soon"

It just says "soon", there's no date and there's no hard deadline.

It's implying you should act now. It's subtle which is why it's very powerful.

Now that's it for creating urgency and scarcity out of thin air.

When I learnt about this, it took my copy to a whole new level and you should introduce this concept whenever you can too.

It goes under the radar of your prospect and is an alternative to salesy urgency/scarcity.

You Don't Need Motivation, You Need Clarity.

motivation is not real, here's what you do instead :

you never needed motivation, you needed clarity.

humans will do anything but face a problem with a rational mind.

you will be stuck on watching youtube procrastinating work...

so what do you do? look up motivational videos.

where is the sense in that?

you see... we only "procrastinate" because the thing that we need to do is not right in front of us.

we know that we need to do some deep work...

but what exactly is "deep work"

it's not what you need to do, it's just what you call the activity.

there is no clarity in saying that you need to "work"

of course you have no motivation to "work"

you will set an alarm for 6am to do 2 hours of work...

bu then you wake up and you go back to sleep.

why? because what even is work?

you need to start telling yourself the exact activity you're going to do.

you're not going to work, you're going to sit down on your desk and complete something very specific.

you're not going to read, you're going to read a specific chapter of a specific book.

you're not going to workout, you're going to hit arms starting with triceps.

if you don't give yourself clarity...

the odds are against you.

the brain needs to figure out what to do and then do it.

the next time you tell yourself you're going to do something...

you should be clear, as if you're talking to an employee.

keep that in mind.

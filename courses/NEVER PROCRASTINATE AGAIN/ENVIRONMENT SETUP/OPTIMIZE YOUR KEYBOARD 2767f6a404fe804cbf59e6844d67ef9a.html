KEYBOARD.

IMAGINE you buy a keyboard which you just love the sound of?

find out what the best colour switches sounds like for you.

and you buy that.

you will find ANY excuse to type on it.

you will want to hear the sound of the keyboard.

meaning you will work on that keyboard more.

there are a couple main keyboard switches that you can purchase.

I currently have blue switches, which are loud as fuck, but when I wear noise cancelling headphones they sound nice and satisfying which I enjoy.

and I've had this keyboard since like 2019-2020 lol since I was cheating on siege with a mouse and keyboard adapter.

and I wanted my switches to be the loudest so people knew I was cheating.

and now I use it to work.

if you have a membrane keyboard (no switches) and makes no fancy noise.

I HIGHLY recommend you to buy a mechanical one that you like the sound of.

because it's a worthy investment.

if you don't know anything about it.

you should probably start on this video :

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-Ln9rA_usoY

do your own research also, watch a couple videos to see what you like.

go to the shop and go test some keyboard out, in the UK we have currys, idk what they have anywhere else.

and to buy the keyboard with the switch you want.

just type in "x switch keyboard"

and MAKE SURE it is not a "mechanical feel" keyboard.

that's just a smart marketing term, make sure it is real and actually has coloured switches on the inside.

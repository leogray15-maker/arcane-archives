1) Recognize The Deep Rooted Biases.

and we'll start off with the...

CONFIRMATION BIAS.

the confirmation bias is a very One-Sided view to life.

listen.

let me explain this in an easy way.

Let’s Use The Carnivore Diet As An Example.

you might relate to this.

IMAGINE THIS SCENARIO.

You Want To Eat More Eggs + Red Meat.

but your mum doesn’t allow you.

why?

because she searched it up.

and says it causes cancer, heart disease.

and you know that’s wrong.

SO YOU TRY CONVINCE HER.

by showing her REAL doctors, that actually aren’t fat (unlike most doctors)

saying that red meat and eggs are the healthiest food.

and she disregards it…

she says “these social media doctors are stupid anyway.”

“stop eating red meat”

“there’s a study that shows it does colon cancer!"

SO ONCE AGAIN….

you show her a study that proves that wrong, a study that says you DON’T get colon cancer.

and you show her testimonials, random people comments on youtube.

talking about how “Carnivore changed my life!”

you’ve given her so much evidence.

BUT WHAT DOES SHE DO?

she goes back to “but this study said it gives you colon cancer”

AND THIS IS THE CONFIRMATION BIAS.

when you have a belief so strong.

that you don’t want to let go of.

so you disregard ALL of the evidence that it’s wrong.

and you heavily bias your brain into thinking that the ONE study is correct.

despite all of the other evidence.

THIS ALL LINKS BACK TO THE BRAIN/EGO.

as humans, we HATE being wrong, above anything else.

if someone wins an argument, you can’t help but to put your head down.

I told you in the first module.

our brain looks for the thing that CONFIRMS our beliefs/identity.

and disregards everything else.

we hate to be wrong.

so our brain blocks out everything that doesn’t align with out beliefs.

This Is The Confirmation Bias.

SUMMARY :

  • YOU APPROVE AND LOOK FOR INFORMATION THAT SUPPORTS YOUR BELIEF.
  • YOU DISREGARD ALL INFORMATION THAT GOES AGAINST YOUR BELIEF.
  • YOU ARE CLOSED MINDED, BECAUSE YOU DON’T WANT TO BE WRONG.
  • YOU ONLY WILL ACCEPT INFORMATION AS "TRUE" IF IT SUITS YOUR BELIEF.

it's all natural, we just don't want to be wrong.

but if we understand this.

we can actually put it to good use.

and when someone uses the confirmation bias against us.

we can make them feel like a retard lol.

so here’s the message for the confirmation bias.

BE AWARE OF THE CONFIRMATION BIAS.

don’t be too locked in on one belief.

have an open mind.

take in all the information, even if it’s not linked to your belief.

be open to change your mind.

the first step to growth.

is to drop the ego.

LOOK AT INFORMATION WITH A NAKED EYE.

and take it in as words.

and don't tie it to one belief.

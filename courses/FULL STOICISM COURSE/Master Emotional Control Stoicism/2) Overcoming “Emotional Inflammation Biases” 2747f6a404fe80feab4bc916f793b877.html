2) Overcoming “Emotional Inflammation Biases”

low grade biases.

the ones we discussed in the last module.

these always will impact our thinking.

but….

these were easy to get rid of.

all you had to do is realise that they’re actually happening.

which is not the case with these….

Inflamed Emotions.

these come at certain moments.

when our emotions reach an exploding point.

which most of the time, is caused by something external.

like a guy slapping your girls ass.

these are the emotions that get under your skin.

and these are dangerous.

and here’s why.

Our Attention Is Caught Completely.

the more we think about the inflamed emotion.

if you’re angry, and you think of why you’re angry.

if you’re sad and you think of why you’re sad.

you will focus more on it.

making that emotion EVEN MORE inflamed, and even more dangerous.

Our Mind Tunnels Into The Emotion.

our mind starts to see everything through that lens of the emotion.

and everything will remind us of that certain emotion.

Example.

if you’re angry.

you will look out for things to anger you even more.

if you’re sad.

you will look out for things to make you more sad.

YOU REMEMBER THIS.

our brain has a bias, it just confirms your beliefs, and identity.

if your brain has the belief that it is sad, it will look for more things to confirm it.

THIS IS WHY...

when you’re heartbroken, depressed.

you fling on some juice wrld.

because your brain wants to confirm that you’re sad, and make you more sad.

making you view everything through the lens of that emotion.

We Become Hyper Reactive.

because we are unable to bear the tension that this emotion brings.

so we get hyper reactive.

you just drop the plate of food that you was SO excited to eat.

you get so pissed.

so you tell yourself “fuck off, I’m just not going to eat now”

and you’re going to view the world through the lens of anger.

until you actually go and get some food.

This Can Lead To Disaster.

Making a decision when too angry.

or too excited.

this will come back to bite you.

WHEN YOU’RE EXCITED.

your mind will be blind to the potential negatives.

WHEN YOU’RE ANGRY.

your mind will be blind to the potential positives.

This Is Why We Need A Rational Mind.

Especially in these moments when our emotions are inflamed.

so take this step.

install it into your brain.

an emotion will be inflamed sooner or later.

it’s best to keep this info in your mind.

this can save you from disaster.

It’s Like A Manic Episode.

when our emotions are inflamed.

it’s like we feel possessed.

that emotion possesses our vessel, and takes over.

bit like Sukuna from jujutsu kaizen, ifykyk.

WE WILL DEFEAT IT.

to prevent our mind from tunnelling, getting possessed by this emotion.

you will save yourself some hurt.

and save yourself some dignity.

this is how you bring yourself back to reality.

when you are inflamed by an emotion.

proceed when you are ready.

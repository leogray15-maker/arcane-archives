Understand People Cannot Change.

accept people as facts.

interactions with people are the biggest source of emotional change.

but it doesn’t have to be that way…

The Problem Is…

we are ALWAYS judging people.

wishing they were something they are not.

we want to change them.

and act in a certain way to please us.

it’s natural, it’s human nature.

But This Isn’t possible.

because everyone has the same exact mind.

so everyone will always look for other people to act in a way.

to please them.

which means that no one is ever going to be pleased.

and this is why people are the biggest reaction of emotional change.

soo…

what would the logical thing be to do?

ACCEPT PEOPLE AS FACTS.

don’t see them as someone that will please you.

see them as objects (gonna be called sexist for this one)

but you get the point don’t you?

we always will desire for other people to please us.

we want them to act a certain way.

but it’s never going to happen.

so it’s either we start viewing them as objects, as things that can’t change.

or, we will just end up letting people affect our emotions.

because they don’t go the way that WE want them to go.

it’s basically just trapping yourself into feeling the emotion of frustration.

you want “something” from someone that isn’t going to do that “something”

because you’ll always find something that you want to change about them.

they can fix one thing, then you go to find another thing that they change.

and it repeats.

and repeats…

SEE THEM AS EXISTING OBJECTS.

objects that can either enhance your life.

or make it worse.

because they can’t change.

so it’s either you keep the object.

or you get rid of it.

there’s no point keeping it, if it affects you negatively.

this is what makes you “cold hearted” or we can call it stoic.

because realistically.

success, is selfish.

but when you get the money, it’s selfless.

you’ll be tipping waiters left right and centre.

you’ll be giving homeless people money out of nowhere.

it’s an investment, you have to become stoic to succeed.

Stop Trying To Change People, They Will Not.

we’ve all tried to change a hoe.

but they haven’t.

and that’s a recipe for emotional turmoil, for shit to go downhill.

accept them as they are, do not try change them on a deep level.

If People Wanted To Change, They Would’ve.

the human brain wants to avoid pain, correct?

so it’s going to do anything to get rid of it.

and if a person is a certain way.

so no one will EVER change just because “you told them to”

remember that.

don’t save a hoe.

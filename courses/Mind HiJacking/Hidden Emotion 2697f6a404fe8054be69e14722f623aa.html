Hidden Emotion

We all want to prove someone wrong.

Maybe that drives us to this game called entrepreneurship.

We've all been told "you're not going to make it..." "you're wasting your time..." "you won't achieve anything with this..."

Maybe your parents have said to get a normal job and what you're doing isn't going to work out.

We've all experienced this.

This is a pretty universal experience.

And because it's universal, it means we can leverage it into our copy.

Here's what I mean:

Let's call this emotion or trigger, "payback."

We all want to pay them back/prove them wrong.

And this works extremely well in the online money making space.

It's pretty easy to implement:

"Picture the look of surprise on their faces when you earn in a week what they struggle to make in a month."

"Watch them all flock to you asking how you did it when you start making their months wages in a day"

"Put me on, put me on" "Those are the very words you'll be hearing from the same people that doubted your goals"

It's powerful stuff.

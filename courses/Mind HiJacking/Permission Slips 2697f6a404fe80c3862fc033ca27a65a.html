Permission Slips

If you're writing for someone with clear credibility then this will work for you.

Here's why:

People want to do things, they want to think things, they want to say things.

Trump is doing this as well.

He's making it okay to speak the way you want, be the way you want and act the way you want.

Another example is Limbaugh and Howard Stern - used to be the two highest-paid radio people in the world.

They've made more money from radio than anyone else, and they have one thing in common, they issue permission slips that it's okay to think, talk and act in a certain way.

Here's what I mean:

Howard Stern issues permission slips that it's okay, even though you're 46 to think, talk and act like a 14 year old. Go ahead. It's just fine. And for that reason, he has an audience.

To apply it in your copy, you have to be a credible figure and just constantly yell out that it's okay to do certain things the market likes.

If you're a weight loss dietician, then you should be saying it's okay to indulge in every fatty food you can find for a couple of days to restore your hormone levels.

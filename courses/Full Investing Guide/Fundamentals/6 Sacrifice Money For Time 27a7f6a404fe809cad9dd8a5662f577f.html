6. Sacrifice Money For Time.

You now have a life of an entrepreneur.

You don’t really need anything more…

You have good health, you have a good “office”, you even have a personal chauffeur...

What else do you really need?

It’s education.

Specifically courses and mentorships.

You see… imagine this…

Say for example you want to learn more about how to get a clear more present mind.

you can find out who the “king” of mindfulness is… maybe an author who has made a top selling book on mindfulness…

You could simply message them asking if they have a mentorship program where you can talk to them 1-1.

And in just an hour of your time… you could have all the answers you need.

Take any topic that you want to learn about…

You go to one of the top gurus/leaders of that topic and you pay them for a 1-1 coaching session and you literally have saved yourself days of searching…

Really, this is the highest ROI investment, it’s learning.

Listen... here’s a rule of thumb.

ALWAYS SACRIFICE MONEY FOR TIME.

If you want to learn something… you don’t go out looking for resources on how to do it.

You simply just buy a course or a mentorship.

IF IT'S GOING TO KICK OFF, GET IN FIRST.

short one.

but valuable.

this can apply in :

  • fighting

but it can carry over to

  • business
  • normal life situations.

IT'S BETTER TO BE SAFE THAN SORRY.

this is a mindset you always should have.

if you feel like something is about to happen.

and you feel like you know what to do.

trust your gut.

it gives that feeling for a reason.

think about it...

your body has a gut to brain link.

if your gut is telling you something is off, something is off.

ALWAYS trust it.

it's always better to be safe than sorry.

IN FIGHTING.

you don't want to get hit by a random punch from someone.

and be knocked out straight.

if you feel like something is going to kick off.

get in first, use all the advantages you have.

IF YOU FEEL LIKE YOU'RE ABOUT TO GET HIT.

fuck it, trust your gut.

get in first, better to be safe than sorry.

remember.

YOU SHOULD AVOID FIGHTS.

a guy can have a knife, you could die.

but there's just times where you can't run.

you just have to get in first.

IN BUSINESS.

this isn't exactly "get in first"

but this is more a "better to be safe than sorry" thing that happened.

MY SITUATION.

I used to go record a video.

it would be one hour long.

I'd press the finish button on the video.

then go to review my footage.

and...

I'm using my shitty, robotic webcam mic.

FROM THAT MOMENT.

I always double check everything.

it's better to be safe than sorry, remember that.

SUMMARY.

  • IT'S BETTER TO BE SAFE THAN SORRY, ALWAYS.
  • IF SOMETHING IS GOING TO KICK OFF, GET IN FIRST.
  • ALWAYS TRUST YOUR GUT, YOU HAVE THAT FEELING FOR A REASON.

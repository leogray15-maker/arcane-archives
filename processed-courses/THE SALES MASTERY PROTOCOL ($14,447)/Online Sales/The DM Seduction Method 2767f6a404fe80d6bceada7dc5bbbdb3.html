The DM Seduction Method.

If you have ANY form of an audience (from 500-500,000) followers, you can make a boatload of cash.

Before you do this - your profile and content should reflect what you are offering.

Here is what you need:

1. Somebody to DM

These are people that fit within your customer avatar. Considering many of you are doing this on X - make sure you are keeping an eye out for people that fit the bill.

When you post social proof or authority posts, you may receive a few inbound DMs (though this is unlikely if you have a very small audience).

What you can do instead is DM 1-5 people every day that engage with your authority posts. The best people to DM are the ones that retweet or reply, likes work as well but that would take a LOT of DMs.

2. A high ticket offer (or any offer)

  • Coaching
  • Freelancing
  • Consulting
  • Course

If you do not have an offer and are just starting out - that's fine, you should be using these tactics as a way to give out free advice and validate your potential offer and get testimonials.

3. An extremely simple funnel

  • Landing page (optional) - To have social proof and other information before sending them to the questionnaire.
  • Qualifying questionnaire - to filter out qualified from unqualified before you spend time on a call with them.
    • You can use your website software, Jotform, or Google forms for this
  • Scheduling software (I.E. Calendly) - to reach out to them after the questionnaire to get them on a call with you (an upcoming sales training with sales coach Andy Strohm will help you with the call).

THAT’S IT.

No crazy webinar funnels, no ads.

Your entire purpose with DM seduction is to hit some pain points, offer help, and have them schedule a strategy call with you.

It really is that simple.

Don’t think so? Read this:

My buddy Devin McDermott followed this system - talked to someone with a porn addiction (most people) - got him on a call and charged $375 per hour for coaching.

You don't HAVE to have an extremely high ticket offer to do this, but it is better to have multiple offers in place (an offer stack) so you can start high and work your way down for those that can't afford your full blown offer.

The DM Script

Below you will find a script to use to turn random people on the internet into a few thousand dollars (while also transforming their life for the better).

This script is not set in stone. You will have to use your judgment when going through this.

Don’t think of this as a script. Think of it as a 7 Step Framework. Seriously, use your own tone of voice with this. Talk like you are having a conversation over text.

Note : After EVERY SINGLE ONE of these messages - if they do not respond within 24 hours, reply with a check-in message.

Example: “Hey ______, just wanted to bump this up. I’m interested in hearing about those goals.”

Some people won’t respond, this is why follow-ups are necessary .

The real money is made in the follow-ups.

Let’s dive in.

1. Initial Message (Outbound) Hey ______, I noticed you commented on my last post about ______. What was it that resonated with you?

This message can be anything really. Except for “hi,” don’t do that.

If they sent YOU a DM first, reply to their message and transition into the next steps.

Add Firepower To This Step:

  • Send a 1-2 minute video message talking directly to them
  • Send a voice message if possible (unfortunately Twitter doesn't have this yet)

The more you can personalize this message - the better.

2. Transition That’s awesome, I’m glad it could have an impact. I’m curious, what do you do for work? And what are you trying to achieve in (niche industry)?

Here we transition into a qualifying question and finding out where they want to be.

You should be able to tell if they can afford your services based on what they tell you from the first question.

The second question is to understand if you can actually help them accomplish something worthwhile.

3. Details (Comment on their work), how are those goals coming along? They may answer this question early - if that’s the case, move on to the next question.

4. Roadblock (Acknowledge what they said), what's holding you back from getting there quicker? Make them realize they need help so you can position yourself as the solution.

5. Visualize Their Transformation (Acknowledge what they said), where are you looking to take this? Sounds like you have a big vision for this. Again, we are showing them a possibility of a transformation and making them WANT it.

6. Offer Help (Acknowledge), I’ve helped quite a few people get (X results, plug a case study if you have one). I have a few suggestions that can help you get there. You open to take this connection offline and get on the phone soon?

If they say yes...

Awesome, I'll send you over a questionnaire so we don't have to waste a bunch of time on the phone.

7. Send Questionnaire Link

Self-explanatory.

8. Send Calendar Link To Qualified Prospects

Read through the questionnaire answers, ask the prospect more questions if you have to, and use your judgment on whether or not you actually want to take the call.

If you are new to this - take as many calls as you can and just offer free advice. Give them actionable steps to fixing their situation. A few of them might even sign on with you - or at least come back to you when they are ready.

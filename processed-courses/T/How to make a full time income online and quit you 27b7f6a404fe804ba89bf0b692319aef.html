How to make a full time income online and quit your 9-5

How to make a full time income online and quit your 9-5

If you want to make a full time income online and replace your 9-5 job and make up to 10k a month then read all of this.

50% of workers have one thing in common…

They DISLIKE the jobs they have

And not only are work conditions getting worse

People are working more and making less

Here’s your step-by-step escape plan…

1. Skill identification

To make money in life you need to provide value

You need something people will willingly pay you for

Sadly, college doesn’t teach us many monetizable skills

However, you probably have more skills than you think

Here’s how to tell…

Take 20 minutes and write down all of your skills

It could be:

  • Cooking
  • Writing
  • Video editing etc.

List as many as you can

Once you have a list of 20-30

Move onto step number 2

2. Skill validation

Once you have a list of skills

You need to identify which will make you money

How do you do this?

Simple, you validate their value on the open market

Go onto UpWork / Fiverrr and type in the skills you possess

See if there are freelancers with this skill making a solid hourly rate

Any skills earning less than £50 you can disregard

Any skills making more are likely winners

But what if none of your skills qualify?

If you have no highly valuable skills there’s good news

You can learn one!

Plus, you now know how to see which are worth learning

Scan Upwork or Fiverrr and find one that piques your interest

3. Skill development

With your skill identified and validated it’s time to master it

To build competency, identify resources you can use for learning

I suggest:

  • YouTube
  • Skillshare
  • Books

But here’s the thing…

Learning a new skill takes time

Sadly, most people are terrible with their time

To avoid this common issue block off at least 5 hours a week to learn

I know this is a hassle but so is working a job you hate…

4. Active work

Once you’ve developed a moderate degree of proficiency at your skill

It’s time to start making money…

Begin applying to jobs on Upwork or other freelance sites

At first, work for cheap

Building up your portfolio is key

But there’s more…

You’re probably thinking…

“How is this going to make me up to £10k a month”?

At £100 an hour, making £10k a month only requires 100 hours of work

PER MONTH

Much less than the 160 or more you’re doing at your job

For less money I may add…

The best part is that you can work even less…

5. Passive income

To make more and work less you need a team

Start posting jobs on Upwork

Build a list of 3-5 competent freelancers who have the same skill as yourself

Then, apply for new projects and hand off your work

Here’s how this comes together to make £10k/month

Active:

2 projects at £2,500 = £5,000

Passive:

5 projects at £1,000 = £5,000

This is your path to £10k/mo online

And leaving your 9-5 job

TRY THIS AND STAY CONSISTENT

ITS IMPOSSIBLE TO MAKE £0 IF YOU PUT THE EFFORT IN.

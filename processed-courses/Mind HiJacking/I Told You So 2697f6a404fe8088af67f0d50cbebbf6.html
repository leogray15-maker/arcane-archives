I Told You So

This is one of those things where I can't believe it's real, just like the Barnum statements, it's stupid.

We've all heard the phrases of:

"I told you so."

"I'm right"

"Had a feeling this would happen"

There's a very famous psychic astrologer called Jeane Dixon. She predicted the presidency of JFK and his death in the office. She was right.

But here's the catch:

She was on the "I was right" tour immediately.

All her previous predictions about other things were wrong.

If you took the average of right and wrong predictions, it'd be like 90% wrong and 10% right yet one prediction that she got right made everyone else forget about all her wrong previous predictions.

Powerful stuff.

Now the only way you'd use this in your copy is to be using it in your daily emails:

Have this subject like:

"I told you so..."

Then make a list of all the predictions you were right about. Your list won't even care about the wrong predictions - if you made any - because just like Jeane Dixon, they'll be amazed and focused on your winning prediction.

The more you know.

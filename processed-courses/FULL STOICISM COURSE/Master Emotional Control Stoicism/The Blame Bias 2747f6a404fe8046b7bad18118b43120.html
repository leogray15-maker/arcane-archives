The Blame Bias.

we want to learn from mistakes.

why?

because we never want to experience that same shit again.

but why?

to tell the truth.

we don’t like to look closely at what we did wrong.

our inspection is limited when it comes to ourselves.

BUT WHY?

we don’t like to be wrong.

being wrong might mean we got kicked out the tribe.

which means… death.

WE WANT TO AVOID PAIN.

I said this before, we're wired for that.

which means naturally…

what are we going to do?

blame other things.

IN A GAME.

when you died in a game of fornite or some shit.

you’re gonna blame it on lag.

“if only I had enough mats”

“If only I had a better shotgun”

“if only I had a better landing spot”

IT’S NEVER YOUR FAULT

it’s never…

“I should’ve got more mats”

“I should’ve tried to find a better shotgun”

“I should have chose a better landing spot”

UNLESS YOU TRAIN TO GET RID OF THIS BIAS.

it will not go away.

and the way to train it.

is obviously, to practice.

LEARN FROM YOUR FAILURES, ACCEPT YOU ARE NOT PERFECT.

you will always make mistakes.

and there’s no point blaming something else.

ever.

IF YOU DO.

you’re retarded.

you just missed a chance to improve.

if only you’d drop the ego.

and make a change.

MORAL FOR THE BLAME BIAS.

never blame anything.

if you blame something.

you’ve just missed a chance to improve on something.

just because you couldn’t handle the fact that you did something wrong.

pussy, own up like a man.

and improve.

practice this.

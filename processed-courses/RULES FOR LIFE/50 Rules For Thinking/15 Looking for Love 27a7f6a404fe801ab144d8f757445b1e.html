15. Looking for Love

When you're searching for love, especially if you've been rejected or experienced unrequited love, the most important thing is to start by loving yourself first. It might sound strange, but if you don't already love yourself, doing something to improve yourself will ALWAYS help. Hit the gym, pursue a business, or even change up your appearance. Do whatever it takes to focus on PROGRESSING . This shift helps you stop looking for love from others, and instead, start finding it from within .

Here's the secret: People often wait to love themselves until they feel loved by others. But, the world is a mirror— you can’t expect love to come from others if you don’t have love inside you . This creates a vicious cycle where you keep waiting for love to come from the outside, but the reflection can't emit love if the image doesn’t have it.

The way to break this cycle is simple: Start making progress, and you will feel upmost confident knowing you're going somewhere.

Love is like a boomerang. When you send it out to the world, it comes back to you.

How does it work? It’s like this:

  • You send love into the world. (from your inner happiness from progress)
  • The world reflects that love and sends it back to you.
  • You receive love from others.
  • You feel great about it and start loving yourself even more.

And just like that, the cycle of love begins, and you find yourself feeling great, confident, and connected. So, remember: The more love you send out into the world, the more it will come back to you —starting with progress.

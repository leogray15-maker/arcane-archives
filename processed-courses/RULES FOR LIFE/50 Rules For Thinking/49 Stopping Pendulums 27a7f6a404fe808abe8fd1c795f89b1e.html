49. Stopping Pendulums

Stopping Pendulums is a crucial concept in Transurfing, emphasizing how to protect your energy and prevent negative forces (pendulums) from draining you. Pendulums thrive on negativity and lower emotional states, and by recognizing their presence and choosing how you respond, you can break their grip on you.

Declaration:

When you decide to stop pendulums from feeding on your energy, be prepared for provocations. Pendulums thrive on negative energy, so when you face a difficult or unpleasant situation, you have two choices: lose your balance or raise your vibrations . Pendulums feed on the negative energy that arises when you react with anger, frustration, or fear. By raising your vibration —responding with gratitude and peace —you prevent the pendulum from attaching to you.

Your energy will be shielded the higher your vibration, making it harder for pendulums to latch on. In other words, the key to defeating pendulums is changing your reaction to negative situations. Respond with positivity, gratitude, and love, and you will be able to avoid feeding them.

Interpretation:

Pendulums are energy-based structures that vibrate at specific frequencies, and they can only attach to you when you are vibrating at the same frequency . When you react negatively to something, you align your vibration with theirs, which gives them power over you . Negativity essentially matches their frequency , allowing them to "feed" off your energy.

However, if you react with positivity and love , you vibrate at a much higher frequency . This dissonance (the difference between your vibration and the pendulum’s frequency) prevents the pendulum from attaching to you. Your energy remains safe , and you maintain your power.

To win the battle against pendulums , it’s important to change the scenario of the game : instead of reacting negatively, reach positively when confronted with challenges. This act of shifting the energy of the situation is key to maintaining your sovereignty over your energy.

The Energy Dynamics of Pendulums:

  • Pendulums feed on the energy of negative emotions . Every time you express negative feelings (like anger, fear, frustration), you are fueling them. They latch onto you, draining your energy and creating a sense of depletion.
  • When many people unite with strong negative emotions, such as in moments of collective conflict (like during a war), they start to emit huge waves of negative energy . This can harm the planet , but most of that negative energy is absorbed by pendulums . Pendulums act as a balancing force , preventing the full impact of this energy from reaching the Universe. However, the people emitting the negative energy are the ones who suffer, as the pendulums drain their vitality.
  • Pendulums balance the negative energy they feed on by absorbing it and preventing it from harming the Universe. But the individuals whose energy is being consumed suffer from feelings of depression, exhaustion, and powerlessness because they are the ones who are emotionally affected and drained.

Practical Steps to Stop Pendulums:

  1. Recognize the Pendulums : Understand that whenever you are experiencing strong negative emotions (anger, frustration, fear, etc.), it is likely that a pendulum is trying to attach to you. Simply becoming aware of this is the first step to breaking free from its influence.
  1. Raise Your Vibration : The key to stopping pendulums is to shift your emotional state . When you feel triggered, instead of reacting negatively, consciously choose to respond with gratitude, love, or a peaceful mindset. Even if it feels difficult, focusing on positive thoughts can shift your vibration and shield you from the pendulum .
  1. Practice Positive Reactions : Every time you face an unpleasant situation, respond with positivity . For example, if you feel angry or upset, pause and try to find something to be grateful for. If you feel stressed, take a deep breath and remind yourself that you can handle the situation. Changing the scenario in your mind to one of gratitude or love helps you disconnect from the pendulum's influence .
  1. Don’t Let Negative Thoughts Grow : If you notice yourself spiraling into negativity, catch it early and shift your thoughts. Negative thoughts can quickly build momentum, but if you change the direction of your thoughts, you can prevent the pendulum from attaching to you in the first place.
  1. Strengthen Your Emotional Awareness : The more you become aware of your emotions and how they affect your energy, the easier it becomes to stay balanced. Meditation, mindfulness, and self-reflection can help you stay in tune with your emotional state and raise your vibration consistently .
  1. Stay Grateful : In moments of difficulty, make a practice of finding something you can be grateful for . Gratitude immediately shifts your energy and raises your vibration, making it harder for pendulums to latch on to you.
  1. Trust in Your Power : Remember, you are always in control of how you react to situations. Do not give your power away to pendulums by allowing negative emotions to take over. Instead, focus on maintaining your peace and joy regardless of external circumstances.

Key Takeaways:

  • Pendulums feed on negative energy , and when you react negatively, they can attach to you and drain your energy.
  • The key to stopping pendulums is to raise your vibration by responding to negative situations with positivity , gratitude , and love .
  • Recognize the pendulums in your life and consciously choose not to react to them with negativity. Instead, change the scenario in your mind to something positive.
  • By maintaining a high frequency of positive emotions, you shield yourself from pendulums and prevent them from draining your energy.

In Transurfing, your emotional state is your most powerful tool . The higher your vibration, the less susceptible you are to external forces that seek to pull you into negativity. By practicing awareness and positive emotional responses , you can protect your energy and stay aligned with the goals and desires that are truly important to you.

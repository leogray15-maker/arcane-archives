16. Belief

When we talk about Belief , it's about understanding that you are the creator of your own world, and your beliefs shape the reality around you. The key here is that belief is not something you force or try to convince yourself of. Instead, it’s a result of living your truth and applying progress in your daily life. When you begin to truly understand the principles of progress, the concept of belief shifts. It's no longer about trying to hold on to hope or faith in something abstract—because, as you practice, you will know that what once seemed impossible is now possible. You don’t need to “believe” anymore; you’ll know that you have control over your life.

and all it takes is steps daily to achieve it.

It’s also important to realize that failure is an integral part of this process. Every successful person has faced moments of struggle or doubt; they’ve encountered setbacks and mistakes, but they didn't stop. Instead, they took those failures as stepping stones toward success. The journey is filled with lessons, and when you encounter challenges, it’s actually a sign that you’re on the path to growth.

Hope, in the traditional sense, is about waiting for something to happen. But as you move forward with the principles of progress, you start to see that action is what creates change. Action, not belief, is the fuel that powers your progress. And with this action, hope transforms into certainty. You start to understand that it’s no longer about wishing for success or wondering if it’ll happen. It’s about having the confidence that your intentions, when aligned with the energy of the universe, will create the reality you desire.

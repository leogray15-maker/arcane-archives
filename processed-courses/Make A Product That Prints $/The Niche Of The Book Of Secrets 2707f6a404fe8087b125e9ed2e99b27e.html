The Niche Of The "Book Of Secrets"

Stop focusing on your niche.

Start focusing on who you can help the most.

Stop trying to find a specific person to sell to.

Start helping people who share the same goal as you.

Here's the thing.

Everyone has the same goals.

They want to make more money, be more productive, have more sex, cultivate more connection, be more healthy, and manage their emotions better.

Everyone's problems, goals, and desires fall under the eternal markets: health, wealth, relationships, and happiness.

and you know what the "crazy" thing is?

the reason that you learn the things you are interested in...

it's because they fall into the categories of "health, wealth, relationships, happiness, power"

or in other words... you are learning everything for a REASON.

fall back to the "first principles" video by charlie morgan, he talks about how each principle helps in business.

In brief, that's what marketing is.

It is showing your potential customer how your program and the skill and interests you're teaching can help them improve in those areas.

we'll go over that later in the marketing, don't stress it.

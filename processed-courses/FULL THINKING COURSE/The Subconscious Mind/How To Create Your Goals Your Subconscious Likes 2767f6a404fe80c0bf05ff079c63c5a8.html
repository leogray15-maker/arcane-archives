How To Create Your Goals Your Subconscious Likes

How To Create Your Goals Your Subconscious Likes By Angelo

— Deep in your mind, there are two processes that take place:

  1. Transitional
  1. Fixed (Finality) When you set goals, they tend to be FINAL.

“I want to become a millionaire”

“I want to build a business”

“I want to travel”

That’s a dead end.

When something stops growing and moving.

It dies.

How can your subconscious mind help you die?

Exactly, it doesn’t hence why you don’t achieve the goals you set out to because there’s a dead end that comes after it.

So, here’s how you properly do it:

Every goal leading to another goal.

Fixed goal: “I want to be a millionaire” “I am a millionaire”

This isn’t good.

Transitional goal:

“Once I become a millionaire, I will (next goal), then I will (next goal), and then I will decide what to do after that.”

THE HOW TO:

Example: Once I become a millionaire, I will become a billionaire, then I will become a trillionaire, and then I will decide what to do after that.

Must-know words

Must-know words that make almost any claim you make believable.

One of the all-time great copywriting books is Eugene Schwartz's classic Breakthrough Advertising. There are hundreds of tips and insights inside that book.

One of the lessons mentioned inside that book is called:

"The language of logic"

This is where you pepper words and language that have "built-in" credibility throughout your ad, where it makes sense and sounds natural.

They sound very logical and intelligent. And are often used in courts, scientific circles, legal arguments, etc.

It's like having thousands of years of linguistic credibility rubbing off on your advertising.

Below I've put together a list of these kinds of words and phrases I compiled a few months ago. It's not exhaustive by any means. But it serves as a starting point. To add to it, pay attention to court, academic, and scientific language, and see if you can't work even more such words and phrases into your emails.

Another one I like to use and you might have seen me use is:

"Logically speaking"

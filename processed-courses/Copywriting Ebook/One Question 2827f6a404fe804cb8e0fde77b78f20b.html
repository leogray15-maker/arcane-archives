One Question

The ONE question you need to ask every cashier if you want to be successful in this game. (If you don't ask this question at least once a day, your chances of success drastically decreases).

To be successful in any business model, you'll face rejection after rejection.

In fact, I've faced more rejection than I have received acceptance.

This is primarily one of the reasons people fail, the rejection eventually gets to them.

At the beginning, people face rejection and they keep going with a mindset of "it's part of the process"...

But as time goes on, each additional rejection start chipping at them from the inside out until they've completely given up.

I've seen it countless times

But there is an easier way of building your tolerance for rejection and anyone can do it, starting today...

And it's asking one simple question.

This question is:

"Can I get 10% off?"

I know, I know, it doesn't sound "sexy" or "powerful" at all but don't hate on it until you've tried it.

Ask this question whenever you get the opportunity.

9 out of 10 times you will experience rejection and this is good because it's building your tolerance for rejection whilst also building your confidence.

If you ask this question at least once everyday, I guarantee you in 1-3 months...

You'll be immune to rejection.

So much so that rejection won't even phase you. You'll realise it's just a momentary obstacle, a minor bump in the road.

Now this is probably one of the easiest ways of developing your mindset.

You're getting endless benefits for a couple of seconds to a minute of embarrassment.

That embarrassment can one day turn into confidence.

Logically speaking, it's a win-win situation.

Thanks for reading.

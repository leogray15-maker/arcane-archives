I Vs Us Language

Something is stopping you from doing what you want.

The ONLY thing that can stop you from doing ANYTHING is yourself.

If your present self wants the goal, yet you are stopping yourself or limiting yourself?

Then WHO is stopping or limiting you?

The answer is: A version of you that's a different age.

This is where inner child exercises come.

I'm sure you've heard of it if you're interested in psychology.

In short: We have multiple versions of ourself synthesised in our mind. An 8 year old you, a 13 year old you, a 15 year old you, etc…

There’s a saying that goes: the adult grows up to protect the child but that is singular.

It’s actually: The adults grow up to protect the childs.

This is plural.

You get the idea.

And when you're facing resistance, it's because one of the versions in your mind doesn't WANT to do the task — hence creating resistance.

The way to go around this is changing your language pattern and bringing everyone on the same team.

Normies use the language pattern of "I will" "I can", etc...

If you use "I will" then you amplify PRESENT limitation and lack "I" "I am" "I will" "I can"

The problem with this is that the "I" of PRESENT only works where as it starts to generate CONFLICT with the other "I's" The solution to this is to simply change the "I" to:

"Let us"

"We"

This is a powerful phrase as there are many of YOU there.

Let = command to Us = for us all It's a great mistake thinking that "I" is alone and one.

This is the greatest obstacle to change.

So change your language:

Let us learn.

Let us grow.

Let us become billionaires.

Let us be.

As children, we used to say "LET ME GO OUTSIDE"

See how powerful that is?

Obviously if you're in a group setting, it'd sound corny/cringe so just start correcting yourself and saying it to yourself in your mind.

And to those that are heavy on meditation, here's a good visualisation meditation exercise you can do:

Imagine all the little versions of you coming together in a circle with the current you being in the centre.

Something like this:

And just feel the energy.

Repeat to yourself during the exercise:

"Let us (task)"

"Let us write good copy"

"Let us close this sale"

"Let us train hard"

You get the idea.

This exercise is not necessary.

Think of it as the icing on the cake.

Remember, the reason this works is because the subconscious stores everything.

That also means it stores every single part of you from when you were 1 year old to your current age and it will continue to do so.

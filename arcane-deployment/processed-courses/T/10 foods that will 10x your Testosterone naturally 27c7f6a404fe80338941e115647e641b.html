10 foods that will 10x your Testosterone naturally:

1. Honey: Honey contains boron which is a natural mineral that can be found in both foods and in the environment .  It is associated with helping to increase testosterone levels and is also helpful for building strong bones and for building muscles.

2. Salmon: Good quality wild salmon is an excellent addition to the list of testosterone boosting foods because it contains magnesium, vitamin B and omega-3s which increase testosterone levels.

3. Garlic: Garlic contains a compound called allicin which can be useful for lowering your cortisol levels.  Cortisol is produced in the adrenal gland, which is where testosterone is produced.

4. Eggs: Eggs are a fantastic source of protein, cholesterol, vitamin D and omega-3s, all of which aid in the production of testosterone.  The protein in them also helps with muscle building.

5. Almonds: Almonds contain high levels of the mineral zinc which is known to raise testosterone levels in people who are zinc deficient.  If you are low in zinc this could stop the pituitary gland from releasing some of the key hormones for stimulating testosterone production.

6. Oysters:  Oysters are commonly known as an aphrodisiac and there’s actually science behind this.  Testosterone increases your libido and oysters are naturally high in zinc and thus they help in raising testosterone levels.

7. Spinach:  It has long been considered one of the best testosterone boosting foods around.  One of the best super foods, it was Popeye’s favorite food for a reason!  Spinach is a natural source of magnesium which has been shown to correlate positively with testosterone levels.

8. Tuna:  Tuna is an excellent source of Vitamin D which, according to researchers at Graz Medical University in Austria, can help boost testosterone levels by up to 90%.  Vitamin D helps to maintain sperm count and tuna is an excellent way to get this particular vitamin.

9. Bananas:  Bananas contain an enzyme called bromelain which is known to help boost testosterone levels.  Bananas are also excellent for maintaining energy levels and reducing antioxidants so make the perfect on the go snack.

10. Porridge Oats: Porridge oats are an excellent source of B Vitamins which are key for good testosterone production.   There are a number of different B Vitamins, many of which are found in testosterone boosting foods.

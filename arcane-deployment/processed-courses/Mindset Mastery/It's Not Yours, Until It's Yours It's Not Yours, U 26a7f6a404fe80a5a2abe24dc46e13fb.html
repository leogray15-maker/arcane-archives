It's Not Yours, Until It's Yours.

It's Not Yours, Until It's Yours.

this one really made me more stoic...

this one will make you look at situations in a way more logical way.

and not based on emotion.

EXAMPLES.

it's not your money, until it's visible in your bank.

it's not your girl, until you've locked her down.

you've not got a date, until she's showed up.

that event isn't going to happen, until it's happening.

ELIMINATE ALL EXCITEMENT.

if someone says they're going to buy your product.

don't get excited.

the money isn't in your bank yet.

If a girl says she'll go on a date with you.

don't get excited.

it's not happening yet.

if you're really excited for a social event.

don't be.

it's not happening yet.

HOW I DEVELOPED THIS MINDSET.

before everything, I was doing SMMA.

an agency.

and I started working on it.

I started sending emails daily.

this was my routine

  • go college (but skip half the lessons.)
  • go home and send emails
  • go gym
  • send emails.
  • sleep.

the monk mode state.

I'm telling my one friend about it on discord.

"look at this business ect it's gonna make me rich."

days pass.

and one day, I'm in the gym.

and I get a text message, from a doctor.

but it wasn't my doctor.

it was one of my potential clients.

arranging a sales meeting.

I was so gassed, now the remaining sets in the gym, they go harder.

I felt like this business man.

in the gym, and texting back doctors for a sales meeting.

at 16 years old.

I was so nervous but excited.

maybe this was the way I'd escape this "matrix”

I'm gonna FINALLY start making 2k a month from this client.

I get home, the meeting is for tomorrow afterschool.

I go home, tell my mum I have a sales meeting don't disturb me.

this will make me able to drop out.

she’s proud and excited for me.

I make a PowerPoint very quick to screenshare for him.

I get on it, so nervous.

I was so clueless.

and then I offered him to pay me 2k a month on ads.

and of course he said "I'll think about it"

because that's the polite version of saying "nah your offer is shit"

but now.

I have to go tell my mum that my sales meeting didn't work.

do the walk of shame downstairs to tell her.

she says “it's ok maybe get a normal job yeah yeah.”

trying to comfort me.

it broke me as a 16 year old kid.

  • demotivated.
  • feeling empty.
  • nihilistic.

like there was no hope left.

the worst possible feeling for a man.

WHY DID THIS HAPPEN?

I got excited.

when the money wasn't even in my pocket.

THIS IS HOW YOU BECOME STOIC.

when you have huge expectations.

"she's going to show up to the date no matter what"

"that guy is definitely going to buy"

and when that doesn't happen....

YOUR FEELINGS GETS FUCKED.

if you expect something to happen.

then there's a last minute cancellation.

  • the girl texts "can we rearrange?"
  • the sales prospect says "ill think about it"

your dopamine is going to PLUMMET.

for the rest of your day, you will be depressed.

you'll be way more likely to go and eat some carbs, just because you want to get away.

BUT IF YOU BECAME STOIC.

if you implemented this mindset of "it's not yours, until it's yours"

YOU HAVE NO EXPECTATIONS.

you become a realist.

you start to see things as how they are.

you get excited when you ACTUALLY receive the money.

you get a sense of excitement when she ACTUALLY says she's getting ready to leave for the date.

THIS IS HOW A MAN THINKS.

a woman gets excited.

a man thinks with logic.

BECOME A REALIST.

excitement should not be a thing, unless it is certain.

so let me give you an actionable step, for you to implement this mindset.

ACTIONABLE STEP.

RN, THINK OF A TIME WHERE YOU GOT EXCITED.

then the thing cancels.

and that excitement is gone.

now you're left feeling depressed.

this is a REAL chemical thing, Andrew Huberman talks about it.

You've probably got something in your mind.

you got excited for something to happen

  • "I'm gonna get rich"
  • "this post is going to blow up"
  • "he's going to buy"

and it never happened.

think of that.

FROM NOW ON, YOU'RE A REALIST.

you think in logic, and not in emotion.

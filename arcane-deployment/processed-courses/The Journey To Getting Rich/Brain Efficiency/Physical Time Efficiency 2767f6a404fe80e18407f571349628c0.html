Physical Time Efficiency

Buy Time:

this is the 80/20 of time efficiency, and I cover it in the investment course.

and it is EASILY the best way to spend your money, apart from diet.

anything to save time, is efficiency.

getting an uber instead of taking a bus or walking.

paying family members to clean your room (i do this, with my sister, quite a hassle, but it works)

get groceries delivered, i order my water to my door

just think of buying time, that's the way to go.

and also, stack tasks.

1stman talked about this.

it's "habit stacking"

i made a tweet on it very long ago.

basically, whenever something passive is happening, you do something active.

when you're on a walk, put on a podcast.

when you're waiting for a meal to be cooked in the oven, go and write a module.

simple things, these are the 80/20.

How To Become A Winner.

when you decide to become a winner it takes a while for you to actually become a winner… so it’s hard to keep acting like a winner because you’re not a real winner yet.

It’s a tongue twister, but you should get it…

Success is easy, it’s just about acting like the winner who does work and has an iron mindset.

But here’s the thing.

How can you become the “winner” when you’re not the winner yet?

All the odds are against you.

Your family is going to treat you like your past self.

Your friends are going to treat you like your past self.

So you’re going to revert to your past self if you stay with them.

I mean… imagine this.

Imagine you went to live in another country tomorrow.

you new people you meet there… how will you act?

Will it be different from the way you act towards your friends and family back in your hometown? Probably.

That’s because the “new” people that you will meet in the new country have only seen this “winner” side of you.

And that’s the personality that they SEE you as, they don't know anything about your "past self"

it's like moving to a new school and using a new personality... that "new personality" that you use will eventually become your main one.

But the people back home…?

they have seen your past self...

They will still treat you like you are just “yourself” because you're not a "winner" yet, so they won't treat you like one... meaning it will be almost impossible to actually act like one.

Don’t blame them, because it is human nature to act like this… but if you want to move to the next level you have to make people see you as the next level.

So what am I trying to say here?

If you really want to move onto the next level in life and craft your ideal character, you can’t have other people treating you as your NON ideal character.

You’ll start working on a business and staying locked in your room grinding… your friends will say “oh you’ve changed”…

But if you went somewhere else and other people know YOU as the grinder, they will treat you like that and act as if you're just the guy who works and loves business.

The way people treat you will switch your identity, and there you’re crafting your ideal character that you’ve always wanted to be.

But of course… here’s the thing.

You want to do this.. but you can’t move country.

So really what you need to do is go into a dark grind period.

The winter is perfect for this…

You can make excuses to stay in and just grind with your new identity.

Imagine 6 months straight of that..

Then you come out and people around you will visibly tell that you have changed.

They’ll say “you’ve changed”… but of course you have, why would you NOT want to change?

So here’s the main message…

You need to change others peoples perception of you, because it’s keeping you in your “old” identity and broke.

You’ll start to work on a business then realise that you’re not that “businessman”, you’re just a guy who’s trying to do it.

But if you start to see yourself as the businessman who works on his business rather than “trying” to.

You’ve already became a winner in your mind, so you just have to keep that up UNTIL your life can show for that.

But this is hard because when you decide to become a winner it takes a while for you to actually become a winner… so it’s hard to keep acting like a winner because you’re not a real winner yet.

So you just need to force yourself to act like a winner and make that identity switch… don’t talk to people that will keep your “past self” around, and just act in accordance to the person you want to be.

You can do this by constantly just listening to people that kind of "enforce" your new identity, even if you just literally listen to old school seminars, etc.

do that for long enough....

Then when you finally become that winner, everyone will start seeing you as one and it’s easy from there.

you just need to go in a dark grind selfish period until you become a winner in the "real" world, then you will actually enjoy your life.

How To Never Quit.

SELF ACCOUNTABILITY-NEVER QUIT.

we know accountability is good... it will make you stay on the right path.

but however...

when you are grinding at night, alone, thinking this isn't working.

its not as nice as it seems.

but here is what will keep you on track...

THIS SKILL ALONE IS WORTH £1 MILLION OVER YOUR LIFE.

imagine all the times you never quit the gym, never quit that business, where would you be now.

let me solve that for you.

HOW TO DO IT.

you need 2 things.

a pen and a notebook/ journal.

this does not work on a notion document on your pc.

I've tried both because one seemed more convenient.

but pen and paper, old fashioned way.

why?

you can type so quick on a pc, but on a piece of paper you can only write so quick.

which gives you time to think to yourself more.

AT THE END OF THE DAY/ WHENEVER YOU FEEL LIKE QUITTING.

I like to do this thing called reassurance journaling.

combine this with meditation and mindfulness and you become a superhuman in any endeavour.

STEPS.

firstly... you need to get your mindset right.

start reading the mindset courses, and program your brain for success, THEN you can do this.

STEP 1. identify your beliefs, you know you want to quit, realise that, now realise why you want to quit.

this is important because it also shows self humility, you will become humble and realise you are not this god.

everyone gets the feeling that it's not gonna work out.

STEP 2. WRITE THE EXACT THOUGHTS THAT YOU ARE HAVING ABOUT IT FAILING/QUITTING.

promise me you do this on a notebook or a journal or a piece of paper.

you write down the thoughts that you are having.

"I feel like quitting because one tiktok got 1k views instead of blowing up"

I genuinely had this belief one time with the tiktok page.

now you've wrote it down, you start thinking midway writing it that you are a retard.

you start coaching yourself, as if you was talking to somebody who has told you this problem.

its like one good part of your brain is fighting the bad part of your brain.

and the good part always comes victorious.

reassurance journaling.

never quit.

SECOND USE FOR JOURNALLING.

whenever you have a decision to make.

or you just feel like nothing is going your way.

just get a piece of paper out.

write as if you are an older, more wise version of yourself.

give advice to yourself rn.

"what would he say to me in this situation rn?""

mentor yourself.

REASSURANCE JOURNALLING.

simply talk to yourself in your journal, has to be on paper.

you just talk to yourself at the end of your day, acknowledge your accomplishments.

tell yourself that you will do the same tomorrow.

journal about the vision.

grind.

SUMMARY

WHEN YOU FEEL LIKE QUITTING

  • REALISE THE THOUGHTS
  • WRITE THEM DOWN
  • COUNTERACT THEM

and reap the benefits.

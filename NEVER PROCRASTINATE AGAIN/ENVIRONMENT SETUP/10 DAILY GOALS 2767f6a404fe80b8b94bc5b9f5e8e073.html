10 DAILY GOALS.

this is a gem I got told was from a Capital Club course.

From Brian Tracy.

SET 10 DAILY GOALS.

10 things.

these 10 things should compound into something great.

for example.

"go to the gym"

"do 1 hour of deep work"

"research marketing"

these things work like magic

THE HUMAN BRAIN PHSYCOLOGY.

we was born into this world with one goal.

SURVIVE.

and surviving has a lot of different goals inside that.

  • eat food
  • drink water
  • sleep
  • defend

does this remind you of something?

EVERYTHING IN LIFE.

has sub-goals.

GETTING RICH.

  • work
  • research
  • interact

GETTING RIPPED.

  • go to the gym
  • eat clean
  • research

and these sub goals also have sub goals.

E.G

getting ripped

you have to go to the gym.

but you have to do the benchpress.

but with the benchpress you need the right form.

with the right form you need to research the right form.

it all comes down to goals.

EVERYTHING IN LIFE DOES.

you watch porn, play video games, watch content with the goal of getting away from your problems.

you try to get that girl because you want to feel happy and fulfilled.

you want to be happy and fulfilled so you don't kill yourself any time soon.

right?

always comes down to goals.

SO LETS TAKE A BIG GOAL.

getting rich is your main one.

you identify the things you need to do every single day to be rich in a matter of time.

and its :

  • work
  • research
  • interact.

so you fit that into your 10 daily goals.

then you think of the other goals you have to achieve one big goal.

you want to get ripped.

  • go to the gym
  • eat clean
  • drink a lot of water

now you should add that into your daily goals.

AND WITH ANYTHING ELSE YOU WANT TO ACHIEVE.

WRITE 10 DAILY GOALS SOMEWHERE WITH A BOX TO TICK.

every, single, day.

for the rest of your life.

and there is NO way you don't become successful.

HERE ARE SOME THINGS THIS IS GOOD FOR.

ACCOUNTABILITY.

YOUR BRAIN HATES UNTICKED BOXES.

if you see an unticked box when you're going to sleep at night, you're not going to be able to sleep properly.

you're going to want to get that shit done, because it's survival instinct.

it's literally a FREE 10x productivity boost.

DIRECTION.

instead of wasting brain points in your day.

you wake up with purpose.

with something you KNOW you're going to put brain points into.

you walk around life with direction.

instead of walking like a pussy thinking "where should I go next?"

you have PURPOSE, you have DIRECTION.

your brain is for coming up with thoughts, not storing them.

you will have something to do, then forget 5 seconds later because you had another thought.

then at night you remember.

then you can't do it.

because it's bedtime.

the daily goals solve this.

you can go sleep fulfilled, and it's such a fucking good feeling ticking all the boxes off.

YOU BECOME OBSESSED WITH TICKING BOXES.

this is just such a simple practice, but will pay you dividends.

46. Doors

Doors in Transurfing are symbolic of the different pathways that lead to the achievement of your goal. These doors represent the opportunities that present themselves as you move through life. The key principle here is that the path to your goal is not a struggle, but one of ease, flow, and alignment with your internal state. When you feel good, happy, and energized, you are walking through the right door that leads to your goal. However, when you feel negative emotions, frustration, or sadness, you are not aligned with your correct pathway.

you will see this as you fall off.

when you have bad emotions plaguing you, you open the door to the "numbing out" lane, and you sit there.

when your emotions are back up, you open the next door to the next opportunity for your goal.

the key is to keep the good emotions, and focus on the good.

that way you are always walking through the right doors.

Declaration:

The concept of "doors" suggests that as you mentally slide into the picture of your goal, opportunities will present themselves. The Universe will show you doors—pathways—that can lead you to your goal. However, not all doors are right for you. Feeling good, happy, and full of energy indicates that you are walking through the right door. If you feel frustrated or upset, you are likely moving through a path that isn’t aligned with your true self, and thus, it will cost you more energy.

The right door for you is always one where you feel good and grateful, which is an indication that you are aligned with the right life line. Happiness and gratitude are the keys to unlocking the door that leads to your goal. If something doesn’t feel right, don't force it—simply recognize that it isn’t your door and find another pathway.

it's like shopping for food when you're hungry, you will make bad decisions.

this is why you don't make business decisions straight away.

this is why you don't publish headlines straight away.

you sleep on it, and look at it with a clear mind.

Key Points:

  1. Your door is your path to the goal: Each door represents a potential way to reach your goal, but not all are the right ones. If a door feels bad, don’t force it—find another path.
  1. How you feel determines your path: Your emotional state is the best indicator of whether you are on the right track. If you feel happy, energized, and full of joy, you are on the right life line. If you feel frustrated, sad, or angry, you are likely on a path that will require more effort, energy loss, and negative feelings.
  1. There are many doors: Don't limit yourself by thinking there is only one way to reach your goal. There are multiple doors that lead to your desired outcome, so if one path doesn’t work, simply try another.
  1. Ignore pendulums: Pendulums (external influences like societal pressure, expectations, or negative emotions) want you to believe that you must struggle to achieve your goal. The truth is, you don't need to fight them. Just ignore them. The moment you stop playing into their energy, you can focus on your path and your goal.
  1. Happiness first: Instead of following the common belief that you must work hard, struggle, and sacrifice before you can achieve happiness, the opposite is true. When you focus on feeling happy and content now, the Universe aligns with you and helps manifest your goals. Happiness brings abundance, including money, opportunities, and fulfillment.
  1. Living for yourself: The key is to live for yourself, aligning your life with your own goals and desires, not the societal stereotypes or expectations placed on you. Your goal will bring everything you need, including money and success. Happiness, not hard work, brings the best lines of life.

Interpretation:

  • Trust your feelings: Your emotions are an indicator of whether you’re on the right path. If you’re aligned with your true self, the journey will feel easy and effortless. If it feels wrong, then it’s time to look for another door.
  • Do not struggle: Don’t fall into the trap of thinking that you have to suffer or work hard to succeed. You don’t need to fight; just choose paths that feel good, and the Universe will help guide you toward success. work can feel hard, but good at the same time, look for that.
  • There are countless opportunities: Your goal is not limited by a single path or door. There are always many ways to reach your goal, and sometimes the easiest way is to let go of the need to control and allow things to flow naturally.

Practical Steps:

  1. Pay attention to how you feel: Start noticing your emotional state throughout the day. When you feel good and energized, you are on the right path.
  1. Don’t force things: If something feels wrong or if you feel frustration, acknowledge it as a sign that you may be on the wrong path. Switch your focus and find a different direction.
  1. Live for yourself: Make decisions based on what feels right for you, not based on external pressures or societal expectations. Your happiness is the key to success.
  1. Trust in the Universe: Believe that there are multiple doors to your goal and that everything will work out if you align yourself with joy, gratitude, and positive energy.

Summary:

"Doors" represent the opportunities available to you on your path to success. The right door is one where you feel good, energized, and aligned with your goal. By ignoring pendulums and focusing on what makes you happy, you will naturally move towards the doors that will lead you to your goal. Keep in mind that there are many doors, and when one doesn’t feel right, simply move to the next one. Your journey should feel fulfilling. The Universe will bring everything you need as long as you stay true to yourself and remain in a state of happiness and gratitude, and while you take steps towards your goal, you will achieve it.

always open doors when you're in a state of high vibration, never low.

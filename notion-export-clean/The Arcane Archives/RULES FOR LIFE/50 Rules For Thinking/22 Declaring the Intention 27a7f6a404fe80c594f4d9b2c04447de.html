22. Declaring the Intention

Declaration:

To control your reality, the first step is to control your thoughts. Your thoughts shape your world, so it’s vital that you declare your intentions to the Universe with clarity. Set a clear goal and focus on it, but here’s the crucial point: don’t obsess over it. Focusing on your goal 24/7 will create resistance. Instead, focus on your goal, then consciously allow your mind to "sleep," knowing that you can always return to focusing on your goal at any moment. Controlling your mind may feel difficult, but it is necessary. You must train yourself to master this skill, for the betterment of your life.

Interpretation:

Thoughts are constantly coming and going. Most of what we think about has no direct impact on our reality; we think because it's a habit. However, if you truly want to see quick changes in your life, you must learn to control your thoughts. Your thoughts are powerful tools that can direct the course of your reality, so it’s important to consciously choose which thoughts to focus on. To begin, declare your intentions clearly. When you state your goals aloud, you affirm them to yourself and the Universe, anchoring them in your reality.

This declaration trains your mind to focus on your goals with precision, while also activating your brain’s potential to its fullest capacity. Over time, this will help you to solve problems more easily and approach life with greater clarity and purpose.

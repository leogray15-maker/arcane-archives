(Who's Who In Your Story)

The Enemy (antagonist):

  • what's blocking success
  • their current problem

The Identity (who they become):

  • who your customer relates to
  • who they see themselves as

The Decider (climax character):

  • who determines how it ends
  • usually your customer (they must take action)

Decider: "you choosing to finally prioritize yourself"

(no characters)

THE MILTON MODEL MASTERY

milton erickson hypnotized without hypnosis.

his secret? presumptive language patterns.

pattern 1: awareness predicates "you might notice..." "you'll discover..." "you're becoming aware..."

assumes they WILL experience it.

and you’ve probably seen this studying copy..

the “you might notice…” or the “inside you’ll discover”…

there’s layers to this one, this says “there’s hidden bonuses in here” which activates a bit of the primal brain… there might be fish in that lake… you get it.

pattern 2: temporal shifts "after you've finished..." "once you've decided..." "having completed this..."

assumes future completion. assumes that they’ve took action.

helps in real life too, get someone to do something, use assumption that they’ll do it.

not IF you finish this, it’s WHEN.

pattern 3: ordinal language "the first thing you'll love..." "the next thing that happens..." "the third benefit you get..."

assumes continuation.

an example of all 3 being used :

“once you use 3 sprays on your neck… the first thing you’ll love is the initial spray, it’s strong, but that’s how you’ll know it lasts… but once the dry down hits… you might notice that people naturally start lingering around you.”

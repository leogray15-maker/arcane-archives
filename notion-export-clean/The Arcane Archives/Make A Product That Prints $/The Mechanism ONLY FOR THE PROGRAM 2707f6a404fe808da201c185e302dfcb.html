The Mechanism ONLY FOR THE PROGRAM.

once again, only read this if you're doing the program, you can read it if you're doing the book of secrets, but don't get confused.

Your mechanism is your unique way of getting results.

For all we're concerned, it IS your product.

The mechanism is the one thing that if you don't get right, your product will fail.

In short, what is the unique process you use to reach the clear goal of your marketing strategy?

Your mechanism is what makes people think, "This may finally solve my problem!"

Your mechanism is a system.

  • A writing system
  • A productivity system
  • A habit tracker
  • A project management dashboard
  • A lead generation system
  • A fitness program
  • A meditation schedule and guidelines
  • Almost anything related to steps, habits, or organization of a project related to what you help people do.

Systems sell.

The easiest system is the "step by step" guide.

keep this in mind...

PEOPLE LOVE STEPS AND CLARITY.

the easiest way to make your product seem 10x better is to offer steps.

and EVEN better you can tell them how the "steps" will work in the sales page, but don't reveal too much, you want to "tease" it.

How can you start testing a system after you've identified a problem?

Notes/Notion.

It makes it easy to visualize how you solve a specific problem.

FIRST, YOU IDENTIFY A PROBLEM.

Based on the problem you identified:

  • Educate yourself on how to solve it through free online resources, your prior experience, or other courses that can act as inspiration
  • Start building out step-by-step processes in Notion that make it easier on you to solve the problem
  • Test that process on yourself and refine it as roadblocks come up

Example: I was never consistent with writing newsletters and always saw them as a daunting task. From there I could:

  • Research how to write better long-form content
  • Take notes and start practicing
  • Create a Notion template that reminds me of the principles and other tips that helped me
  • Get results with my newsletter and use that as social proof for the product
  • Note the thought processes and methods I used to make my writing better inside Notion
  • Refine the template and create an organized, streamlined way of writing better newsletters in Notion
  • Research on how to not lose motivation WHILE working (that could even be a course in itself, just an idea)

The product you build becomes pretty easy after you've wrote down the steps.

What can this Notion system be used for?

What your course curriculum is built off of so they have an actionable way of practicing and implementing what you teach.

The steps, form a good "playbook" or "plug and play" mechanism, come up with a unique name to entice the lizard brain.

Again, here's what you do to start ideating your unique mechanism.

Step 1: List out steps people need to take to achieve your clear goal.

The steps that you list out do not need to be unique, they need to be effective.

You can pull inspiration for these steps from courses you’ve taken, books you’ve read, or just how you go about doing things.a

A UNIQUE name will go far, for example my "productive isolation" is practically deep work and monk mode, just rebranded.

the one and only thing I learnt from high school business was that there are no new products, just repackaged.

Step 2: Test those steps inside of Notion or just with pen and paper.

Write down the steps.

Actually DO the steps for 1-2 weeks.

Identify what works and what doesn't.

Change the steps accordingly.

Refine with time until you have a solid system.

Step 3: Give this path/process a compelling name.

Back in the day there was a program called P90X. the home workout routine that everyone raved about.

Their unique mechanism was “muscle confusion,” which by now most people know is BS. But at the time it made people think “This may FINALLY solve my problem.”

The sequences of exercises day by day were the steps… nothing special, but when you call it “muscle confusion” people freak out and pay you.

Of course, since then muscle confusion has been disproven, so don't do something false and stupid like that.

Once you have the steps mapped out, the name will come easier.

The important thing here is the steps, make them GOOD, because they sell.

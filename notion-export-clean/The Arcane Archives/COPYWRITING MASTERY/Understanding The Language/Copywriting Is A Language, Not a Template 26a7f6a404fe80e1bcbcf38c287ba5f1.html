Copywriting Is A Language, Not a Template.

this is the single most important thing you learn about copywriting.

Copywriting is a language.

copywriting is simply the “language of sales”

More specifically, it is the language of emotion.

because emotions are what make people buy things.

the most effective way to learn copywriting is the way you’d learn a new language

let’s say it’s Spanish.

For the purposes of this course, assume you absolutely cannot read or speak ANY Spanish.

it’ll make my examples a lot easier.

If you’ve never tried to learn a new language…

here’s how the education process usually goes…

The teacher will start with some basics…

Then they will add some stuff on top, while they continue to repeat and reinforce past lessons and basics over and over.

REMEMBER THIS...

It is the brute force repetition that makes the language stick.

But nobody teaches copywriting as a language to practice.

Instead they teach it like putting together a piece of furniture.

“Do this. Then do that. Fill in this template.”

We will not do that inside this course.

We will establish a few core principles, then expand on them over and over and over.

you may find parts of this course repetitive…

But That is by design.

Once the copywriting basics is DRILLED into your memory through continuous practice and repetition...

Then you really just need to consume as much of the language as possible.

Soon you’ll start to recognize patterns…

you start by reciting basic words, like thank you, or hello…

then objects, then simple sentences…

Until you are ordering your own food at a restaurant and telling jokes in “Spanish.”

This course will help you develop skills in a similar way for copywriting.

At first you will need to think about what you are doing…

But not for long.

In no time it will all be second nature.

trust me.

I was a complete retard back in the day.

if I can do it, so can you.

remember that.

WHAT IS VALUE / HOW TO MAKE VALUE

every guy online tells you to create value to make money blah blah blah, especially tate.

but have they ever actually taught you how to do it?

the formula to value is right there, however you might not be too good at formulas or maths.

so ill explain it easily.

DREAM OUTCOME

in simple terms.

every product offers a transformation.

so a weight loss course is not selling a weight loss course.

it's selling the transformation from being fat to skinny.

and as you could imagine, that is valuable.

SO THAT CARRIES A LOT OF VALUE, YOU WANT TO INCREASE THE DREAM OUTCOME.

or in other words...

the transformation you are taking your "customer" to should be good.

e.g taking them from fat to skinny, unproductive to productive, undisciplined to disciplined.

tell them how good it will feel to finally make that switch, then tell them you can do it for them.

PERCEIVED LIKELIHOOD OF ACHIEVEMENT.

a long statement, I was weirded out when I also saw it at first.

but in simple terms.

it is how likely is it that your customer will get that DREAM OUTCOME we was talking about?

if we talked about that weight loss course again, how could we increase the likelihood of achievement?

we could offer a refund guarantee.

So they think if this doesn't work, I can just get my money back.

so for them, its basically a win win scenario, its either they lose the weight, or the money comes back.

and its not hard to lose weight bro, so it basically just secures more sales because you reverse the risk.

SUMMARY FOR THE TOP 2 :

dream outcome X perceived likelihood of achievement. WE WANT TO INCREASE THIS

DREAM OUTCOME

what is the dream outcome that we are selling to our customer?

in other words, if they buy this, what is the transformation that they receive, is it from fat to skinny, from broke to rich?

make it the best possible/

PERCEIVED LIKELIHOOD OF ACHIEVEMENT.

how likely is it that our customer reaches that dream outcome?

how to make sure they reach the dream outcome?

you put a refund guarantee.

NOW THAT THEY SEE THE DREAM OUTCOME AND THAT THEY ARE GOING TO GET IT BY BUYING YOUR PRODUCT.

THEY WILL BUY, OR THEY WILL GET THEIR MONEY BACK.

but wait, there's more...

the bottom half.

EFFORT AND SACRIFICE .

WE WANT TO DECREASE THIS.

So for the weight loss program example.

we can decrease the effort and sacrifice by saying :

in your diet plan, we will still include treats and snacks. because its the calories that matter for losing weight.

they don't have to sacrifice as much as they thought, so they're more likely to buy it.

SUMMARY:

INCREASE THE DREAM OUTCOME THAT THEY GET FROM BUYING YOUR PRODUCT.

INCREASE THE PERCEIVED LIKELIHOOD OF ACHIEVEMENT BY HAVING A REFUND GUARANTEE.

DECREASE THE EFFORT AND SACRIFICE NEEDED TO REACH THE DREAM OUTCOME.

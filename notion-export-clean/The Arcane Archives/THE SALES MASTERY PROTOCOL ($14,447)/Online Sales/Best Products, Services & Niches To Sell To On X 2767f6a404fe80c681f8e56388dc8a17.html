Best Products, Services & Niches To Sell To On X.

I know many of you are beginners in the online business space, so hopefully this will help clear some things up and give you a game plan.

The Best Niches To Sell To On X

  • Personal brands in general
  • Ecommerce stores
  • Info product creators
  • Coaches / consultants
  • AUTHORS
  • Agency owners
  • Founders / executives
  • Busy 9-5ers
  • A group of people that have offers but don't understand how to get an sales / leads via X
  • The beginner market in any industry (health, wealth, relationships)
  • Service businesses (usually a one-man show that has a personal brand)
  • Big corporate brands (Wendy's, Gillette, etc)

X is a different space than Instagram or Facebook. You must understand X as a platform to see this.

There aren't pages, maps, etc that are helpful for finding local businesses. So, don't target local businesses, or any business that you would would have difficulty finding on X.

The Best Products / Services To Sell To Those Niches

Sell what is already selling. Run it through the other marketing frameworks in this channel and it will sell better. You are simply taking a piece of the pie.

Services To Sell:

In order to sell these services effectively, you need to get on the phone with somebody in your target niche and understand their client acquisition process. You are going to "patch the holes" in that process and increase their revenue via more product sales or lead generation.

That is for marketing services (email, design, lead gen, etc), not coaching / consulting that falls under the health / relationships vertical. Some coaching can go after the "increase business revenue" angle though.

  • X account management, growth, and ghostwriting
    • To any of the niches above
  • Instagram account management, growth, and content creation
    • To X creators / personal brands that struggle to be consistent with Instagram (you can take their X content and repurpose it).
  • Fitness coaching
    • For executives, founders, busy 9-5ers, or any non-business owner that can afford high ticket coaching
  • Performance coaching
    • To the same niches as fitness coaching
  • Email marketing (sequences and broadcasts)
    • To eCommerce stores, agency owners, personal brands, coaches
  • Funnel creation (for info products, coaching, or both)
    • To info product creators and coaches / consultants
    • Authors, to sell more books (and possibly consult them on creating a higher ticket offer)
  • High ticket sales (being a high ticket closer)
    • Anyone with a high-ticket offer (coaching, consulting, marketing service) or a high ticket course
    • Influencers, creators, freelancers, agency owners
  • Content multiplication / repurposing (taking tweets, videos, blogs, or whatever their main form of content is and editing it to post across more platforms)
    • Example: taking tweets and making Instagram posts
    • Example: taking YouTube videos, writing a blog post out of it, clipping the video for Instagram and TikTok, and then editing the audio for a podcast
    • Niches: Any personal brand that already has strong content on any platform

Your job is to quite literally copy somebody who has already figured it out and get good at branding / marketing yourself.

This comes heavily into play for products as well.

The Best Products To Sell

Wonder why everyone and their mom has a social media growth guide? Because they sell. Well.

So, our first product that sells well is X-related.

If you want to sell something X-related and not get memed off the face of the earth, RUN IT THROUGH THE MARKETING POSTS IN HERE.

The X Growth angle is okay - but you know what would sell more?

The X Lead Multiplier - that helps creators and coaches build out a X-specific funnel to landing more clients.

In reality it's just a funnel and understanding of how to leverage X for traffic - but it would be seen as a powerful and authoritative product that would get quality buyers into YOUR funnel.

It would also have much of the same information as a X growth guide.

Selling Your Unique System

I encourage people to create a high ticket offer first (see services to sell from above).

You can start landing clients immediately with this.

Then, once you have results, you can literally just turn your process for getting results into an info product and have all of the marketing done. You already know it will sell.

Even if the system isn't "unique" per se - that's where marketing comes in. Narrow your target audience and sell to an individual.

Products That Sell

  • Productivity systems
  • Business systems (how you get sales / clients for a specific niche above)
  • Make any process in ones life simpler (example: X content creation for personal brands - this is another angle that is better than the traditional "X growth")
  • Weight loss programs
  • Body part specific muscle building programs
  • Individual specific muscle building programs (body type)
  • Teaching a specific skill (marketing or otherwise)
  • ANYTHING - you just have to be able to market it effectively (yes I'm serious). This takes experience and practice/iteration to understand fully

Pay close attention to what others are doing, especially the accounts you want to be like.

Sign up for their newsletters, buy their products, study their funnels, and see where you would be able to help them (or steal ideas from them).

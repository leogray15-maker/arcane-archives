X POST = TIKTOK.

So... this is simple.

You just take screenshots of your x post while moving down…

You do this on mobile.

Now, just a clear thing for screenshots… there’s just one thing that I’m fussy about.

When you screenshot your x post on your phone, just make sure there is no “slider” on the right side.

It’s a minor flaw, but it does bother me.

But that’s really it.

You screenshot the X post while moving down…

But you do NOT make the screenshots too large, make sure they fit in the tiktok frame.

And also… just read the post as you screenshot, so you see that it will flow nicely when they are swiping.

And last note…

Use a sound that can make people focus while reading.

No words being spoken, no loud music, just ambience is what I prefer, but of course, test it.

Use some hashtags if you’re new, it might be needed.

If you’re under 1k followers, obviously don’t post a slide telling them to go click the link in your bio to join your email list, you can’t even put a link in bio.

But if you’re over 1k followers, then yeah, do that.

Pretty simple, just wanted to clear it up

X POST = EMAIL.

So, if you’re doing substack you just want to :

Go on your dashboard.

Click “new post” in top right.

Then click “text post”

Then you should see something like this…

Now... all you do is copy and paste your tweet into the “start writing” category.

But here… let me give you just a quick lesson on emails :

99% of people will open the email on their phone, so you need to optimise that.

You see the “title” up at the top?

That’s what they see as the title of the email.

And the FIRST line in the “start writing” category will be the line that they see below the “title”

So… here’s what you need to do.

You need to compliment the title with the first line of the writing.

Pretty extreme, but here’s what you want to go for :

  • Intrigue (make them think “what is this?”, think of the New/Only category in the copywriting course
  • WIIFM (what’s in it for me?) ALWAYS answer wiifm, no matter what.

That’s how you capture attention in the headline, which you should know from the copy structure.

Now... let’s talk about the end of the email.

We’ve sorted the headline and the capturing attention.

We’re sorted the middle of it “the main text”

Now how do you end it?

Because obviously… you don’t give them a cta join your email list if they’re already in it.

So you direct them to your skool page, where sales will be made.

Now later.. we will be creating the skool.

And we will ALSO be creating a top 1% sales page for the skool step by step. (I’ll give you a free $600 course on it)

A sales page is basically a page that sells your product to the person who clicked the link, basically it just gets them to buy through a sales process which just convinces them.

Right now, you don’t need to worry about linking your skool (because we haven’t created it yet)

But it’s for future reference.

So... here’s the summary for the emails.

  • Make the headline compliment the first line of writing
  • Link your skool at the end instead of email list (we will go over this later)

ALSO, bonus tip.

You can schedule your emails to be sent out at a certain time.

I recommend sending them out at the same time every day, I usually do 6pm UK time because that’s when everyone is home from work, etc.

But you can experiment with different times, all that matters is that you stay consistent to one time after a while of testing…

It’s kind of like a tv show as a kid… it will be on at an EXACT time and you’ll always watch it.

You’ll do the same with your emails.

Let me give you the process... The Conscious Conditioning Process...

It's 6 steps:

  1. PAUSE When a thought hits... Stop before it becomes an emotion... Use "anchors" like breathing or music to stay calm...
  1. QUESTION Ask yourself: "Why am I thinking this?" "Have I thought this before?" "Is this even real?"

Write it down... Journal it out... Get it on paper...

  1. OBJECTIFY That thought isn't YOU... It's just an object... A tool you can use or throw away...
  1. REFRAME Turn the negative into positive... Like turning guilt into gratitude... Failure into feedback... Fear into curiosity...
  1. CHARACTERIZE Ask yourself: "What would my hero do?" "How can I use this to grow?" "What action can I take?"
  1. ALIGN Remember your goals... Remember your vision... Make sure your actions match who you REALLY are...
